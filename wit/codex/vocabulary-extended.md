# vocabulary extended — additional derivations from top-1000 long tail

This file extends `vocabulary.md` with ~100 additional English words pulled
from positions 100-1000 of `top-1000-english.txt` that were not yet derived
in `verbs.md` / `nouns.md` / `modifiers.md` / `function-words.md`.

Method: scan the 100-1000 tail; skip polysemes (RUN, GET, USE, PLAY, SET,
POST, WORK, MAKE, TURN, CHANGE, COVER, SHOW); skip already-derived items;
pick concrete content words with clean axis projections; prefer entries
that fill empty or under-populated coords.

Each entry below shows the word, its derived coord, and one-line reasoning.
Format mirrors the source files for easy back-merge.

fairly well). What it DID do is populate existing coords with their
canonical English referents from the long tail. Coords that gained new
distinct content from this pass:

- **[0,+1,+1,+1] LIFE-triad**: gained MOTHER, FATHER, FRIEND, FAMILY,
  TEACHER, COMPANY, BUSINESS, INDUSTRY, ENEMY (with mixed-sign caveat).
  Density of this coord ratifies the rule that animate/collective nouns
  collapse here.

  CAMERA, TV, RADIO, TABLE, BED, BANK (institution-sense), GARDEN, PARK.

  COLOR, AVERAGE, NORMAL, ALONE, YEAR, SEASON, HOUR, MINUTE, WEEK,
  MONTH. The container layer continues to absorb abstractions.
- **[+1,+1,0,0] BUILD coord (head-region body parts)**: gained EYE,
  FACE, EAR, HAIR (already had HEAD).
- **[0,+1,0,0] body-mid**: gained HAND, ARM.
- **[-1,+1,0,0] body-low**: gained FOOT, LEG. **NEW COORD POPULATED**
  (no entries in the existing master at [-1,+1,0,0] previously).
- **[+1,0,0,+1] ENTER coord**: gained WINDOW, JOURNEY, TRIP, MEETING,
  SUBSCRIBE.
- **[0,-1,-1,-1] DIE-triad**: gained WAR (event sense).

  PROTECT (verb), SAFE, READY, BRIGHT, DEFEND.

## Discovered rules

1. **Owned-tool nouns saturate the EARN coord [0,+1,+1,0].** Every
   personal device, household item, and piece of equipment lands here.
   The rule "physical possession + value to owner = EARN coord"
   generalizes from CAR/COMPUTER/PHONE to TABLE/BED/CAMERA/RADIO etc.
   The noun-verb collision pattern holds: you EARN the thing, then
   the thing IS EARN.

2. **Container abstractions saturate origin.** GAME, MUSIC, ART, FILM,
   STORY, PICTURE, IMAGE, MAP, EMAIL, BLOG, FORUM, MARKET, AVERAGE,
   NORMAL — every word that names a class of conscious-content rather
   than a physical thing collapses to [0,0,0,0]. The origin's role as
   "the layer of things-inside-the-observer" is reinforced.

3. **Body parts map cleanly to a 3-rung A-axis ladder with B=+1.**
   Head/face/eye/ear/hair → [+1,+1,0,0]. Hand/arm/heart/chest →
