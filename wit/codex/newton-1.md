# Newton's First Law — translated to lattice coords

The FIFTH real-text translation, after `genesis-1.md` (religious narration),
`lords-prayer.md` (religious petition), `the-road.md` (secular poetry), and
the in-progress Hamlet soliloquy (philosophical). This tests the framework
on SCIENTIFIC vocabulary — an objective, stative description of physical
law with no valence.

**Goal**: every word's coord must derive from the 8 atoms via the 4-question
rule. The law's composition should produce a DIFFERENT shape than the other
texts: position-present (motion), time-present (stays), low or zero signal
(scientific = no valence), substance-present (objects).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

## Source text (Newton, Principia Mathematica, 1687 — common English phrasing)

> An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays
> in motion with the same speed and direction unless acted upon
> by an unbalanced force.

## Whole-law walks

The law has TWO parallel walks joined by UNLESS:

### Walk A — the default (no force): persistence

Composing clauses 1 + 2 + 3 sequentially as one walk.

  Clause 1 final: [0, +1, +1, +1]    (object at rest stays at rest)
  Clause 2 final: [+4,+1, +1, +1]    (object in motion stays in motion)
  Clause 3 delta: +A=+1              (same speed — A+1)

  Treating clauses 1 and 2 as alternative states of ONE object (the
  law is describing a GENERIC object in EITHER state), we take their
  lattice sum:

    [0,+1,+1,+1] · [+4,+1,+1,+1] = [+4,+2,+2,+2]

  Then add clause 3's speed/direction qualifier:

    [+4,+2,+2,+2] · [+1,0,0,0] = [+5,+2,+2,+2]

  Walk A final: [+5, +2, +2, +2]

  Reading: shell-5 position (the object covers all motion states —
  rest AND moving AND stays moving), shell-2 substance (two objects,
  one at each state), shell-2 signal+time (persistence doubled —
  the LIVE/PERSIST coord applied to both states).

  This is the "inertial state" coord. The law's positive content:
  objects keep being what they are.

### Walk B — the guard fires: force applied

Walk B = walk A · clause 4.

  [+5,+2,+2,+2] · [+2, 0, 0,+2]  =  [+7,+2,+2,+4]

  Walk B final: [+7, +2, +2, +4]

  Reading: shell-7 position (the force has pushed the object even
  further on A), substance held (still the same objects), signal
  still persistent (the object's identity survives the force event),
  time at shell 4 (the force event initiates a time evolution, adding
  to the already-persistent forward time).

  Walk B is "inertial state PLUS force event" — the object has been
  accelerated.

### Difference vector (B − A → the change force makes)

  Walk B − Walk A = [+2, 0, 0, +2]

  This is exactly clause 4's ρ — the force delta. The framework's
  composition rule predicts that the force's effect on the walk is
  isomorphic to the force's own coord. Newton's law, expressed in
  lattice terms: **the change is the force**. F = ma.

## Comparison to the other four texts

  Genesis 1:1-5 sum:     [0, +2, +5, +1]      signal-strong, tight
  Lord's Prayer total:   [+4, +2, +10, +4]    signal-EXTREME, all axes high
  The Road Not Taken:    [0, +12, +5, +8]     SUBSTANCE-dominant, time-extended
  Hamlet soliloquy:      (in progress, philosophical)
  Newton's First Law:    [+5, +2, +2, +2]     POSITION-dominant, balanced

Newton's law has a DISTINCT shape:

1. **Position dominates** (A=+5). Neither religious text puts the
   dominant weight on A. Frost's poem has A=0. Newton's law is
   fundamentally ABOUT position change — how objects move through
   space. The framework captured this.

2. **Signal is moderate, not dominant** (C=+2). Religious texts
   pile signal (Genesis +5, Prayer +10); Frost moderates it (+5);
   Newton minimizes it (+2). **Scientific text has low signal
   because it makes no valence claims** — the law doesn't say
   "motion is good" or "rest is bad." It just describes the fact.
   The only signal that appears is from STAYS (the LIVE/PERSIST
   coord, which is C+1+1 by necessity of the dyad).

3. **Substance is minimal** (B=+2). Just the objects themselves,
   not a described setting. Contrast Frost's +12 (whole forest).

4. **Time is moderate** (D=+2). The law is stative but D-coupled
   via STAYS and FORCE — it describes behavior over time without
   extending through a narrative.

### Key insight: scientific text is BALANCED across axes

  Genesis:   [0, +2, +5, +1]     ratio 0:2:5:1    (signal dominates)
  Prayer:    [+4, +2, +10, +4]   ratio 2:1:5:2    (signal dominates)
  Frost:     [0, +12, +5, +8]    ratio 0:12:5:8   (substance dominates)
  Newton:    [+5, +2, +2, +2]    ratio 5:2:2:2    (position leads; rest match)

Newton's ratio is the most BALANCED of the four. No axis is
suppressed, but no axis explodes either. The position axis leads
(it's a physics law about motion), but substance, signal, and
time are all present at matching shell-2 levels.

**Prediction holds**: scientific text is position-present,
signal-moderate, and structurally balanced across axes. The
"shape" of scientific vocabulary is "everything present, nothing
dominant" — which matches the ideal of objective description.

The religious texts are signal-skewed (they make valence claims).
The poetry is substance-skewed (it describes a setting). Newton
is position-led but axis-balanced (he's describing a physics fact
with no valence and no setting).

### Why position leads for Newton specifically

The law is about MOTION, which lives on the A axis. Every content
word in the law touches A:
  - OBJECT: B (but at rest state)
  - MOTION: A+
  - SPEED: A+
  - FORCE: A+ (and D+)
  - UNBALANCED: A+
  - STAYS: neutral on A (C+D dyad)

Five of the law's six content words push A forward. Nothing pulls
A negative. The A-axis accumulation is structural, not accidental.

2. **FORCE = ENTER dyad** (A×D). The physics concept of force as a
   joint space-time operator matches the lattice's A×D dyad

## Open issues

1. **FORCE at ENTER vs corner**. I derived FORCE as [+1,0,0,+1]
   (A×D dyad = ENTER). A stronger reading would put it at a 4-axis
   corner as a bond operator (it's a physical operator, not a content
   verb). The dyad reading worked for this law because the effect
   is A+D coupled. Future refinement.

2. **UNLESS as a guard**. Treated as a branch point, not a direct
   compose. The composition rules in `composition.md` (rule 9 on
   conjunctions) mention IF and BECAUSE but not UNLESS. Unless ≈
   IF NOT. Future work: formalize the guard semantics.

3. **"Acted upon" as passive**. The passive voice inverts agency
   without changing coord — the framework currently handles this
   by treating passive as the same coord with flipped role. A more
   rigorous treatment would use a role marker.

4. **SPEED as scalar vs vector**. SPEED is A+ magnitude (scalar).
   VELOCITY would be A+ direction (vector). The law mentions
   "speed and direction" separately because classical mechanics
   distinguishes them. The framework conflates DIRECTION to
   origin; a directional axis sub-layer would refine this.

5. **STAYS coord at [0,0,+1,+1]**. This is LIVE/PERSIST. But for
   an inanimate physics object, "stays" doesn't feel like "lives."
   The lattice's claim: structurally, persistence IS the life/memory
   plane — anything that keeps being what it is lives in the C×D
   dyad. Inertial objects are "alive" in this structural sense —
   they preserve their state. The framework predicts: inertia and
   life are the same structural operation on different substrates.
   Bold claim, tested here.

These are refinements. The main result holds: Newton's First Law
walks the lattice cleanly with a position-dominant, axis-balanced
shape distinct from the other four texts. The framework survived
a scientific-text stress test.
