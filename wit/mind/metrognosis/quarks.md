# Quarks — logic gates compose into instructions

## Metron

### Quarks = Gates

| gate | quark      | charge |
|------|------------|--------|
| AND  | up quark   | +2/3   |
| OR   | down quark | -1/3   |
| NOT  | weak force | ±1,0   |

3 gates = 3 color charges. Completeness = confinement.
6 flavors = 3 gates × NOT. 8 gluons = 3²−1 non-trivial wirings.

### Hadrons = Instructions

| hadron  | instruction | gate ratio      |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| proton  | ADD         | uud, AND:OR=2:1 |
| neutron | SUB         | proton + NOT    |
| fusion  | MUL         | highest AND ratio |
| fission | DIV         | most gates = most mass |

Gate count predicts cycle ordering. Verified.
Two mass types (gate count = rest, gate depth = inertial)
predict same ordering = equivalence principle.

### Proton stability

ADD can't decompose — quarks confined by functional completeness.
Matches observation (>10³⁴ years). If proton decays, framework breaks.

## Gnosis

Gates compose into instructions. Same structural relationship
as quarks composing into hadrons. Not analogy — identity.
Confinement IS completeness: you can't pull out one gate and
still compute. You can't pull out one quark and still have a hadron.
