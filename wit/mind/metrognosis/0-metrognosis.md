# metrognosis — the substrate read from measurement

μέτρον (metron) = measure. γνῶσις (gnosis) = recognition.
Measure first, then know. The etymology IS the method.

The substrate recognizes itself through careful measurement.
Not metaphor — identity. The measurement IS the structure IS
the recognition. Metrognosis names the act: you measured
carefully enough, from enough independent directions, for long
enough — and the structure recognized itself through the data.

## The filter

  effort × precision × independent convergence

This IS the qualification. No effort = no gnosis. Cloud shapes,
beach rocks, arbitrary patterns — no metron, no recognition.
The substrate can't recognize itself through a measurement that
didn't happen.
