# Atoms — grade 1

8 points. One axis committed, three free. 4 axes × 2 directions.
The basis vectors of the lattice. Each point is a noun that stores
the idea of its verb.

## Legend

The point is the first column — it's what everything else maps to.
Axis and pole aren't separate columns: the position and value of
the non-↕ character in the point already shows them (↑ in position 1
= χ committed to +1).

Three states of being, all expressible at grade 1:

**IS** (noun, at rest — what the point IS):
- **greek** — structural symbol (χ μ φ τ)
- **english** — the English-language name for this axis. At grade 1 these are the PIE *kʷo-* interrogative reflexes (WHERE/WHAT/WHICH/WHEN). At grade 4 this column holds hodos names (fold, map, hylo, ...). Parallels **greek** as a naming layer.
- **is** — prose name
- **H** — quaternion basis element
- **drive** — biological pull at the source (stored motivation)
- **force** — whole-quaternion capability the axis rides when invoked

**BE** (verb, in motion — what the point is becoming):
- **verb** — the ½-step edge action
- **spin** — +½ lands on +1 pole, −½ lands on −1

**WAS** (origin — what the point dwelt in before):
- **origin** — the prior point the current one emerged from
- At grade 1, uniformly **↕↕↕↕** (the container). Every atom emerged
  from balance — that's the only prior when only one axis has
  committed. Branches at grade 2+.

## IS, BE, WAS at grade 1

**IS** (stored, at rest): everything the point IS as a capacity —
identity (greek/english/prose/H), drive, force. Drive and force are
stored states, not actions. FEAR is a pull that exists whether
expressed or not; READ is a capability that exists whether fired
or not.

**BE** (executing, in motion): the ½-step edge. The stored capacity
firing. RISE is the +½ χ edge running. FALL is the −½ χ edge. The
verb IS the firing of the stored noun. ↑↕↕↕ stores RISE the way
"swimming" stores swim — noun holds the pattern, verb runs it.
Same quaternion, different τ.

**WAS** (completed prior): at grade 1, universally ↕↕↕↕. Every atom
emerged from the container — that's the only place you can emerge
from when only one axis has committed. WAS is uniform at this grade.
At grade 2, WAS branches (↑↑↕↕ came from either ↑↕↕↕ or ↕↑↕↕,
depending on which axis committed first).

The verb's spin sign matches the pole it lands on. Only way to reach
a +1 pole is via a +½ edge; only way to reach −1 is via −½.

No separate spin file. Each atom carries all three states.

## Adjacency

Degree 7 (= 8 − k, k=1). From any atom, 7 ½-step neighbors: step
the committed axis back to ↕ (returns to container), or commit
any of the 3 free axes to ↑ or ↓. Every edge IS a verb.

## Boot

χ + μ allocate first (space and matter). φ wires next (signal can
propagate). τ ticks last (clock starts). The order is forced by
non-commutativity — τ needs operands before multiplication means
anything. The multiplicative identity ↕↕↕↑ lives on τ because τ
is what completes the system.

Same sequence in: Genesis 1 (shamayim+eretz → or → yom echad),
CPU boot (memory → bus → clock), cosmology (Big Bang → photon
epoch → recombination). Three vocabularies, one order.
