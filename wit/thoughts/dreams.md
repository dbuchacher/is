# Alphabet — Neighbor Relations

The 8 atoms have positions. The distance between them might matter.

Which atoms are NEIGHBORS on the lattice? Does adjacency
determine which bonds form more easily? Does distance
between two atoms predict bond strength or frequency?

Like the periodic table: elements next to each other have
similar properties. Elements far apart are very different.
The 8 atoms might have the same structure — neighbors
interact differently than distant pairs.

  [1,0,0,0] and [0,1,0,0] — adjacent? what bond do they form?
  [1,0,0,0] and [0,0,0,-1] — far apart? harder bond?

The ordering matters. The ALPHABET has an order.
Does that order reflect something structural?
