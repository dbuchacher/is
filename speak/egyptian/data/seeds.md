# Egyptian seeds

Same discipline as PIE + Sumerian. ~25 core morphemes that ground
in deixis; everything else reduces to seeds + already-defined
non-seeds.

## 1a — Pronouns and Deictics (6)

- **wj** — I / me (1sg suffix). Points at speaker.
- **tw** — you (2sg suffix). Points at addressee.
- **sw** — he / him (3sg-m suffix). Points at third body, masculine.
- **sy** — she / her (3sg-f suffix). Points at third body, feminine.
- **pn** — this (masculine demonstrative). Points at near-masc.
- **tn** — this (feminine demonstrative). Points at near-fem.

## 1h — Being (3)

- **wn** — to be / to exist. Bare existence-verb.
- **iw** — to be / is (circumstantial). Locative being.
- **xpr** — to become / come into being / transform. The
  ↑ of existence-arising; central to creation theology (Khepri =
  becoming-god, scarab-beetle).

## 1i — Motion (2)

- **Sm** — to go / proceed. Bare motion-verb.
- **ii** — to come. The toward-speaker motion.

## 1j — Perception (3)

- **mAA** — to see / behold. Seeing-primitive.
- **sDm** — to hear / obey / understand. Hearing = obeying (as in
  Sumerian ŋeštug = hearing AND wisdom).
- **rx** — to know / recognize.

## 1k — Speech (1)

- **Dd** — to say / to speak.

## 1e — Cosmic axes / natural phenomena (4)

- **pt** — sky. The vault above. Paired with tA.
- **tA** — earth / land. The ground below. Paired with pt.
- **ra** — sun / sun-god. The bright-disc crossing pt daily. Central
  theological figure AND the basic light-source.
- **mw** — water. The liquid that flows in the Nile and falls
  from pt as rain.

## 1d — Body (4)

- **tp** — head / top / chief. Body-top and status-top (same word).
- **ib** — heart / mind / inner-seat. The thinking-organ AND the
  interior-of-body.
- **rA** — mouth / opening / utterance. Same word for body-mouth,
  opening-of-anything, AND the utterance that comes out.
- **Hr** — face / sight / in-front-of. The face-surface AND the
  direction it points AND the preposition "on/upon."

## 1m — Qualities (2)

- **aA** — great / large / big.
- **nDs** — small / humble.

## Total: 25 seeds

All grounded in deixis (pointing-at-world). Each may reference
other seeds but not non-seeds (since non-seeds don't exist yet).

## Differences from PIE and Sumerian seed sets

- **Heart/mind fused**: Egyptian `ib` is both heart-organ and mind-
  seat. Parallel to Sumerian `šà` (heart=interior=middle), extends
  the cross-linguistic pattern of thought-in-the-chest.
- **Sun-god is basic**: `ra` is both the sun-disc and the god. Like
  Sumerian `an` (sky=god), Egyptian fuses the celestial body with
  the divine-entity at the seed level.
- **Mouth fused with utterance**: `rA` is mouth AND utterance —
  the opening AND the thing that comes out of it. Parallel to
  Sumerian `ka`.
- **xpr (become)** is a seed here (not in PIE or Sumerian seeds)
  because Egyptian creation theology places it at the center: Atum
  self-becomes (xpr) from Nun. The becoming-verb is foundational.
