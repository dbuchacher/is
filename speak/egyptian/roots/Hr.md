# Hr

**seed** | category: 1d

Egyptian: **ḥr** (written with the face-glyph 𓁷)
Gloss: face / sight / upon / in-front-of
Source: foundational; also the name of the god Horus (Hr)

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the front-surface of the body — where the
eyes sit, where [mAA](mAA.md) happens, the way-faced a body
faces. But `Hr` is also "upon / on" (the preposition): the face is
the top-front of a thing, and "on top of" follows. And it is
"in-the-presence-of" (Hr-tp = before, at the head of). AND the
god Horus (the falcon-headed sky-god) is `Hr` — the face of
divinity, the watching-eye. One word for face-organ, visual-
way-faced, prepositional-upon, and divine-watcher.
