# sy

**seed** | category: 1a

Egyptian: **=s** / **sy** (3sg-f)
Gloss: she / her / it (feminine third-person)
Source: standard pronominal set

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a third body, feminine — not [wj](wj.md),
not [tw](tw.md), and in the feminine gender. Where [sw](sw.md) is
the masculine-third, `sy` is the feminine-third. Egyptian marked
gender on the third-body pronoun where many tongues later flatten
it. The feminine-third-pole.
