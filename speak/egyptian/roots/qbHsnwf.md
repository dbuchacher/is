# qbHsnwf

category: 1e

Egyptian: **qbḥ-snw=f** ("Who-refreshes-his-brothers" — Qebehsenuef)
Gloss: Qebehsenuef / fourth son of Horus / guardian of the intestines / west
Source: funerary canopic-jar texts

## Definition

The falcon-headed fourth son of [Hr](Hr.md) — guardian of the
intestines of a dead body. Stands at [imntt](imntt.md) (west) of
the four canopic jars, watched over by Serket. Name is transparent
compound: `qbH` (refresh with water) + `snw=f` (his brothers) =
"who refreshes his brothers" — the name is the ritual role. The
west-pole son, the falcon-faced, echoing his father [Hr](Hr.md)
(also falcon-headed).
