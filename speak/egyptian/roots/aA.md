# aA

**seed** | category: 1m

Egyptian: **ꜥꜣ**
Gloss: great / large / big / senior / important
Source: productive quality-word throughout Egyptian

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what takes up more space, weight, or rank
than its neighbors — a body bigger than another body, a king
greater than his subordinates, an offering larger than customary.
Like Sumerian `gal` and PIE `megh`, `aA` is the productive-
bigifier: compounds it to make pr-aA ("great house" = palace =
"Pharaoh"), nb-aA ("great lord"), etc. The big-pole of the
size axis.
