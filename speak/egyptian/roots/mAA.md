# mAA

**seed** | category: 1j

Egyptian: **mꜣꜣ** (written with sickle + eye)
Gloss: to see / to behold / to look at
Source: standard perception verb throughout Egyptian

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the eye-act — what [Hr](Hr.md) (the face)
does when it turns toward something. The seeing-primitive of
Egyptian. When a scribe writes `mAA.n=j` ("I saw"), the speaker
is naming the act of [Hr](Hr.md) facing and taking-in. Paired
with [sDm](sDm.md) (hearing): seeing and hearing are the two
perception-poles.
