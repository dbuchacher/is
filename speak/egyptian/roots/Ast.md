# Ast

category: 1e

Egyptian: **Ꜣst** (Isis — mother-goddess, throne-goddess)
Gloss: Isis / throne / the mother-goddess
Source: omnipresent in myth, funerary literature, hymns

## Definition

The great mother-goddess, sister-wife of [wsir](wsir.md), mother
of [Hr](Hr.md). Her name written with the throne-glyph 𓊨 — `Ast`
IS both the goddess and the throne, one word for the divine-
mother-body and the seat she gives her son. The king sits upon
`Ast` (the throne) AS the son of `Ast` (Isis). Mother-pole of
the Osirian family quartet (wsir father, Ast mother, stS uncle-
antagonist, nbt-Hwt sister). The 6th god of the Ennead.
