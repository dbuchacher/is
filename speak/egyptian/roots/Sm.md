# Sm

**seed** | category: 1i

Egyptian: **šm**
Gloss: to go / to proceed / to walk
Source: standard motion-verb, ubiquitous

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a body moving across [tA](tA.md) — the
feet lifting, the stride continuing. The bare going-verb of
Egyptian. Paired with [ii](ii.md) (coming): `Sm` goes away from
the speaker, `ii` comes toward. Together they cover outward-
and-return motion.
