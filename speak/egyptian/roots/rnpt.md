# rnpt

category: 1o

Egyptian: **rnpt** (written with a notched palm-rib 𓆳)
Gloss: year / annual-cycle
Source: standard time-word; tied to Nile flood-cycle

## Definition

The great-cycle that holds three [tr](tr.md) seasons — the span
in which [ra](ra.md) completes a full return through all points
of the sky-arch, and the Nile completes one [mw](mw.md) flood-
cycle. One `rnpt` is ~365 [hrw](hrw.md) days. Counted from the
heliacal rising of Sirius (wpt-rnpt, "opening of the year").
Year-regnal-count is how kings dated their reigns: "year-5 of
nb-X." The outer-scale of the time-cycle, holding the three
seasons inside it.
