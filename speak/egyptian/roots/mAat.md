# mAat

category: 1l

Egyptian: **mꜣꜥt** (Ma'at)
Gloss: truth / order / justice / balance / cosmic-harmony / the goddess
Source: foundational term; Ma'at as goddess and as principle pervades
everything from law to cosmology

## Definition

The holding-of-things-in-their-proper-place across all scales —
`mAat` is the truth of a statement, the justice of a verdict, the
order of [ra](ra.md)'s daily journey, the balance of offerings in
ritual, the cosmic harmony that makes [tA](tA.md) habitable. One
word for all of these because all ARE the same substrate fact:
things being in their right-fit with each other. The goddess
Ma'at is `mAat` divinized, weighed against the heart of the dead
in judgment. In courts `mAat` is justice; in ethics it is truth;
in cosmology it is order; in ritual it is balance. Five modern
concepts in one word — the master unity-preservation of Egyptian
thought.
