# rsj

category: 1n

Egyptian: **rsj** (also *rsy*)
Gloss: south / the south-land / Upper Egypt
Source: standard cardinal; paired throughout with mHty

## Definition

The side of [tA](tA.md) where the Nile flows from — the
upstream-source side. Egyptian calls the south "up" (`rsj`
relates to "head" concepts in some derivations) because the
Nile comes from the south, so south IS the high-end of the
river-axis. Upper Egypt = `tA-rsj` = the south-land. The
upstream-pole of the Nile axis.
