# tA

**seed** | category: 1e

Egyptian: **tꜣ** (written with the land hieroglyph 𓇾 — three grains of sand)
Gloss: earth / land / ground / country
Source: foundational; paired with pt throughout

## Definition

Grounds by pointing down and around — the ground the body stands
on, the land where crops grow, the country of Egypt as a whole.
Where [pt](pt.md) is the vault-above, `tA` is the earth-below.
Geb (the earth-god) is `tA` divinized. Also extends to "the two
lands" (tAwy) for Upper and Lower Egypt united. The down-pole of
the cosmic axis.
