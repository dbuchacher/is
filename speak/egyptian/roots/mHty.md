# mHty

category: 1n

Egyptian: **mḥtj**
Gloss: north / the north-land / Lower Egypt
Source: standard cardinal

## Definition

The side of [tA](tA.md) where the Nile flows to — the
downstream-delta side. Egyptian calls the north "down" because
the Nile goes out to sea there, so north IS the low-end of the
river-axis. Lower Egypt = `tA-mHty` = the north-land. The
downstream-pole of the Nile axis, the downstream-pole of the Nile axis
(south/upstream). The two-lands axis (`tAwy`) IS the rsj-mHty
pair united.
