# Hapy

category: 1e

Egyptian: **ḥꜥpy** (Hapy, son of Horus — distinct from the Nile-god ḥꜥpj)
Gloss: Hapy / second son of Horus / guardian of the lungs / north
Source: funerary canopic-jar texts

## Definition

The baboon-headed second son of [Hr](Hr.md) — guardian of the
lungs of a dead body. Stands at [mHty](mHty.md) (north) of the
four canopic jars, watched over by Nephthys. Paired with the
other three sons of Horus as the 4-axis guardian quartet. The
north-pole son, the baboon-faced. Distinct from the Nile-god of
the same name despite sharing consonants — Egyptian scribes
context-disambiguated by classifier.
