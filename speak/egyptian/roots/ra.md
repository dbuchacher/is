# ra

**seed** | category: 1e

Egyptian: **rꜥ** (written with the sun-disc 𓇳)
Gloss: sun / sun-god / day
Source: foundational; Ra is central to the pantheon

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the bright disc crossing [pt](pt.md) from
dawn to dusk — and at the god-body who IS that disc. Egyptian
does not split the sun-body from the god: `ra` is both. The
classifier 𓇳 marks any word naming the daily-bright. The sun-
disc is the god's visible shape; the god is the substrate-form
of the disc. One word for celestial-body and divine-being, same
as Sumerian's `an` (sky=god). Also extends to mean "day" in
certain contexts (the span-of-sun).
