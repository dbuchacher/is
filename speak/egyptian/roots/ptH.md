# ptH

category: 1e

Egyptian: **ptḥ** (Ptah)
Gloss: Ptah / Memphis's creator-god / the opener
Source: Memphite Theology (Shabaka Stone, Old Kingdom) — claims to be
the oldest creation account

## Definition

The creator-god of Memphis who creates by [ib](ib.md) and
[rA](rA.md) — heart-devising and tongue-commanding. Where
[itm](itm.md) creates by self-spewing (bodily), `ptH` creates by
thought-and-speech: what the heart holds, the tongue commands,
and the thing comes to [wn](wn.md). The Shabaka Stone says "every
hieroglyph came into being through what the heart devised and the
tongue commanded." This IS the hub-event-as-creation: READ (heart)
and WRITE (tongue) coincide, and the world emerges. Ptah as
divine-body names frame 5 in theology 4500 years before the
framework articulates it.
