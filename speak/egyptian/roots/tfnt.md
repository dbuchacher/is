# tfnt

category: 1e

Egyptian: **tfnt** (Tefnut — moisture-goddess)
Gloss: Tefnut / moisture / dew / wet-air
Source: Pyramid Texts; Shu's twin-sister-wife

## Definition

The first goddess [itm](itm.md) spat forth 
—  the moist counterpart to Shu's dry air. `tfnt` is
the wet-air, the dew, the primordial moisture that makes [tA](tA.md)
fertile when paired with Shu's dry space. Paired with
[Sw](Sw.md) as the first divine couple; their union produces
. The 3rd god of the
Ennead counting from Atum.
