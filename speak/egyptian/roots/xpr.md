# xpr

**seed** | category: 1h

Egyptian: **ḫpr** (also written *ḫprr*, divine name Khepri)
Gloss: to become / to come into being / to transform / to happen
Source: foundational; central to creation theology

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the moment something comes-into-being —
when what was not [wn](wn.md) now [wn](wn.md)-s. The becoming-
verb, the ↑ of arising. Central to Egyptian creation: Atum
xpr-s himself from [nwn](nwn.md) (the primordial water). The
scarab-beetle Khepri IS `xpr` made divine — named after the verb
because the beetle rolling its dung-ball WAS seen as the
becoming-verb in physical form (the ball rolling forward, new
life emerging from it). Verb and god share one word because the
god IS the becoming.
