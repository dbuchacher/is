# ii

**seed** | category: 1i

Egyptian: **iꜢ** / **ii** (two-reed sign)
Gloss: to come / to arrive
Source: standard verb paired with Sm throughout Egyptian

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a body moving toward the speaker — the
opposite pole of [Sm](Sm.md). When the scribe names a body
approaching, the verb is `ii`. In sacred use: `ii.n` "has come"
marks arrival into a holy place, the coming-to of a god into a
shrine, the return of a soul. The coming-pole of the motion pair.
