# tn

**seed** | category: 1a

Egyptian: **tn** (feminine demonstrative "this")
Gloss: this (fem) / this-here-fem
Source: standard demonstrative

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at something near, feminine. Feminine pair of
[pn](pn.md). When a scribe writes `sbAyt tn` ("this teaching") the
speaker is pointing at the teaching-at-hand. The proximal-
feminine pointer.
