# pr

category: 1f

Egyptian: **pr** (written with a house-plan 𓉐)
Gloss: house / estate / household / temple-complex / domain
Source: ubiquitous; base of hundreds of compounds and titles

## Definition

The walled-holding a body or a family dwells in — four walls
and a roof enclosing [tA](tA.md) underfoot. But `pr` extends from
the dwelling of one body to the estate of a noble (the whole
land, stores, and servants belonging to a master) and further to
the temple-complex of a [nTr](nTr.md) (the god's house). One word
for the small room, the great estate, and the divine-dwelling —
because all three are the-walled-holding-of-the-master. The
classifier 𓉐 marks any walled-holding-of. Base morpheme of the
great compound `pr-aA` (great-house = pharaoh).
