# imntt

category: 1n

Egyptian: **imntt**
Gloss: west / land-of-the-west / land-of-the-dead
Source: standard cardinal; also the common funerary euphemism

## Definition

The side of [tA](tA.md) where [ra](ra.md) sets — the side
where the bright disc enters [nwn](nwn.md) each evening to travel
the underworld until dawn. `imntt` is both cardinal-side and
the realm of the [mwt](mwt.md) dead: "the Westerners" are the
glorified [Ax](Ax.md)-spirits who dwell where the sun rests. One
word for geographic west AND death-realm because in Egyptian
cosmology the two ARE one thing: the sun dies into the west and
travels the underworld with the dead. Unity-preservation at
cardinal scale.
