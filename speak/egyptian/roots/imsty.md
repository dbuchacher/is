# imsty

category: 1e

Egyptian: **imsty** (Imsety)
Gloss: Imsety / first son of Horus / guardian of the liver / south
Source: funerary canopic-jar texts throughout Egyptian period

## Definition

The human-headed first son of [Hr](Hr.md) — one of four guardians
of a dead body's organs. `imsty` holds the liver and stands at
[rsj](rsj.md) (south) of the four canopic jars, watched over by
Isis. Four sons, four organs, four directions, four protecting
goddesses — a formal 4×4 grid of guarded parts. This is the
south-pole son, the human-faced of the four. Paired in the
quartet with [Hapy](Hapy.md), [dwAmwtf](dwAmwtf.md),
[qbHsnwf](qbHsnwf.md) as the four [nTr](nTr.md)-sons.
