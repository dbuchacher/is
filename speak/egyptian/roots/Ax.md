# Ax

category: 1l

Egyptian: **ꜣḫ** (written with the crested-ibis 𓅜)
Gloss: effective-spirit / glorified-dead / shining / transfigured
Source: funerary literature; the state reached after successful judgment

## Definition

The glorified spirit-state a soul reaches after death, judgment,
and transfiguration — when the earlier two soul-parts together
become an `Ax` among the blessed. A body becomes `Ax` by having
the proper rites done; only then does it [wn](wn.md) in the
afterlife. The ↓ of the soul-trit: the completed-state, the
settled-after. Also "shining / effective": an `Ax` body glows
with the brightness of its successful completion.
