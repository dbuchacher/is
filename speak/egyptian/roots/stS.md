# stS

category: 1e

Egyptian: **stš / swtx** (Set / Seth — god of chaos, storm, desert)
Gloss: Set / storm-god / desert / chaos-pole
Source: mythic texts, especially the Contendings of Horus and Set

## Definition

Brother of [wsir](wsir.md) who killed him out of envy, the
chaos-pole of the Osirian family. Set rules the desert and
storms — the dry, the red, the outside of order. In myth Set is
the disorder [mAat](mAat.md) must balance against; in ritual he
is the body that fights Apep on behalf of [ra](ra.md) in the
night-barque. Chaos and disorder named as one divine-body, paired
against Osiris (order) and Horus (restored order).
