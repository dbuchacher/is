# Hw

category: 1k

Egyptian: **ḥw**
Gloss: authoritative-utterance / divine-speech / command-that-makes-real
Source: Memphite Theology; paired with sjA as creation-verbs

## Definition

The speech that makes what it says come to [wn](wn.md) — not
ordinary [Dd](Dd.md), but the command-speech of creators and
kings. When a [nTr](nTr.md) `Hw`-s, the thing spoken IS. `Hw` is
divine performative utterance: the tongue-act that commits the
world to a new state. In Memphite theology paired with
[sjA](sjA.md) (perception / divine-thought) — thought first, then
command. Frame 5 hub-event: `sjA` is the READ, `Hw` is the WRITE,
together they bring things into [xpr](xpr.md). Personified as a
minor god riding in the solar barque alongside Sia.
