# hrw

category: 1o

Egyptian: **hrw** (written with the sun-determinative 𓇳)
Gloss: day / daytime / the bright span
Source: standard time-word throughout Egyptian

## Definition

The span when [ra](ra.md) is above [tA](tA.md) — from dawn at
[iAbtt](iAbtt.md) to sunset at [imntt](imntt.md). The bright-
span of the day-night pair. Paired with [grH](grH.md) (night) as
the two halves of the sun-cycle. One `hrw` is also one
count-unit of time: the span of one full ra-journey across
[pt](pt.md). Dies each evening at [imntt](imntt.md) and is reborn
each morning at [iAbtt](iAbtt.md) — the cycle-pole of the time-
axis.
