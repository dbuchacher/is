# mnw

category: 1e

Egyptian: **mnw** (Min)
Gloss: Min / the fertility-god / lord-of-harvest / potent-one
Source: very ancient; Predynastic cult at Coptos and Akhmim

## Definition

The fertility-[nTr](nTr.md) with raised arm and upright phallus —
Min is the visible male-potency made divine-body, the god of
crops bursting from [tA](tA.md), of herds increasing, of the
harvest-[tr](tr.md) when grain fills the granaries. One of the
oldest [nTr](nTr.md) in the Egyptian pantheon; his cult precedes
the unification. Paired with [xnmw](xnmw.md) as the two body-
making gods: Khnum shapes the body, Min plants the seed. The
potency-pole at the scale of [nTr](nTr.md). White-bull [kA](kA.md)
was his sacred herd-body.
