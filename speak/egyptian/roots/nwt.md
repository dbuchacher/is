# nwt

category: 1e

Egyptian: **nwt** (Nut — sky-goddess)
Gloss: Nut / sky / the heavens-goddess
Source: Pyramid Texts; wife-sister of Geb

## Definition

The sky-goddess, [pt](pt.md) divinized and personified — her
arched body is the sky-vault over the earth-god. Where `pt` is
the bare sky-substance, `nwt` is the goddess whose body IS that
sky. She swallows [ra](ra.md) each evening and births him each
morning — the daily solar-cycle is Nut's own birth-cycle
repeated. Mother of the four Osirian siblings
([wsir](wsir.md), Isis, Set, Nephthys). The sky-as-god pole of
the vertical axis.
