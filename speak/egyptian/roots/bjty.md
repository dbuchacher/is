# bjty

category: 1g

Egyptian: **bjtj** (written with the bee 𓆤)
Gloss: king of Lower Egypt / bee-king / king-of-the-north
Source: royal titulary; paired with nsw

## Definition

The king of [mHty](mHty.md) (Lower Egypt, the north-land) —
named by the bee, the heraldic-creature of the delta-land. The
bee IS the north-land's heraldic-sign; the king of that land is
named by it. `bjty` pairs with the sedge-king of the south in
the formula "nsw-bjty" = king of the two lands united. Bee AND
king-of-north as one word — the same unity-preservation as the
sedge-king shows with its plant: each half-land's king is the-
heraldic-creature-of-the-land made royal-body.
