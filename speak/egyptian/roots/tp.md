# tp

**seed** | category: 1d

Egyptian: **tp**
Gloss: head / top / chief / first / upon
Source: one of the most polysemous Egyptian morphemes

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the top of the body — the round part
above the shoulders where [Hr](Hr.md) (face), [rA](rA.md) (mouth),
and the eyes all sit. But `tp` is also "top of anything" — the
top of a mountain, the top of a list, the first-of-a-series. And
it is "chief" (the tp-of-the-staff, the leading one). And it is
the preposition "upon" (on top of). One word for head-of-body,
top-of-thing, first-in-order, chief-of-group, on-top-of. Same
five-way unity Sumerian showed with `saŋ`.
