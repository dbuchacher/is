# nbtHwt

category: 1e

Egyptian: **nbt-ḥwt** (Nephthys — "Lady of the House")
Gloss: Nephthys / the mourner-goddess / sister of Isis
Source: funerary literature; the mourner-pair with Isis

## Definition

Sister of [Ast](Ast.md), wife of [stS](stS.md), one of the four
Osirian siblings. Her name is transparent compound: `nbt` (lady,
feminine of [nb](nb.md)) + `Hwt` (house, enclosure) = "Lady of
the House." Paired with Isis as the two mourner-goddesses who
weep over [wsir](wsir.md)'s body and perform funerary rites.
Guardian of the dead alongside Isis. The 8th god of the Ennead.
