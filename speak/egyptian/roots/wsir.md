# wsir

category: 1e

Egyptian: **wsir** (Osiris)
Gloss: Osiris / lord of the dead / the resurrected one
Source: central in funerary and myth texts

## Definition

The [nTr](nTr.md) who was killed, dismembered, reassembled, and
[xpr](xpr.md)-ed into the lord of the [mwt](mwt.md) realm. After
a body `mwt`-s, it becomes "Osiris-X" (e.g., "Osiris-Unas" in
the Pyramid Texts) — every dead king is identified with `wsir`.
Paired with his sister-wife Isis (Ast) and son Horus ([Hr](Hr.md))
as the core family-triad of Egyptian theology. Osiris rules the
underworld where souls are judged by [mAat](mAat.md); the
successful dead become [Ax](Ax.md) and join him.
