# iAbtt

category: 1n

Egyptian: **iꜣbtt**
Gloss: east / the east-land / where [ra](ra.md) rises
Source: standard cardinal; written with the east-determinative

## Definition

The side of [tA](tA.md) where [ra](ra.md) rises at dawn — the
first-bright side of the day-cycle. When a body stands
on the banks of the Nile and faces south (the way the water
comes from), `iAbtt` is on the left. Egyptian mapped cardinal
directions by body-orientation to the river: south=upstream =
"up," north=downstream = "down," with east and west fixed by
[ra](ra.md)'s daily path. `iAbtt` is the rising-side pole of the
sun-axis.
