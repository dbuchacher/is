# pt

**seed** | category: 1e

Egyptian: **pt** (written with the sky hieroglyph 𓇯 — a horizontal line held up)
Gloss: sky / heaven
Source: foundational; the classifier 𓇯 marks sky-related terms

## Definition

Grounds by pointing up — what is above the body of the speaker,
what [Hr](Hr.md) turns toward when lifted, what holds [ra](ra.md)
during the day and the stars at night. The overhead-vault of
Egyptian cosmology. Paired against [tA](tA.md) (earth): `pt` and
`tA` are the two cosmic poles, sky-above and earth-below. Nut
(the sky-goddess) is `pt` divinized, her body arched over `tA`.
