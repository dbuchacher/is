# mw

**seed** | category: 1e

Egyptian: **mw** (written with water-ripple sign 𓈗)
Gloss: water
Source: foundational; classifier for liquid-related words

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the liquid — the Nile flowing past [tA](tA.md),
rain falling from [pt](pt.md), the [ra](ra.md)-god's path through
the waters of [nwn](nwn.md) (primeval flood) each night. The
water-primitive. Central to Egyptian civilization because the
Nile's annual flood IS the life-bringing `mw`. Also used for
semen, saliva, and any liquid bodily substance — the liquid
category is one word.
