# pn

**seed** | category: 1a

Egyptian: **pn** (masculine demonstrative "this")
Gloss: this (masc) / this-here-masc
Source: standard demonstrative, attested throughout

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at something near, masculine. The proximal-
masc demonstrative. When a scribe writes `hrw pn` ("this day")
the speaker is pointing at the day-at-hand, today. Paired with
[tn](tn.md) (feminine proximal) by the gender split.
