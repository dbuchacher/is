# nDs

**seed** | category: 1m

Egyptian: **nḏs**
Gloss: small / little / humble / commoner
Source: standard quality-word

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what takes up less space or rank than its
neighbors — the small-body compared to the [aA](aA.md)-body, the
commoner compared to the king, the humble compared to the great.
The small-pole of the size axis. In social use: `nDs` is the
small-man, the commoner — which parallels Sumerian using `tur`
with kin-and-status senses. Size-word fuses with rank-word.
