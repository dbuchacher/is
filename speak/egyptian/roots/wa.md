# wa

category: 1b

Egyptian: **wꜥ** / **wꜥ.w**
Gloss: one / single / unique / alone
Source: standard numeral; also means "the only one" in royal titles

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at one thing by itself — a single body, a
single offering, a single [nTr](nTr.md) standing alone. The first
count. But `wa` carries a further weight: "unique, alone" — when a
scribe calls Amun `wa.w` ("the unique one"), the same word that
counts one body claims that [nTr](nTr.md) as sole-of-his-kind.
One-count AND uniqueness as one morpheme. The count-pole at ↑:
the single, the separated-out, the not-yet-joined.
