# rx

**seed** | category: 1j

Egyptian: **rḫ**
Gloss: to know / to be aware of / to recognize
Source: fundamental cognition verb

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the holding-of-truth in the body — what
[ib](ib.md) (heart / mind) does when it has taken in [mAA](mAA.md)
and [sDm](sDm.md) and keeps the shape of the world there. The
knowing-primitive. Paired with [sDm](sDm.md) and [mAA](mAA.md) as
the three perception-cognition verbs, `rx` is the settled state
that follows the active taking-in: first see, hear; then know.
