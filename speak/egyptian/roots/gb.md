# gb

category: 1e

Egyptian: **gb** (Geb — earth-god)
Gloss: Geb / earth / the ground-god
Source: Pyramid Texts; central to Ennead

## Definition

The earth-god, [tA](tA.md) divinized and personified — son of
the dry-air-god and his moisture-twin. Where `tA` is the bare
ground-substance, `gb` is the god whose body IS that ground. Geb
lies beneath the sky-goddess, and their union was the second
cosmic separation: the air-god holds the sky up away from Geb's
body to make the world. Father of the four Osirian siblings
([wsir](wsir.md), Isis, Set, Nephthys). The earth-as-god pole of
the ground-sky axis.
