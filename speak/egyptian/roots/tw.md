# tw

**seed** | category: 1a

Egyptian: **=k** (2sg-m) / **=T** (2sg-f) / **tw** (older form)
Gloss: you (second-person singular)
Source: standard pronominal set

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the addressee — the body receiving the
utterance through [sDm](sDm.md). The hearer-pole. When the scribe
addresses a named reader or the deceased in funerary texts, the
morpheme is `=k` / `=T`. Paired against [wj](wj.md) as the two
poles of every utterance.
