# mwt

category: 1h

Egyptian: **mwt**
Gloss: to die / death
Source: standard verb throughout Egyptian

## Definition

The end of [wn](wn.md) for a body — when breath stops, the heart
stops beating, the body stops [Sm](Sm.md)-ing. Paired against
[xpr](xpr.md) (becoming) as the two ends of the life-walk:
`xpr` opens, `mwt` closes. After `mwt`, the soul-parts separate
— the life-force lives on through offerings, the individual-soul
travels, and the body is prepared for the glorified state if
rites succeed. The ↓ of the existence-trit at body-scale.
