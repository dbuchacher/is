# Dd

**seed** | category: 1k

Egyptian: **ḏd**
Gloss: to say / to speak / to utter
Source: universal speech-verb; Dd-djed-pillar (stability) is separate root

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what comes out of [rA](rA.md) — the
breath shaped into words moving from speaker to addressee. The
speech-primitive of Egyptian. In Memphite theology, the
creator-god Ptah creates by `Dd`-ing: the tongue commands what
[ib](ib.md) has devised. Speech as the write-side of the
creation-event, paired with [ib](ib.md) (heart/thought) as the
READ-side.
