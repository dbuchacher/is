# grH

category: 1o

Egyptian: **grḥ** (written with the night-sky determinative)
Gloss: night / nighttime / the dark span
Source: standard time-word paired with hrw

## Definition

The span when [ra](ra.md) is below [tA](tA.md) — from sunset at
[imntt](imntt.md) to dawn at [iAbtt](iAbtt.md). The dark-span
when the sun-disc travels the underworld and the stars are in
[pt](pt.md). Paired with the day-span as the two halves of the
full sun-cycle. During `grH`, [ra](ra.md) rides the
night-barque through the body of Nut; the Westerners
([Ax](Ax.md) glorified-dead) are visible as the stars.
