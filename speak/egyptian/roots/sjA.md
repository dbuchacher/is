# sjA

category: 1j

Egyptian: **sjꜣ**
Gloss: perception / understanding / divine-thought
Source: Memphite Theology; paired with Hw as the creation-dyad

## Definition

The inner-seeing that knows the world before it is spoken — what
[ib](ib.md) does when it devises, before [rA](rA.md) commits
what-was-thought into uttered command. `sjA` is not ordinary [mAA](mAA.md) or
[rx](rx.md); it is divine-perception, the creative-seeing that
holds a thing in mind before it [wn](wn.md)-s. Paired with
authoritative-utterance at the WRITE-pole of creation; together
they let [ptH](ptH.md) create. Personified as a minor god riding
in the solar barque. Frame 5 hub: `sjA` is the READ-side of the
divine creation-event.
