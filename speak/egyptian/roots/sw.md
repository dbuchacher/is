# sw

**seed** | category: 1a

Egyptian: **=f** / **sw** (3sg-m)
Gloss: he / him / it (masculine third-person)
Source: standard pronominal set throughout Egyptian

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a third body, masculine — not [wj](wj.md)
(speaker), not [tw](tw.md) (addressee), but the third that both
turn and name. When a scribe tells a story and points at a
not-present body, the morpheme is `=f` / `sw`. Paired with
[sy](sy.md) (feminine third-person) as the two gender-poles of
the third body.
