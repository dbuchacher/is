# rA

**seed** | category: 1d

Egyptian: **rꜣ** (written with the mouth-shape 𓂋)
Gloss: mouth / opening / utterance / formula
Source: foundational body-part AND classifier for speech-acts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the opening of the body through which
[Dd](Dd.md) comes out and food enters. `rA` is mouth-and-opening,
but extends further in Egyptian to
mean "utterance / formula / spell" — the named-thing that comes
OUT of the mouth. A ritual utterance is `rA` itself; Chapter
headings in the Book of the Dead are marked `rA` ("spell / chapter").
Mouth AND what-comes-out-of-mouth as one word. In Memphite
theology, the tongue (part of `rA`) is where Ptah's commands are
spoken — the WRITE-side of creation paired with [ib](ib.md).
