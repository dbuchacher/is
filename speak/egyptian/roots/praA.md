# praA

category: 1g

Egyptian: **pr-ꜥꜣ** (literally "great house")
Gloss: Pharaoh / the king / the great-house
Source: royal title from New Kingdom onward; source of the word "Pharaoh"

## Definition

Transparent compound: [pr](pr.md) (house) + [aA](aA.md) (great)
= "the great house." First the royal-palace itself, then by
slide-of-naming the body who dwells in it. By New Kingdom, `pr-
aA` is the king himself — a [nTr](nTr.md)-on-earth, the living
[Hr](Hr.md), the [nb](nb.md) of the two lands. The English word
"Pharaoh" is `pr-aA` borrowed through Hebrew and Greek. Naming-
the-king-by-his-dwelling: the great-house IS the king, because
the king IS the royal-body that inhabits it. Unity-preservation
of dwelling and dweller, held in one transparent compound.
