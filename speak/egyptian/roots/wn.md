# wn

**seed** | category: 1h

Egyptian: **wn**
Gloss: to be / to exist / there is
Source: fundamental verb in narrative and existential statements

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what IS. When speaker names a thing that
stands in the world and needs no further claim, the verb is `wn`.
The bare existence-verb. Used both of past-existence ("there was")
and of general presence. Paired at the state-middle by
[iw](iw.md) (circumstantial being) and at the commit-pole by
[xpr](xpr.md) (becoming).
