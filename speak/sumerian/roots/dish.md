# dish

**seed** | category: 1b

Sumerian: **diš** (also *aš*)
Gloss: 1 / one
Source: first Sumerian numeral; written as a single wedge

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at one thing — one body, one [a](a.md)-cup,
one stone. The bare count of one. Written as a single vertical
wedge in cuneiform: the sign IS the pointing-at-one-thing made
visible. The first count, from which all higher counts are
built by repetition in Sumerian's sexagesimal system.
