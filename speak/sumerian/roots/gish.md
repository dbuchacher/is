# gish

category: 1g

Sumerian: **ŋiš / giš**
Gloss: tree / wood / wooden-thing
Source: foundational; also the determinative prefixed to wooden things

## Definition

The standing-tall plant on [ki](ki.md) with roots below and
branches above, AND the hard substance cut from it, AND any tool
or tool made of that substance. One word for the living plant
and the dead material it becomes — Sumerian did not split live-
wood from cut-wood. Every wooden thing in cuneiform is prefixed
with the sign for `ŋiš`: bed, plow, boat, chariot, door. The
wood-primitive.
