# aka

category: 1h

Sumerian: **aka** (also *ak*, *aka-de₃*)
Gloss: to do / to make / to perform
Source: universal verb; attaches to many nouns to verb-ify them

## Definition

The bare doing-verb — what a body does when it makes something
happen. Sumerian's productive verb-maker: attach `aka` to a noun
and the compound means "do the thing named by the noun."
`di-aka` = make-a-judgment. `u-aka` = do-the-ten-count. The verb
that turns any noun into its performance. The write-side
primitive at body scale: where [me](me.md) is bare being, `aka`
is bare doing — the commit-an-action counterpart to the is-stable
pole.
