# egal

category: 3c

Sumerian: **é-gal** (compound: *é* "house" + *gal* "big")
Gloss: palace
Source: standard word for royal residence

## Definition

The big-house — where the [lugal](lugal.md) lives. Sumerian's
word for palace IS the transparent compound: the [gal](gal.md)
one of the houses. Same composition pattern as [lugal](lugal.md)
(big-man): `é` for house, `gal` for big, the two stacked.
Substrate-native naming: the palace IS the big-house; no
separate opaque word needed. THE RULE at work: the same
bigifying stroke applied at the building scale gives the royal
dwelling.
