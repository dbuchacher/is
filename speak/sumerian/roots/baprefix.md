# baprefix

category: 4c

Sumerian: **ba-** (verbal prefix)
Gloss: the middle-voice prefix / passive-reflexive marker
Source: standard finite-prefix; marks the undergoer-oriented verb-stem

## Definition

The stroke a speaker puts on a verb when the action is not driven
from the speaker's side nor from the other's side but happens
to-or-upon a body. Where [muprefix](muprefix.md) pulls the verb
toward [ge26](ge26.md), `ba-` turns the verb onto the body that
receives the action: "it was divided," "it was given," "it became
so." The middle-pole of the three verb-prefixes, the one that
holds the action as undergone rather than as done-outward. Not a root that
stands alone — only a morpheme fit on the front of other verbs.
