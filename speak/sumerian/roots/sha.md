# sha

**seed** | category: 1d

Sumerian: **šà**
Gloss: heart / interior / middle / inside
Source: common body-part and spatial preposition "inside"

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the inside of the body — where the beating
organ sits, where breath feels held, where hunger is felt. One
word for the organ, the inside-of-anything, and the middle-of-
anything. The heart IS the interior IS the middle. When a
speaker says "in the heart of the city" Sumerian says `šà uru` —
same word for organ and for interior-of. The hub-at-body-
scale: everything closes toward `šà`.
