# gi6

category: 1e

Sumerian: **ŋi₆** (also *gi₆*, *mi*)
Gloss: night / dark / black
Source: standard opposite of [ud](ud.md)

## Definition

The dark span of no-[ud](ud.md) — when the sun has gone below
and the sky holds only [iti](iti.md) and the far lights. Where
[ud](ud.md) is the bright-pole (sun, day, light, time-of-light),
`ŋi₆` is the dark-pole: the night, the black, the span where
bodies rest and [ush](ush.md) comes easier. Sumerian names night
and blackness with one word because the dark IS the night —
same substrate fact at two angles. Paired with [ud](ud.md) as
the other half of the day-night cycle.
