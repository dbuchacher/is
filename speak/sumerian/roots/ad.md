# ad

category: 1c

Sumerian: **ad**
Gloss: father
Source: standard kinship term

## Definition

The male body held as source of the child — the one who did not
bear the child but is named alongside the bearing body in the
family tree. The father-primitive. Sumerian's word is one syllable,
matching the shape father-words take across unrelated languages.
