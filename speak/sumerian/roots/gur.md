# gur

category: 1i

Sumerian: **gur**
Gloss: to turn / to return / to reverse / to change
Source: common verb

## Definition

To turn the body or face — what a walking body does when it
changes which way it is going. When [ene](ene.md) has been going
toward [kur](kur.md) and stops and turns around, the verb is
`gur`. When water in an irrigation channel is redirected, `gur`.
When a speaker changes speech mid-sentence, `gur`. The turning-
stroke: not the full return of [gi4](gi4.md), but the moment of
reversing-the-way-it-goes itself. Kin to [gi4](gi4.md) — together they
name the full cycle (gur starts the turn, gi4 completes the return).
