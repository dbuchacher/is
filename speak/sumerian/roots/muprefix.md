# muprefix

category: 4c

Sumerian: **mu-** (verbal prefix)
Gloss: the finite prefix marking present-focus / speaker-adjacent action
Source: standard finite-prefix on Sumerian verbs; attached in front of the root

## Definition

The stroke a speaker puts on a verb to bring the action close to
[ge26](ge26.md) — the here-now marker at the verb-layer. When a
body [du](du.md)-s and the speaker wants to say "I go, right here,
right now," the verb-chain carries `mu-` in front of
[du](du.md). The proximal-pole of the three verb-prefixes: the
one that pulls the action toward the speaker's standing [ki](ki.md).
Not a root that stands alone — only a morpheme that fits on the
front of other verbs. Held as the near-pole of a three-prefix
stack that also marks middle-voice and distal actions.
