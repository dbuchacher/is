# dug4

**seed** | category: 1k

Sumerian: **dug₄** (also *du₁₁*)
Gloss: to speak / to say / to utter
Source: universal speech verb in Sumerian

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the act coming out of a [ka](ka.md): the
breath of the speaker shaped into words, moving from speaker
([ge26](ge26.md)) to addressee ([zae](zae.md)). `dug₄` is the bare
speech-verb — not commanding, not naming, not swearing, just the
general act of words leaving a mouth. The voiced counterpart to
[ŋeštug](geshtug.md)'s silent receiving: one body speaks, another
body hears. The utterance-primitive.
