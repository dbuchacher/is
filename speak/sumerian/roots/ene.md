# ene

**seed** | category: 1a

Sumerian: **e-ne**
Gloss: he / she / it (3rd person singular pronoun)
Source: attested throughout Sumerian literature

## Definition

Grounds by pointing away from speaker ([ge26](ge26.md)) and
away from addressee ([zae](zae.md)) — the third body, the
not-here-in-the-exchange body. When speaker and addressee both
turn and point at a body neither of them is, that body is `e-ne`.
The third pole of the person triad: speaker / addressee /
other.
