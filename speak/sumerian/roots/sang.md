# sang

**seed** | category: 1d

Sumerian: **saŋ**
Gloss: head / top / first / chief
Source: common body-part; also used as "first, chief" in compounds

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the top of the body — the round part
above the shoulders, the part where [igi](igi.md) and [ka](ka.md)
and [geshtug](geshtug.md) all sit. The head is the top of the
body, and Sumerian uses one word for top-of-anything: the top of
a list, the first of a series, the chief of a group. The head
IS the top IS the first. Vertical-axis-up at body-scale, paired
against [ki](ki.md) at cosmic scale.
