# edubba

category: 3c

Sumerian: **é-dub-ba** (compound: *é* "house" + *dub* "tablet" + *-ba* locative)
Gloss: tablet-house / scribal school
Source: the word for the Sumerian school where [dubsar](dubsar.md) were trained

## Definition

The house-of-tablets — where young bodies are taught to
[sar](sar.md) [dub](dub.md). Transparent compound:
[e](e.md) + [dub](dub.md) + suffix = the house-at-the-
tablets. The school IS the tablet-house; no separate opaque
word. Three-layer transparent composition: [e](e.md) for
the bounded-dwelling, [dub](dub.md) for the work done
inside, and the locative ending to hold them together.
Produces [dubsar](dubsar.md) — the trained tablet-writer who
leaves the `é-dub-ba` and serves the [lugal](lugal.md).
