# bi

**seed** | category: 1a

Sumerian: **-bi** (suffix) / **bi** (demonstrative)
Gloss: that-of-his / that / 3rd person possessive and demonstrative
Source: ubiquitous suffix in administrative and literary texts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing-back — the referent previously named in the
same utterance is re-pointed-at with `bi`. When a body, a thing, a
place has been named, then `bi` hangs on a later word and says
"that one again, the one we just spoke of." It is pointing-by-
recall rather than pointing-by-finger. The anaphoric pole of the
deictic set.
