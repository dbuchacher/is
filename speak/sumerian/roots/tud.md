# tud

category: 1h

Sumerian: **tud** (also *tu*)
Gloss: to give birth / to be born / to beget
Source: kinship and origin-texts

## Definition

The opening of [til](til.md) — the moment a new body leaves the
bearing body and becomes its own breathing body in the world. The
birth-primitive. Also used of divine origination: when the gods
`tud` mortals, they bring them into being from bearing-sources.
The ↑ of the being-verb trit at body-scale; paired at the other
pole by the ending of the life-walk.
