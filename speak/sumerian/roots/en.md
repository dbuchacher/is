# en

category: 1c

Sumerian: **en**
Gloss: lord / high priest / master
Source: prefix in many god-names (En-lil, En-ki); standard word for ruler-of-rank below [lugal](lugal.md)

## Definition

A body of high standing — the one whose word [dug4](dug4.md)-s and
is heard as command by those beneath him. Where [lugal](lugal.md)
is the big-man at the top of the rank-stack, `en` is the high
one under or beside him — the temple-head, the city-head, the
one who holds the [nin](nin.md)-complement at the male pole. In
divine names `en` marks the lord-of-a-domain: the one who rules
a given [kur](kur.md) or [a](a.md) or [lil](lil.md). The master
primitive.
