# abzu

category: 1e

Sumerian: **abzu** (also written *ab-zu*)
Gloss: primordial freshwater / the underground ocean / source-of-wisdom
Source: creation myths; the domain of Enki; the water-body under [ki](ki.md)

## Definition

The [a](a.md) that sits under [ki](ki.md) — the freshwater ocean
beneath the ground from which rivers rise and wells draw. Where
rain-water falls as [im](im.md) from above, `abzu` is the water
that was always below: the primordial-pool before [an](an.md)
and [ki](ki.md) were separated. The Sumerian speaker names it
with [a](a.md) + [zu](zu.md): water-that-knows, water-as-the-
source-of-grasping. Wisdom rises from `abzu` because the knowing
primitive and the deep-water primitive share one name. The
first-water and source-pole of the creation picture.
