# nin

category: 1c

Sumerian: **nin**
Gloss: sister / lady / queen
Source: attested throughout texts; many goddess names bear it (Nin-ḫursaŋ, Nin-lil)

## Definition

A female body of a certain standing — sister when pointed at a
male speaker's female sibling, lady when pointed at a woman of
rank, queen when pointed at the ruler's wife or a goddess. One
word for all three because all three are the same substrate
feature: a female body positioned above or beside in the family
or political tree. Where [gal](gal.md) makes things big, `nin`
names the female-one whose standing is already high. The status-
female primitive.
