# ama

category: 1c

Sumerian: **ama**
Gloss: mother
Source: universal kinship term; cognate with cross-language *mama*

## Definition

The female body that bore and feeds the child. When a child
points at the woman who made it and nursed it, the word is
`ama`. The mother-primitive. Sumerian's word is simple, two
syllables, the same shape mother-words take across unrelated
languages: the body-sound a child makes reaching for milk.
