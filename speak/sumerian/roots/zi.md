# zi

category: 1d

Sumerian: **zi**
Gloss: life-breath / soul / throat / spirit
Source: common in prayers and curses; "by the life of the god" formula

## Definition

The breath that keeps a body [til](til.md)-ing — the in-and-out
of [lil](lil.md) at [ka](ka.md), the pulse in the throat, what
leaves the body when [ush](ush.md) happens. Where [lil](lil.md)
is the moving-air of the world, `zi` is that same air
held-inside-a-body as its life. Sumerian names breath-and-soul
with one word because the soul IS the breath-the-body-holds — no
separate stuff. When [ush](ush.md) comes, the `zi` leaves. When
[tud](tud.md) opens, the `zi` enters. The life-breath primitive
at body-scale.
