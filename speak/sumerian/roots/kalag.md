# kalag

category: 1m

Sumerian: **kalag** (also *kal*)
Gloss: strong / mighty / precious
Source: literary and royal vocabulary; frequent epithet of kings and heroes

## Definition

The quality of a body that does not break when pushed, a
[gish](gish.md)-beam that does not bend, a stone that does not
crack. Sumerian uses `kalag` for both physical strength and for
what-is-held-as-precious — because what is strong is also what
endures, and what endures is what is kept. Where [gal](gal.md) is
big and [mah](mah.md) is surpassing, `kalag` is the inner
strength-pole: not bigger, but unbreakable. Paired against the
weak and the brittle. The strong-pole of the quality-set.
