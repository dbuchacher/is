# ush

category: 1h

Sumerian: **úš** (also *ug₇*)
Gloss: to die
Source: standard verb

## Definition

The end of [til](til.md) — what happens when a body stops
breathing, stops moving, stops [me](me.md)-ing. The body that
`úš`-es goes to [kur](kur.md) (the netherworld). The ending-of-
life primitive, the ↓ of the being-verb trit at body-scale;
paired at the other pole by the opening of the life-walk.
