# mu

category: 1e

Sumerian: **mu**
Gloss: year / name / cry
Source: ubiquitous in year-formulae ("year when X happened") and in name-giving

## Definition

The count of one full cycle of [iti](iti.md)-s — when the moon
has closed its loop twelve times, one `mu` has passed. But `mu`
is also the name a body is called by — because Sumerian
year-names are literally "the `mu` when [lugal](lugal.md) built
the [edingir](edingir.md)," so the year IS its name IS the event
it is known-by. And `mu` is also the cry-out, the voice that
calls a body by its name. One word for the cycle-of-time, the
name-of-a-body, and the cry-that-names — because naming IS
calling out across time, and year-naming IS calling the year by
what happened in it. The loop-closure of [iti](iti.md) at the
larger cycle.
