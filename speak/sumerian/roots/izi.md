# izi

category: 1e

Sumerian: **izi** (also *bar₇*)
Gloss: fire / flame
Source: common natural-phenomenon morpheme

## Definition

The hot bright that a body lights and tends — the cooking-flame
under a pot of [a](a.md), the torch in the dark, the burning
reeds. Paired against [a](a.md) (cold-wet) as the hot-dry
counterpart. Comes down from [an](an.md) in lightning and up
from dry [gish](gish.md) when rubbed. The heat-and-light
primitive.
