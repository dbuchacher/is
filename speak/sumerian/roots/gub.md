# gub

**seed** | category: 1i

Sumerian: **gub**
Gloss: to stand / to be stationary / to be set up
Source: common verb in texts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a body that is upright and not moving —
feet planted on [ki](ki.md), vertical, stable. Where [du](du.md)
is the walking body, `gub` is the body that has stopped walking
and stands. Also used for things set-up-and-standing: a stele, a
statue, a king taking his place. The stillness-verb of the body.
