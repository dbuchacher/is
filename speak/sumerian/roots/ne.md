# ne

**seed** | category: 1a

Sumerian: **ne** / **ne-e**
Gloss: this / this-here (proximal demonstrative)
Source: literary and administrative texts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what is close to the speaker —
near-at-hand, within reach, touchable. Where [ur5](ur5.md) is
the general demonstrative, `ne` is the proximal one: the body
or thing the speaker can put a hand on. The here-pole of the
here-there axis.
