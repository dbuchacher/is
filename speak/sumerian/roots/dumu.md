# dumu

category: 1c

Sumerian: **dumu**
Gloss: child / son / young-one
Source: standard kinship; also used for "junior member of X"

## Definition

The body that came from [ama](ama.md) and [ad](ad.md) — the
young-one in the family tree, the one still [tur](tur.md) relative
to its parents. Both son and daughter can be `dumu`; when gender
needs to be marked, Sumerian compounds: `dumu-nita` (son) and
`dumu-munus` (daughter). The child-primitive, paired against
parents in the generation stack.
