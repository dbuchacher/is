# tur

**seed** | category: 1m

Sumerian: **tur**
Gloss: small / little
Source: standard quality-word

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what takes up less [ki](ki.md) than its
neighbors — a body shorter than another body, a house smaller
than another house. `tur` is the bare lesser-size-than-baseline
quality, the opposite pole to [gal](gal.md). Where the big-one
becomes the chief-one in Sumerian compounds, the `tur`-one is
the lesser, the junior, the minor. The small-pole of the size
axis.
