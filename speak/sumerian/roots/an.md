# an

**seed** | category: 1e

Sumerian: **an** (written 𒀭)
Gloss: sky / heaven / god / divine / up
Source: foundational morpheme; also the divine determinative prefixed to every god's name

## Definition

Grounds by pointing up — what is above the speaker's head, what
the body cannot touch, what the [igi](igi.md) looks toward when
lifted. `an` is the sky, and `an` is also god — one sign, two
"concepts" modern languages treat as separate. In Sumerian writing
every god's name is prefixed with the sign `an` because a god IS
a sky-being; sky and divine are one substrate fact. Paired against
[ki](ki.md) (down, earth, place): `an-ki` names the whole-cosmos
as the up-down pair. The up-pole of the vertical axis.
