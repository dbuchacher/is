# mah

category: 1m

Sumerian: **maḫ**
Gloss: great / magnificent / exalted / mighty
Source: literary and royal vocabulary for surpassing-great

## Definition

Greater than [gal](gal.md) — the quality-word for what rises
above even the big-ones. Where [gal](gal.md) is the bare big-
pole paired against [tur](tur.md), `maḫ` is the surpassing
quality: the god who is `maḫ` among gods, the king who is `maḫ`
among [lugal](lugal.md)-s, the mountain that is `maḫ` among
[kur](kur.md). Sumerian sometimes names the lion with this word
too — the surpassing-great animal is the same stroke as the
surpassing-great anything-else. One quality named across scales.
The exalted-pole of the size-cluster.
