# gal2

**seed** | category: 1h

Sumerian: **ŋal₂** (also spelled *gál*)
Gloss: to be / to exist / to be there (locative existence)
Source: throughout Sumerian texts, often as predicate for "X is at Y"

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a body in a place. Where [me](me.md) is
bare being (the thing IS), `ŋal₂` is located being (the thing IS
HERE, at this [ki](ki.md), in this place). The locative existence-
verb. Every "X is at Y" statement uses `ŋal₂`: the fact of being
is the fact of being-somewhere. The middle between bare being and
full specification.
