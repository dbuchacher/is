# ur5

**seed** | category: 1a

Sumerian: **ur₅**
Gloss: this / that / so / thus
Source: common demonstrative in literary texts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a thing without specifying near-or-far.
Where [ne](ne.md) points at what is close and [bi](bi.md) points
at what was already named, `ur₅` points with a looser grip — at
whatever the speaker has turned attention toward. Often used as
"thus" or "so": pointing at the shape of a situation rather than
a single body.
