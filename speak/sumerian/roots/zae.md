# zae

**seed** | category: 1a

Sumerian: **za-e**
Gloss: you (2nd person singular pronoun)
Source: attested throughout Sumerian literature

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the addressee — the body being spoken
to, the one whose [ŋeštug](geshtug.md) is receiving the utterance
of [ge26](ge26.md). The opposite pole to the speaker: every
utterance has a speaker and an addressee, and `za-e` is the one
not speaking but hearing-and-expected-to-answer. The addressee-
pole of every utterance.
