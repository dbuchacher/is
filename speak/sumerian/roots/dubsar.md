# dubsar

category: 3c

Sumerian: **dub-sar** (compound: *dub* "tablet" + *sar* "to write")
Gloss: scribe / tablet-writer
Source: the standard word for the scribal profession

## Definition

The one who [sar](sar.md)-s [dub](dub.md) — the tablet-writer.
Transparent compound: [dub](dub.md) + [sar](sar.md) = tablet-
writer. A Sumerian speaker hearing `dub-sar` hears both roots at
once: the clay and the mark-cutting stroke. Same composition
pattern as [lugal](lugal.md) and [egal](egal.md): the name
carries its derivation. The scribe IS the tablet-writer; no
separate opaque word. One of the most common professional titles
in Sumerian administration, and one of the most transparent.
