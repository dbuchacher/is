# ki

**seed** | category: 1e

Sumerian: **ki**
Gloss: earth / ground / place / here
Source: foundational morpheme; one of the two cosmic axis-poles

## Definition

Grounds by pointing down and around — the ground the speaker
stands on, the flat extent around the speaker's feet, the place
where the body [gub](gub.md)-s. `ki` is the earth as substance
(the ground one walks on) AND the earth as where-a-thing-is
(this place, that place, the place where X [gal2](gal2.md)-s).
Sumerian did not split place from earth: to be somewhere IS to
be on the earth. Paired against
[an](an.md) (up, sky, god): `an-ki` = whole-cosmos, the up-down
pair that contains everything.
