# me

**seed** | category: 1h

Sumerian: **me**
Gloss: to be / to exist / divine essence / operating principle
Source: throughout Sumerian; the famous *me* as "divine decrees" in Inanna and Enki

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what IS. When speaker and addressee both
see a body standing there, and nothing more needs to be said, the
verb for that standing-there is `me`. The bare being-verb — no
place-named, no quality, no becoming, just the fact that the
thing [gub](gub.md)-s in the world. Sumerian also uses `me` for
the operating-principles the gods hand out: the bare fact of
being IS the set of what holds. Being and principles-that-hold
share one word because both are the stable what-is.
