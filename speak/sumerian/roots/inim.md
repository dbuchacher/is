# inim

category: 1k

Sumerian: **inim** (also *enim*)
Gloss: word / speech / matter / thing / command
Source: common noun in literary and legal texts; the noun-form paired with [dug4](dug4.md)

## Definition

What comes out of a [ka](ka.md) when [dug4](dug4.md) happens —
the shaped-breath that carries [me](me.md)-ing from one body to
another. But `inim` is also the matter-at-hand: the thing being
spoken of, the business, the affair. Sumerian named word and
matter with one stroke because the word IS the matter made
audible. When a judge asks "what is the `inim`?" he asks both
"what is said?" and "what is the case?" — one question, one
word. The said-thing-and-the-thing-itself as one. Paired with
[dug4](dug4.md) (the speaking) as the said-thing-pole of the
speech-pair.
