# min

**seed** | category: 1b

Sumerian: **min**
Gloss: 2 / two
Source: second numeral; written as two wedges

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at two things — a pair, [dish](dish.md) and
another [dish](dish.md). The count that follows one. When speaker
and addressee are both present, they are `min` together. When two
oxen pull a plow, they are `min`. Written as two vertical wedges:
the sign IS the pointing-at-two made visible. The paired count.
