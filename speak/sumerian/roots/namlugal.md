# namlugal

category: 3c

Sumerian: **nam-lugal** (prefix *nam* "role-of" + *lugal* "king")
Gloss: kingship / royal-office / the role of king
Source: literary and royal inscriptions

## Definition

The role-of-king taken apart from the particular king holding it.
When a [lugal](lugal.md) dies and another takes his place, what
continues is `nam-lugal`: the office, the role as such.
Sumerian nominalizer-prefix `nam-` takes a concrete role-word
and makes it the role-as-such. Parallel to other `nam-`-prefixed nominalizations (e.g., the
wished-for life-as-such). Shows the compositional layer above the
transparent compound: first `lú` + `gal` = king, then `nam-` +
king = kingship. Each morpheme layer visible in the name.
