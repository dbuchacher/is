# sar

category: 1h

Sumerian: **sar**
Gloss: to write / to inscribe / to cut marks on a surface
Source: the verb for making marks on [dub](dub.md); central in scribal vocabulary

## Definition

To press the reed-stylus into wet [im](im.md) until marks hold
— the writing-stroke. A body that [sar](sar.md)-s cuts lines
into [dub](dub.md) so that later [igi](igi.md) can read them.
Where [dug4](dug4.md) is the speaking-out and
[geshtug](geshtug.md) is the hearing-in, `sar` is the making-of-
marks that fixes [inim](inim.md) on clay for later eyes. The
write-stroke primitive — the verb-side of the tablet-writer
role, paired with the noun-side by direct compounding.
