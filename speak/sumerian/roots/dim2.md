# dim2

category: 1h

Sumerian: **dim₂**
Gloss: to make / to create / to fashion / to build
Source: central verb in creation myths and craft texts

## Definition

To shape a new thing from a substance — what a potter does with
[im](im.md), what a smith does with metal, what a god does when
[an](an.md) and [ki](ki.md) are first made. Where [aka](aka.md)
is bare doing, `dim₂` is the more specific stroke of crafting-
into-existence. The creation-verb. Paired with [me](me.md): me
names what exists after dim₂ has been done. The making-new primitive.
