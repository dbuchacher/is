# namtil

category: 1l

Sumerian: **nam-til₃** (prefix *nam* "role-of" + *til* "live")
Gloss: life / salvation / the role of being-alive
Source: prayers, incantations, blessings

## Definition

The living-as-such taken apart from the particular body doing the
[til](til.md)-ing. When a Sumerian prays for `nam-til₃` the prayer
asks for the whole-quality-of-living to be granted: not one
breath, not one day, the whole living-condition. `nam-` prefix
turns a concrete verb into the quality-as-such. Life-in-general
derived from [til](til.md) (live-and-complete) by one prefix.
Transparent composition at the role layer.
