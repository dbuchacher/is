# gi4

category: 1i

Sumerian: **gi₄** (also *gi*)
Gloss: to return / to come back / to restore / to do again
Source: extremely common verbal root

## Definition

To go back to where the [du](du.md) began — the walking-away
followed by the coming-back. When a messenger has been sent and
returns, `gi₄`. When a broken thing is restored to its state, `gi₄`.
When something done is done again, `gi₄`. One verb for the whole
pattern of going-out-and-returning, of reversal, of repetition.
The closing-of-the-walk primitive: the body [du](du.md)-s one
way, then `gi₄`-s the other. Paired against forward motion as
the return-pole. Loop-closure named as a verb.
