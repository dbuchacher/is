# lugal

category: 3c

Sumerian: **lugal** (compound: *lú* "man" + *gal* "big")
Gloss: king
Source: omnipresent in royal inscriptions

## Definition

`lú-gal` — the big-man, the man who is [gal](gal.md). Sumerian's
word for king IS the transparent compound: the [gal](gal.md) one
of the men. No separate opaque root: the name tells the role.
When a speaker says `lugal`, an addressee hears "big-man" and
knows it points at the ruler. THE RULE at work in the vocabulary:
one substrate operation (gal = bigger than baseline) applied at
the human-rank scale gives the king. The role IS in the name.
