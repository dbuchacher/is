# ge26

**seed** | category: 1a

Sumerian: **ŋe₂₆** (also written *ŋe₂₆-e*)
Gloss: I (1st person singular pronoun)
Source: attested throughout Sumerian literary and administrative texts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the speaker — the one from whom the
utterance comes, the body whose mouth ([ka](ka.md)) is speaking.
The self-pole of every utterance: when the speaker says `ŋe₂₆`,
the referent is the speaking body itself. Paired against
[zae](zae.md) (addressee) and [ene](ene.md) (third-body).
