# igi

**seed** | category: 1j

Sumerian: **igi**
Gloss: eye / face / front / to see / in front of
Source: ubiquitous body-part and verb; also used as preposition "before"

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the seeing-organ AND the seeing itself —
Sumerian does not split the face-part from the operation the face
does. The two eyes of a body are `igi`, and so is the act of
[igi](igi.md)-ing (looking, seeing). The front of a body is `igi`
because the face is the front; "in front of X" uses `igi` because
the front of a body is where its eyes point. One morpheme for the
organ, the act, and the line the act points along. The seeing-
pole of perception.
