# enki

category: 3c

Sumerian: **en-ki** (compound: *en* "lord" + *ki* "earth/place")
Gloss: Enki — the lord of earth / god of fresh waters / god of wisdom
Source: creation and wisdom myths; chief god of Eridu; master of [abzu](abzu.md)

## Definition

The lord-of-the-ground, the [en](en.md) whose domain is
[ki](ki.md) and the [abzu](abzu.md) beneath it. Transparent
compound: [en](en.md) + [ki](ki.md) = lord-of-the-earth-layer.
His seat is in the freshwater deep under the plain. In the
divine triad he holds the lower pole — paired with [an](an.md)
(upper-pole sky-god) and [enlil](enlil.md) (middle-pole
wind-god). Because [abzu](abzu.md) is both deep-water and
source-of-[zu](zu.md) (knowing), the lord-of-earth is also the
lord-of-wisdom: the god's name carries his domain, and the
domain carries two meanings (water AND knowing) as one.
