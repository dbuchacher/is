# ka

**seed** | category: 1d

Sumerian: **ka**
Gloss: mouth / gate / opening / pass-through point
Source: body-part and noun for any threshold or opening

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the opening of the body through which
[dug4](dug4.md) comes out and food comes in — the hole between
lips, the threshold of [ge26](ge26.md) and [zae](zae.md) in
communication. But `ka` is also the city-gate: the opening in the
wall of a settlement, the pass-through between inside and outside
at the scale of a whole city. Body-mouth and city-gate share one
word because they share one operation: bounded threshold through
which things pass. The opening-primitive — at the body scale
the opening is a mouth, at the city scale it is a gate, both are
`ka`.
