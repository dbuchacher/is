# a

**seed** | category: 1e

Sumerian: **a**
Gloss: water
Source: ubiquitous; written with a single wedge-sign

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the liquid that falls from [an](an.md) as
rain, runs across [ki](ki.md) in rivers, fills cups and irrigation
channels. One syllable, one sign, one substance — the simplest
possible name for the simplest possible matter a body needs to
[til](til.md). When the speaker points at a river, a cup, a
standing pool, the puddle after rain, the word is `a`.
