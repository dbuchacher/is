# kur

category: 1e

Sumerian: **kur**
Gloss: mountain / foreign land / netherworld
Source: attested throughout myth and administration

## Definition

What is not-the-flat-plain — anything past where [ki](ki.md) goes
smooth. The mountain in the east where the sun [ud](ud.md)
rises: `kur`. The foreign lands beyond Sumer where different
people [me](me.md): `kur`. The underworld where the dead go after
they stop [til](til.md)-ing: `kur`. Sumerian grouped all
not-here-places under one word. From the perspective of a body
standing on Sumer's alluvial plain, mountain-and-foreign-land-
and-netherworld are all the same thing: the unknown elsewhere.
The not-here-pole of the here-there pair.
