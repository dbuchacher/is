# du

**seed** | category: 1i

Sumerian: **du / ŋen**
Gloss: to go / walk / come
Source: ubiquitous motion verb

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a body moving from one [ki](ki.md) to
another — the feet lifting, the body traveling across the ground.
`du` covers both going-away (from speaker) and coming-toward
(to speaker); directional specification is added separately with
suffixes. The bare motion verb of Sumerian: [me](me.md) is
still-being, `du` is walking-being.
