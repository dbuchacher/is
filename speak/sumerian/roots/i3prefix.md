# i3prefix

category: 4c

Sumerian: **i₃-** (verbal prefix; also *ì-*)
Gloss: the remote/neutral finite-prefix / third-party marker
Source: standard finite-prefix; often used where the action is neither close nor middle-voice

## Definition

The stroke a speaker puts on a verb when the action sits away
from [ge26](ge26.md) and away from the hearer — on a body that
is neither here nor the undergoer. Where [muprefix](muprefix.md)
is proximal and the middle-voice prefix marks the action as
undergone, `i₃-` is the remote-third pole: the verb whose doing
is pointed at [ene](ene.md) from a distance. The distal-pole of the three
verb-prefixes. Not a root that stands alone — only a morpheme fit
on the front of other verbs. Together the three prefixes mark the
three deictic stances of the verb: near, on-the-body, and far.
