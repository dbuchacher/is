# Sumerian seeds

Same discipline as PIE seeds. These are the morphemes allowed to
ground in deixis (pointing at world). All non-seed morphemes must
reduce to seeds + already-defined morphemes.

## 1a — Pronouns and Deictics (6)

- **ŋe26** — I (1st person, speaker). Points at self.
- **za-e** — you (2nd person, addressee). Points at addressee.
- **e-ne** — he/she (3rd person, the other). Points at
  non-speaker, non-addressee.
- **bi** — 3rd-person possessive / demonstrative (that-of-his, that).
  Points at something previously named.
- **ur5** — this / that (general demonstrative). Points at
  something.
- **ne** — this / this-here. Proximal pointer.

## 1h — Being / basic actions (3)

- **me** — to be / to exist / divine essence. The bare being-verb.
  (Also the Sumerian word for the abstract "operating principles"
  handed out by gods — the word ITSELF is the substrate claim,
  because "to be" and "the principles that ARE" are one word.)
- **ŋal2** — to be / to exist / to be there. Locative being-verb.
- **til** — to live / complete / finish. Life as loop-closure.

## 1i — Motion (2)

- **du / ŋen** — to go / walk / come.
- **gub** — to stand (still). The stationary body.

## 1j — Perception (3)

- **igi** — eye / face / to see / the front. Seeing-organ AND the
  seeing.
- **ŋeštug** — ear / wisdom / to hear / to understand. Hearing-organ
  AND the hearing AND the understanding.
- **zu** — tooth / to know / to grasp. Biting-grasp AND cognitive-
  grasp.

## 1k — Speech (1)

- **dug4** — to speak / say.

## 1e — Natural phenomena (cosmic axes) (3)

- **an** — sky / heaven / god / divine. Up-axis + sacred-axis as
  one sign. Points up.
- **ki** — earth / ground / place / here. Down-axis + location-axis
  as one sign. Points down and around.
- **a** — water. The simplest sign, the simplest substance.

## 1d — Body (3)

- **saŋ** — head / top / first / chief.
- **šà** — heart / interior / middle. The center of a body.
- **ka** — mouth / gate / opening. Any pass-through point.

## 1m — Qualities (2)

- **gal** — big / great.
- **tur** — small.

## 1b — Numerals (2)

- **diš** — 1. The first count.
- **min** — 2. The paired count.

## Total: 25 seeds

These 25 seeds ground in deixis (pointing / direct experience).
Every other Sumerian morpheme must be definable using ONLY these +
already-defined non-seeds.

## Differences from PIE seed set

- **No dual pronouns** — Sumerian had no grammaticalized dual.
- **Fewer motion verbs** — Sumerian's core motion is carried by
  `du / ŋen` as a single seed; other motion words (`e3` rise, `ku4`
  enter, `gub` stand) are either seeds or derive from composition.
- **Polysemy-rich seeds** — many Sumerian seeds name multiple
  concepts (an = sky+god, igi = eye+see, zu = tooth+know). This
  IS the cross-test evidence: Sumerian preserved unity PIE split.
