# ph2ter

category: 1c

PIE: *ph₂tḗr
Gloss: father
Descendants: father, Latin *pater*, Greek *patēr*, Sanskrit *pitr̥*

## Definition

The [h2wiH-ro](h2wiH-ro.md) who does [peh2](peh2.md) — the man
standing over the hearth, the flock, the child, holding the
bounded [h1en](h1en.md) against what comes at it from outside.
`ph2ter` names the protecting stance, not the begetting: the
begetting is [genh1](genh1.md)'s work. The root binds
[peh2](peh2.md) into a role the body takes on, and the same body
that [bher](bher.md)-s food back to the fire is the one the
child points at and calls by this word. Stands as father-pole of
the two-poles the young hold between.
