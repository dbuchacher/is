# dhugh2ter

category: 1c

PIE: *dʰugh₂tḗr
Gloss: daughter
Descendants: daughter, Greek *thugatēr*, Sanskrit *duhitr̥*

## Definition

The [gwen](gwen.md)-to-be that [ph2ter](ph2ter.md) and
[meh2ter](meh2ter.md) brought forth through [genh1](genh1.md) —
the young female of the down-generation. The root is tied by old
tradition to the milk-drawing motion the young girl did at the
byre, the one who [peh3](peh3.md)-s from the beast, though the
word had already widened to name the child herself. Paired with
[suHnus](suHnus.md) as the two kinds of young kin the parent-
pair bring forth.
