# h1dont

category: 1d

PIE: *h₁dónt-
Gloss: tooth
Descendants: tooth, Latin *dens/dentis*, Greek *odōn*

## Definition

The hard white row set [h1en](h1en.md) the mouth of
[kap-ut](kap-ut.md) — the bone-pieces that bite through meat
before [dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md) pushes it down. `h1dont` names
the tooth as the cutting-and-grinding tool the body carries
always: what the [wlkwos](wlkwos.md) shows when it snarls,
what the child grows painfully one by one, what stays in the
skull long after the flesh goes. Points at a single tooth or
at the whole row depending on context. Hard like
[h2ost](h2ost.md) because it is bone of a kind, but rooted in
the jaw where it can strike food. Paired against
[dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md): tongue soft and moving, tooth hard and
still.
