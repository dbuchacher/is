# wekw

**seed** | category: 1k

PIE: *wekʷ-
Gloss: to speak
Descendants: voice, vocal, Latin *vox*, Greek *epos*

## Definition

To put out through the open mouth breath-shaped sound that
another ear can catch through [h2ews-b](h2ews-b.md) — the
outward stroke that pairs with the inward stroke of
[weid](weid.md) and [h2ews-b](h2ews-b.md). What [men](men.md)
holds inside, [wekw](wekw.md) pushes back out at [h1e](h1e.md);
the hearer's [men](men.md) then holds it too, and the loop of
[kwel](kwel.md) closes between two selves.
