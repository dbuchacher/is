# gweyh3

category: 1h

PIE: *gʷeyh₃-
Gloss: to live (biological)
Descendants: quick, vital, vivid, Latin *vivere*

## Definition

To be the kind of thing that breathes ([h2enh1](h2enh1.md)),
eats ([h1ed](h1ed.md)), drinks ([peh3](peh3.md)), moves
([h1ey](h1ey.md)) on its own — not stone, not [wodr](wodr.md),
not the dead body laid in [dheghom](dheghom.md), but the warm
walking-body that holds together only as long as it keeps doing
those strokes. `gweyh3` names the whole-of-those-strokes as one
pointing: being-alive. Where [h1es](h1es.md) names bare holding,
`gweyh3` names the kind of holding only a body-with-breath does
— the quick one, the vivid one. Paired against [mer](mer.md)
(the moment the strokes stop) as the life/death axis of the
body. The old English "quick" for alive keeps this shade: the
living is what moves, the dead is what stays still.
