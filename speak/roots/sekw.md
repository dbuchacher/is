# sekw

category: 1i

PIE: *sekʷ-
Gloss: to follow
Descendants: sequel, second, social, Latin *sequi*, Greek *hepomai*

## Definition

To walk behind another body along the same path — [h1eg](h1eg.md)
sets foot where the lead-body's [pods](pods.md) set foot first,
and keeps this lag while the two move. Where [h1ey](h1ey.md)
names the going-away of a body by itself and [gwem](gwem.md)
names the coming-toward, `sekw` names the walking-after: a line
of bodies in order, the second stepping in the track of the
first. The root carries the shade of ordered sequence — what
comes next in a line — so its descendants give "second" (the
one that follows [sem](sem.md) one, the following-one) and
"social" (the binding that comes from walking together).
Sibling with [seh2g](seh2g.md) (the track-following that is
also seeking).
