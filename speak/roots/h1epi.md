# h1epi

category: 1n

PIE: *h₁epi / *opi
Gloss: at / near
Descendants: epi-, ob-, Latin *ob*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a spot pressed close against [ke](ke.md) —
not the here itself, but the rim just touching it, the spot that
[gwem](gwem.md) has already reached. Where [h1e](h1e.md) names a
there held far off, [h1epi](h1epi.md) names a there pulled in
against the here, resting on it or almost so: the close-to side,
the nearly-touching side, the spot the hand falls on when it
lowers onto what sits beside the speaker.
