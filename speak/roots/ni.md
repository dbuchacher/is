# ni

category: 1n

PIE: *ni-
Gloss: down
Descendants: nether

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the drop from [ke](ke.md) toward [upo](upo.md) —
the falling leg of the vertical split, the way [h1ey](h1ey.md)
moves when it lets go of its height. Where [upo](upo.md) names
the ground-ward side held still below, [ni](ni.md) names the
going-down itself: the sinking from above to beneath, the mirror
of [h2en](h2en.md)'s tilt upward, the line along which what was
lifted drops back toward the earth.

## Groups

- [vertical-axis](vertical-axis.md) — the sinking-down motion along the sky-to-ground line
- [down-drop](down-drop.md) — the sinking itself, paired with [upo](upo.md)'s held-low side
