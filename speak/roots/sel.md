# sel

category: 1h

PIE: *sel-
Gloss: to take / grasp
Descendants: select, consult, Latin *seligere*

## Definition

To reach past the rest and pick out one — the grasp that chooses
which thing to take, where [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) closes on one item
while [h3okw](h3okw.md) tells the hand to skip the others. Where
[ghabh](ghabh.md) names the keeping-after, `sel` names the
picking: the stroke paired with seeing, the take that sorts
[sem](sem.md) (one) out of the many. A gatherer `sel`-s berries,
leaving the unripe; a hunter `sel`-s the wounded from the herd;
a speaker `sel`-s words from the throng in the head. Picking-
shade of the polysemy headed by [ghabh](ghabh.md).
