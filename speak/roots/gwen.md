# gwen

category: 1c

PIE: *gʷḗn
Gloss: woman
Descendants: queen, Greek *gunē* (gynecology), Sanskrit *gnā*

## Definition

The adult female of the band — the one who stands full-grown,
able to carry a child through [bher](bher.md) and feed it at her
own breast. The root is the plain female-adult, standing as the
female-pole of the full pair. `gwen` is the woman plain — the
female standing on her own [pods](pods.md), past the child-years
and before the way of [mer](mer.md). Later the word rose through
royal tongues to name the king's mate — the queen — because the
line-head's partner held weight beside him.
