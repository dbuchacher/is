# mer

category: 1h

PIE: *mer-
Gloss: to die
Descendants: mortal, murder, Latin *mori*, Russian *smertʹ*

## Definition

The moment the body stops doing what [gweyh3](gweyh3.md) names
— breath ([h2enh1](h2enh1.md)) halts, warmth drains into
[dheghom](dheghom.md), the eyes ([h3okw](h3okw.md)) go still,
the walking-body becomes a laid body that [legh](legh.md)-s and
will not rise. Where [swep](swep.md) is the reversible crossing
out of wakefulness, `mer` is the one-way crossing: the body
[h1es](h1es.md)-es no more, cannot [bhuH](bhuH.md) back. A
hunter `mer`-s at the end of the fight; a mother `mer`-s at
childbirth; a beast `mer`-s by the spear. Paired with
[gweyh3](gweyh3.md) as the two poles of body-being: the life/death
axis. What `mer`-s returns to [dheghom](dheghom.md) and becomes
earth-stuff again.
