# weid

**seed** | category: 1j

PIE: *weid- / *weyd-
Gloss: to see / know
Descendants: wit, wise, video, view, idea, Latin *videre*, Greek *oida/idein*, Sanskrit *veda*

## Definition

To take in through the open eye what stands at [h1e](h1e.md) and
carry it inward so that [men](men.md) holds it after — the
looking and the having-seen folded together, since in PIE once
the light has reached you it is already known. Sight is not a
first step before knowing; seeing has-seen built in.
