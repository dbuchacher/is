# bhey

category: 1l

PIE: *bʰey- / *bʰeyh₂-
Gloss: to fear
Descendants: abide?, Old Russian *bojatʹ*

## Definition

To see or hear what could kill and have the body start at
once — the cold stroke that throws [h1eg](h1eg.md) backward
before [men](men.md) has caught up. `bhey` is the alarm-run-
away: the startle at the snapped twig behind, the lurch at the
[wlkwos](wlkwos.md)'s eye in dark, the body's own no-stay that
pulls the feet into [bhegw](bhegw.md) flight. It is fear as
movement-outward, the feet already going before the thought;
[men](men.md) only catches up after, naming what the body
already knew. Paired with [h1ng](h1ng.md): `bhey` is the
outward startle, `h1ng` is the inward constriction — two shapes
of the same warning against danger.
