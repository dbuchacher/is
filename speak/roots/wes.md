# wes

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *wes-
Gloss: to stay / dwell
Descendants: was, were, German *war*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at a thing that holds its place across many lookings.
[h1eg](h1eg.md) points at the hearth today and it is there; points
tomorrow and it is still there. That through-holding is `wes`: the
dwelling of a thing in its place, the staying that lets a spot become
a home. Where [bhuH](bhuH.md) is the upward edge into
[h1es](h1es.md), `wes` is the downward settling after — the
staying-put that takes [steh2](steh2.md) or [sed](sed.md) as its
body-posture. A camp `wes`-es at a spring; the spring itself
[h1es](h1es.md); the moment the camp arrived was [bhuH](bhuH.md).
