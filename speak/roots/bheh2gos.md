# bheh2gos

category: 1g

PIE: *bʰeh₂ǵos
Gloss: beech / oak
Descendants: beech, Latin *fagus*

## Definition

A named [deru](deru.md) — the particular great tree whose nuts
feed the hunter and whose wood takes the axe. Where
[deru](deru.md) holds tree-and-firmness as one substance,
`bheh2gos` points at this one kind: the broad canopy, the smooth
pale bark, the mast on the ground in cold months that small
beasts and foragers can eat. Stands tall at the edge of forest;
its leaves turn color before falling. The first named particular
under the general [deru](deru.md), as the named four-footed
hunter is the first named particular under the general wild
beast.
