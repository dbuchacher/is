# teuh2

category: 1m

PIE: *teuh₂-
Gloss: to swell
Descendants: thumb, tumor, Latin *tumere*

## Definition

To rise-outward from within — what a thing does when its inside
pushes its skin outward into a rounded fullness. `teuh2` names
the belly of the [gwen](gwen.md) carrying the child, the udder
of the doe before the fawn takes, the ripe berry tight on its
stem, the bread-paste rising under the cloth, the bruise
swelling on the struck knee ([genu](genu.md)), the river
([wodr](wodr.md)) pushing out of its banks after heavy rain. To
`teuh2` is to get bigger from the inside — not by being piled
onto from outside but by filling with more of itself until the
surface stretches. The thick thumb ([ghesr](ghesr.md)-end) took
its name here: the swelled one among the fingers. Kin to growth
but narrower — growth is [bhuH](bhuH.md)-ing in size over time;
`teuh2` is the fat-pushing-out of the moment.
