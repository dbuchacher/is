# derk

category: 1j

PIE: *derḱ-
Gloss: to see
Descendants: Latin *cernere* (discern), Greek *derkomai*

## Definition

To fix the open [h3okw](h3okw.md) on one spot and hold it there
until the shape breaks clear — the piercing-sight that sorts one
thing from another, where [weid](weid.md) is the broader taking-in
of what light brings. `derk` is the hard-look: the hunter picking
the [wlkwos](wlkwos.md) from shadow, the eye that narrows at
[h1e](h1e.md) and cuts edges out of the muddle. What
[weid](weid.md) gathers, `derk` sharpens into this-not-that,
and [men](men.md) afterward holds the sorted shape.
