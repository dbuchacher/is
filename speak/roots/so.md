# so

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *so- / *seh₂- / *tod
Gloss: "this / that"
Descendants: this, the, Greek *ho/hē/to*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at there — the speaker marks out a spot held
in shared attention, neither fully near nor fully far, as the one
being talked about right now. No reduction; the marking gesture is
the meaning.
