# per

**seed** | category: 1i

PIE: *per-
Gloss: to go through / forward
Descendants: for, fore, through, per-, Latin *per*

## Definition

To keep stepping straight on past what stood in the way — the
walking of [h1ey](h1ey.md) pushed forward through
[h1en](h1en.md) and out the far side, not stopping at the
boundary but crossing it. Where [kwel](kwel.md) bends the path
back, [per](per.md) holds the line and drives onward along it.
