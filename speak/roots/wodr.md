# wodr

category: 1e

PIE: *wódr̥ / *wed-
Gloss: water
Descendants: water, vodka, Greek *hudōr*

## Definition

Water as the cold clear stuff that fills the cup, that the tongue
([dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md)) meets, that [peh3](peh3.md) takes into
the body — the draught, the portion, the substance itself held in
a shape the hand can carry. Where [h2ekweh2](h2ekweh2.md) names
the water that runs loose on the land, `wodr` names the water
that [h1eg](h1eg.md) drinks, the skin of a full pouch, the
still-surface of a stored pool in a hollow stone. Both are the
same clear stuff; the two roots split by how the speaker meets
it. `wodr` is water-as-drunk, water-as-held; the kind that enters
the body against [ter-s](ter-s.md) and makes the body keep
holding. Without it the body fails in a handful of days — the
most necessary substance the mouth takes in after air itself.
