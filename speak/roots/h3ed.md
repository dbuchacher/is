# h3ed

category: 1j

PIE: *h₃ed-
Gloss: to smell
Descendants: odor, olfactory, Latin *odor*

## Definition

To take in through the [nas](nas.md) what the air carries —
smoke off [peh2wr](peh2wr.md), rot off dead flesh, the wet dirt
after rain, the hot breath of the [wlkwos](wlkwos.md) still near.
`h3ed` is the reading that works in the dark when [weid](weid.md)
fails and through brush when [spek](spek.md) cannot cut: the body
catches what blew in on [h2weh1](h2weh1.md) and names it before
the eye has seen. Paired with [gusH](gusH.md): smell is the
taste-at-a-distance, taste is the smell-held-in-the-mouth, both
catching what the world is made of through the tongue-and-nose
pair.
