# seh2kro

category: 3d

PIE: *seh₂k-ro-
Gloss: sacred / bound
Descendants: sacred, saint, Latin *sacer*

## Definition

A thing bound off from daily use and set apart for the
[deiwos](deiwos.md) — the stone, the grove, the vessel, the man
marked so that ordinary hands do not touch it. `seh2kro` names the
binding-off itself: what was open to the folk's common handling
is now held separate, hedged by rule, approached only through the
right ritual. The stroke is a [dwis](dwis.md) stroke laid across
the world: this side common, that side bound. Latin *sacer* keeps
the full edge (both "holy" and "cursed" descend from it, because
what is bound-off is both protected and dangerous). The bound
thing has been put out of [h1eg](h1eg.md)'s grasp and into the
[dyews](dyews.md)-side of the world. The state-of-being-bound, the
boundary-stroke around anything given over to [deiwos](deiwos.md).
