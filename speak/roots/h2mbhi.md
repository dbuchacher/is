# h2mbhi

category: 1n

PIE: *h₂m̥bʰi-
Gloss: around / on both sides
Descendants: ambi-, amphi-, Latin *ambo*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at both flanks of a here at once — the speaker
swings the pointing from [ke](ke.md) out to one side and then to
the other, closing the sweep so the here is ringed on every side.
Where [dwis](dwis.md) splits one into two standing apart,
[h2mbhi](h2mbhi.md) holds the two sides together as a surrounding
wrap, the encircling hand that takes in what lies between.

## Groups

- [surround-zone](surround-zone.md) — the wrapping-around gesture itself
- [both-sides](both-sides.md) — the two-flank pairing that extends [dwis](dwis.md)
