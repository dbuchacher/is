# deik

category: 1k

PIE: *deik̂-
Gloss: to show / say
Descendants: teach, dictate, index, Latin *dicere*, Greek *deiknumi*

## Definition

To point at a thing and say it in the same stroke — the finger
and the mouth working as one. `deik` is where the pointing-hand
of [ke](ke.md) meets the speaking of [wekw](wekw.md): the elder
points at the track and says what made it, at the plant and says
which one eats which one kills, at the child and says his name.
Teaching springs from here — to teach is to show-and-say over
and over until the young one's [men](men.md) holds the pairing.
"Index" is the finger that does the pointing; "dictate" is the
saying-that-commands, where what is pointed-at has to be made so
by the one who hears.
