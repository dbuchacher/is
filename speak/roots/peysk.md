# peysk

category: 1f

PIE: *péysḱ-
Gloss: fish
Descendants: fish, Latin *piscis*

## Definition

The [ghwer](ghwer.md) that lives [h1en](h1en.md) the water
([wodr](wodr.md)) — a cold silver body without [pods](pods.md),
moving by bending through the stream, breathing something other
than what [h1eg](h1eg.md) breathes. `peysk` names the beast
reached through the surface: the hunter leans over the river and
sees it hang below him, and must strike fast with spear or trap
before it [bhugh](bhugh.md)-s away into deeper water. Lives in
shoals that turn as [sem](sem.md) — many becoming one flash and
then gone. Stays eaten only if caught; forgets the hook as soon
as the hunter leaves.
