# uper

category: 1n

PIE: *upér-
Gloss: above / over
Descendants: over, hyper-, super-, Latin *super*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing past [h2en](h2en.md) — not merely the sky-side
of here ([ke](ke.md)) but the reached-over side, the part that
arches above and clears what stands between. Where [h2en](h2en.md)
names the upper side, [uper](uper.md) names the side that passes
over the top and lies on the far flank of what is raised, the
mirror-pole of [upo](upo.md) across the vertical split.
