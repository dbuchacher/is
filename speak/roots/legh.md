# legh

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *legʰ-
Gloss: to lie down
Descendants: lay, lie, lair, Greek *lechos*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at a body laid long-side onto [dheghom](dheghom.md):
head, torso, and [pods](pods.md) all at the same level, the up-axis
of [steh2](steh2.md) collapsed flat against the ground. The full
weight of the body spreads across its length rather than stacking on
feet or haunches. A sleeper `legh`-s; a dead beast `legh`-s; a
hunter `legh`-s behind a ridge to stay out of sight. Where
[sed](sed.md) halves the upright stack, `legh` erases it — the
deepest settling, the posture closest to [dheghom](dheghom.md) itself.
It carries into descendants the meaning of the bedding-place (lair)
where a body regularly `legh`-s.
