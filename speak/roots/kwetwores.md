# kwetwores

category: 1b

PIE: *kʷetwóres
Gloss: four
Descendants: four, Latin *quattuor*, Greek *tettares*

## Definition

The pace after [treyes](treyes.md): two [dwoh1](dwoh1.md) pairs
held together, or the full tally of one [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) minus
the thumb. [h1eg](h1eg.md) lays out four [ke](ke.md) spots and
the eye no longer takes them whole in a single [weid](weid.md) —
the count now needs [men](men.md) to keep the earlier spots
alive while new ones add on. Four is the first number that leans
entirely on inward holding: the pair-of-pairs that the hand and
the foot ([pods](pods.md)) wear as their four-cornered shape.
