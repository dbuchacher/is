# ghelh3

category: 1m

PIE: *ǵʰelh₃-
Gloss: yellow / green / gold
Descendants: yellow, gold, gall, Latin *helvus*

## Definition

The band of color the eye reads between [h1rewdh](h1rewdh.md)
and the green of [wesr](wesr.md)-growth — the yellow-gold of
[soh2wl](soh2wl.md) at noon, the yellow-green of new
[leyp](leyp.md), the shine of gold pulled from streambed, the
bitter yellow of gall in the opened [yekwr](yekwr.md) of a kill.
`ghelh3` names the quality that sits where grass rises and where
ripe grain stands — the color of living growth and of the sun
that feeds it. One word for yellow and green because the speaker
sees them as one band: the color of young leaves turning to
summer, of grass going from pale to dark across the season.
Later tongues split this band, but here it holds as one naming.
