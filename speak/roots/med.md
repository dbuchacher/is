# med

category: 1j

PIE: *med-
Gloss: to measure / think
Descendants: meditate, medicine, mete, Latin *mederi*, Greek *medomai*

## Definition

To hold something against a measure — a hand-span, a known mark,
a rule carried in [men](men.md) — and see how it stands. `med` is
the weighing-thought: how far the spear must fly, how much meat
for how many mouths, how deep the cut into sick flesh so the body
lives. The healer measures the wound and mixes the herb; the
elder measures the dispute and says the fair share; the builder
measures the beam. Where [men](men.md) holds what was seen, `med` is the middle
step: taking the measure, fitting thing to thing, finding the
just amount — before the settling-thought closes with yes.
