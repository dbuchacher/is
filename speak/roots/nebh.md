# nebh

category: 1d

PIE: *nēbʰ-
Gloss: navel / cloud
Descendants: navel, Latin *umbilicus*, nebula

## Definition

The knot in the middle of [krep](krep.md) where the cord once
joined child to [meh2ter](meh2ter.md) — the small puckered mark
a speaker can touch and say "here I came from." `nebh` holds
together two pointings that to the Paleolithic eye are one: the
navel-knot on the belly and the cloud-knot in the sky, because
both are round middle-swellings suspended in a larger thing —
one in the skin, one in the air. The body's middle ([medhyos](medhyos.md))
bears this mark, and the same word names the misty puff overhead.
The later Latin *umbilicus* and the scientific "nebula" hold
different halves of one Paleolithic unity.
