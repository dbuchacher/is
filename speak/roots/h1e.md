# h1e

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *h₁e- / *tod
Gloss: demonstrative "that / it"
Descendants: it, that, the, Latin *is/id*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at there — the speaker points away from self
at a spot held in shared attention but not close at hand. No
reduction; the far outward gesture is the meaning.
