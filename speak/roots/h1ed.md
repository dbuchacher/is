# h1ed

category: 1h

PIE: *h₁ed-
Gloss: to eat
Descendants: eat, edible, Latin *edere*, Greek *esthiō*

## Definition

To take food in [mr-gh](mr-gh.md), bring it to [h1dont](h1dont.md),
and pass it past [dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md) into the body — the stroke by
which the outside world becomes the inside. [h1eg](h1eg.md) chews
meat, nuts, root; the teeth break it small; the tongue sends it down
past the throat where it stops being not-self and starts feeding the
body that [gweyh3](gweyh3.md)-s. Without `h1ed`, the body stops
[gweyh3](gweyh3.md)-ing and [mer](mer.md)-s. Paired with
[peh3](peh3.md) (the drinking that does the same work with water) and
with [peh2](peh2.md) (the feed-and-protect that gives food to another
mouth). The root's edge is chew-and-swallow: what crosses into the
body through teeth, not through a wound.
