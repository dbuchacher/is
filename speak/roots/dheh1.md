# dheh1

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *dʰeh₁-
Gloss: to put / do / make
Descendants: **do**, **deed**, **thesis**, face, fact, Latin *facere*, Greek *tithēmi*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing and placing: [h1eg](h1eg.md) takes a thing in
[mr-gh](mr-gh.md) (hand) and puts it at the spot [ke](ke.md) marks —
the stone onto the hearth, the stake into [dheghom](dheghom.md), the
meat onto the fire. The root names the act that causes a thing to
[h1es](h1es.md) where it did not before: to `dheh1` is to make-hold.
It is the write-side edge: before the act, the spot was empty;
after, the thing stays there and [wes](wes.md)-es. From that placing
the root widens into shaping (to `dheh1` a pot from clay) and into
doing a deed at all — any act that leaves a thing standing where
[h1eg](h1eg.md) wanted it. Cousin of [deh3](deh3.md) (give-into-another's-
hand) and [bher](bher.md) (carry-across-space before placing).
