# meh2ter

category: 1c

PIE: *méh₂tēr
Gloss: mother
Descendants: mother, Latin *mater*, Greek *mētēr*

## Definition

The [gwen](gwen.md) who [bher](bher.md)-s the child in the womb,
then feeds it from her own body — the first [ke](ke.md) the
infant points at, the first mouth-sound *ma* the infant makes
while feeding. `meh2ter` carries both the nursing motion and the
standing-over-the-young that binds it to [peh2](peh2.md): she
feeds and she shields. The root is the child's-eye-view of the
nearest adult, the one whose [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) and whose warmth
hold the whole early world. Paired with [ph2ter](ph2ter.md) as
the two poles the young stand between.
