# h1regwos

category: 1e

PIE: *h₁regʷos
Gloss: dark
Descendants: Erebus, Greek *erebos*

## Definition

The dark itself — not the [nokwts](nokwts.md) that is the span of
night, but the pure unlit state, the black the eye meets when no
[leuk](leuk.md) has reached it. `h1regwos` is what fills a cave
past the last light, what lies under stones, what the fire-circle
holds off by a few paces and no more. Where [nokwts](nokwts.md)
is a time-span (the dark hours), `h1regwos` is a quality the
speaker meets anywhere [leuk](leuk.md) is absent — the dark in
the belly of the earth, the dark behind the hunter when she
faces the fire, the dark the dying eye sinks into. Paired against
[leuk](leuk.md) as the negative pole of what lets [weid](weid.md)
work: where `h1regwos` holds, the eye finds nothing.
