# serp

category: 1f

PIE: *serp-
Gloss: snake / creep
Descendants: serpent, Latin *serpere*

## Definition

The [ghwer](ghwer.md) that goes without [pods](pods.md) — a long
body pressed flat against [dheghom](dheghom.md), moving by
[kwel](kwel.md)-ing its own length in waves so that belly slides
forward. `serp` names the going first, the beast second: it is
the one whose [h1ey](h1ey.md) is this creeping, the way of going
that belongs to no other beast. [h1eg](h1eg.md) watching it move
calls it by the motion — as if the verb became a noun the moment
a speaker pointed and said "the thing that creeps." Its tooth
carries death into a foot that steps wrong; its body vanishes
under a stone before the hand reaches.
