# enteros

category: 1n

PIE: *énteros
Gloss: between / within
Descendants: inter-, enter, internal, Latin *inter*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the held-in zone that lies flanked by more
than one [ke](ke.md) — where [h1en](h1en.md) marks the inside of a
single boundary, [enteros](enteros.md) marks the stretch that sits
caught between two or more such heres. The speaker points at the
gap, not the walls — the region cupped by [h2mbhi](h2mbhi.md) on
its flanks, the in-between zone that [per](per.md) crosses on its
way through.

## Groups

- [surround-zone](surround-zone.md) — the zone held by a surrounding [h2mbhi](h2mbhi.md)
- [middle-zone](middle-zone.md) — the between-stretch, extending [h1en](h1en.md) from one wall to many
