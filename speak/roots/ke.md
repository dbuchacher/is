# ke

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *ḱe-
Gloss: "this / here" (proximal)
Descendants: he, here, hither

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at here — the speaker points close to self, at
a spot within arm's reach of the speaker-pole. No reduction; the
close-in gesture is the meaning.
