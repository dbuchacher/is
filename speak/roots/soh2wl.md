# soh2wl

category: 1e

PIE: *sóh₂wl̥ / *sh₂un-
Gloss: sun
Descendants: sun, solar, Latin *sol*, Greek *hēlios*

## Definition

The bright round one that [kwel](kwel.md)-s across [dyews](dyews.md)
each day — climbs [h2en](h2en.md) from the side where
[h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md) opens, stands overhead at the high point,
and sinks [upo](upo.md) the far edge to let [nokwts](nokwts.md)
begin. `soh2wl` is the source of the [leuk](leuk.md) by which
[weid](weid.md) works: when it stands high, every shape is sharp;
when it goes low, shadows stretch long across [dheghom](dheghom.md)
and the eye loses its grip. It gives the [gwher](gwher.md) that
warms the skin and the ground, and when the cold months come it
rides lower and weaker. One body that returns each day the same
way — the first thing the speaker counts the turning of
[dyews](dyews.md) by.
