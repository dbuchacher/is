# peh3

category: 1h

PIE: *peh₃(i)-
Gloss: to drink
Descendants: potable, Latin *potus*, Greek *pinein*

## Definition

To take [wodr](wodr.md) past the lips and down the throat — the
liquid stroke that does for thirst what [h1ed](h1ed.md) does for
hunger. [h1eg](h1eg.md) cups [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) at a spring, or
tips a skin-bag, and the water crosses the mouth-edge without
chewing: [h1dont](h1dont.md) plays no role, [dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md)
guides the flow only. Without `peh3` the body dries (as
[ter-s](ter-s.md) names) and [mer](mer.md)-s faster than it would
without [h1ed](h1ed.md). Sibling to [h1ed](h1ed.md) in the
eat-drink pair; cousin of [peh2](peh2.md) (give-drink as part of
feed-and-protect).
