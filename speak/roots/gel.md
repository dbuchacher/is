# gel

category: 1e

PIE: *gel-
Gloss: cold
Descendants: cold, gel, Latin *gelu*

## Definition

Cold as the body shrinks from it — the bite on fingers and face
in [ghyem](ghyem.md), the stiffening that comes into a body left
out in [nokwts](nokwts.md) without fire, the freeze that turns
[wodr](wodr.md) to [snoygwh](snoygwh.md) and hard ice. `gel` is
what the skin meets on the far side from [gwher](gwher.md): the
body pulls inward, the hand loses grip, the breath shows as a
pale cloud when it goes out. The speaker feels it first at the
nose and fingertips — the parts furthest from the heart — and
knows to come close to [peh2wr](peh2wr.md) or to press against
another body. Strong enough `gel` freezes standing
[h2ekweh2](h2ekweh2.md), hardens [dheghom](dheghom.md) so the
spade fails, kills the unprotected body through a long
[nokwts](nokwts.md). Paired against [gwher](gwher.md) as the
shrink-pole of the skin-reading.
