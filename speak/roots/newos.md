# newos

category: 1m

PIE: *newos
Gloss: new
Descendants: new, Latin *novus*, Greek *neos*

## Definition

Just-come-into-being — what has [bhuH](bhuH.md)-ed only a short
while ago and still carries the freshness of its entry. `newos`
names the moon in its first thin sliver after dark
([meh1ns](meh1ns.md)-returning), the leaf ([leyp](leyp.md)) just
uncurled in [wesr](wesr.md), the child at the breast of the
[meh2ter](meh2ter.md), the track pressed in [snoygwh](snoygwh.md)
this morning. The `newos` thing has not yet been long in
[h1es](h1es.md)-ing — it shows no wear, no weathering, no
thinning from use. It is the entry-edge of the age-line, where
something first stands before [weid](weid.md) as itself.
