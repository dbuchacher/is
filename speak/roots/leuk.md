# leuk

category: 1e

PIE: *leuk-
Gloss: light / bright
Descendants: light, lunar, Latin *lux*, Greek *leukos*

## Definition

Light itself — what [soh2wl](soh2wl.md) pours onto the world, what
[peh2wr](peh2wr.md) throws from the hearth, what the eye needs to
[weid](weid.md) anything at all. `leuk` is the quality that
[h1es](h1es.md) when things are seen and fails when
[h1regwos](h1regwos.md) takes over. To [weid](weid.md) requires
`leuk`: no light, no sight. The root pairs with seeing at the
ground level — light is not just a bright-thing floating
separately, it is what sight depends on. The speaker names the
quality where it [h1es](h1es.md): the brightness of a
[meh1ns](meh1ns.md)-lit snowfield, the glint off moving
[h2ekweh2](h2ekweh2.md), the pale glow of a coal in the dark that
guides the hand to it. Kin to [bhel](bhel.md) (white-shining) —
both name the bright, but `leuk` is the active giving-of-sight
while `bhel` is the quality of a bright surface that holds the
light given.
