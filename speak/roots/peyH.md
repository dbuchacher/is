# peyH

category: 1m

PIE: *peyH-
Gloss: fat / thick
Descendants: Greek *piōn* (via)

## Definition

Thick-with-fat — the quality of a beast whose flesh carries a
deep layer of fat under the skin, whose belly hangs heavy, whose
meat drips rich onto the [peh2wr](peh2wr.md). `peyH` names what
the hunter hopes for: the [h2rtkos](h2rtkos.md) fattened on
summer berries before [ghyem](ghyem.md), the deer that has eaten
well, the fish ([peysk](peysk.md)) full-bodied in the run.
Where the thin-ribbed kill gives little, the `peyH` kill feeds
the camp long. From the beast the word carries to any thick-full
body: the heavy cream on settled milk, the ripe udder, the rich
soil. A `peyH` thing is opposite of [h1lengwh](h1lengwh.md)
(light-bodied) — it has heft, it sinks in the hand. The quality
that [gusH](gusH.md) reads as rich on the tongue.
