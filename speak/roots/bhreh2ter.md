# bhreh2ter

category: 1c

PIE: *bʰréh₂tēr
Gloss: brother
Descendants: brother, Latin *frater*, Greek *phrater*

## Definition

The other [h2wiH-ro](h2wiH-ro.md) whom the same [meh2ter](meh2ter.md)
bore, who stood through the same childhood under the same
[ph2ter](ph2ter.md) — the male kin of one's own
[genh1](genh1.md) line, same generation. [h1eg](h1eg.md) and
`bhreh2ter` point together at the same hearth when naming their
parents: this is the holding that makes them brothers. Later
tongues widen the word to a man of the same band or fellowship,
but the root's ground is the shared begetting.
