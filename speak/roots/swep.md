# swep

category: 1h

PIE: *swep-
Gloss: to sleep
Descendants: sleep?, Latin *sopor* (soporific), Greek *hupnos* (hypnosis)

## Definition

The body [legh](legh.md)-s down at [nokwts](nokwts.md) and the eyes
([h3okw](h3okw.md)) shut; breathing ([h2enh1](h2enh1.md)) slows;
[h1eg](h1eg.md) stops hearing what [h2ews-b](h2ews-b.md) would hear
awake; the inside goes dark while the body stays warm and
[gweyh3](gweyh3.md)-s on. To `swep` is to cross from the waking hold
of [h1es](h1es.md) into a [wes](wes.md)-ing that lasts until
[h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md) — the mind unplugged from the world-touch while
the heart still beats. Unlike [mer](mer.md), the body returns from
this crossing: [swep] is reversible, [mer] is not. Sister to
[legh](legh.md) (the body-posture that makes room for `swep`) and
[key](key.md) (lying-at-rest more generally).
