# krep

category: 1d

PIE: *krep- / *kʷrep-
Gloss: body
Descendants: corpse, corporal, Latin *corpus*

## Definition

The whole standing shape of [h1eg](h1eg.md) from crown to
[upo](upo.md)-sole, pointed at when a speaker sweeps hand along
self and says "this." `krep` names the single held form that
[h1es](h1es.md) on [h2en](h2en.md) two feet and moves as one
when it [h1ey](h1ey.md)-s or [gwem](gwem.md)-s — all the parts
at once, bound [sem](sem.md) into one carrier. It is what
[wes](wes.md) warm while the speaker lives and goes cold when
[gweyh3](gweyh3.md) leaves. Every other body-part root points
at a place on this whole; `krep` is the whole itself. Paired
against what lies [h2epo](h2epo.md) the skin: the `krep` is
the inside-carrier, the world outside is what it walks through.
