# h1rewdh

category: 1m

PIE: *h₁rewdʰ-
Gloss: red
Descendants: red, rust, rouge, Latin *rufus*, Greek *eruthros*

## Definition

The red the eye reads in [h1esh2r](h1esh2r.md) spilling from a
fresh kill and in the heart of [peh2wr](peh2wr.md) after the
flames have burned low — the two giving-grounds of the word.
`h1rewdh` is what the hunter sees on his hands after butchering,
the glow deep in the coal-bed at the center of the hearth, the
streak across the sky at [h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md) and at the
sinking of [soh2wl](soh2wl.md). A berry ripe enough to eat is
`h1rewdh`; a wound is `h1rewdh`; the cheeks of the child who has
run hard in the cold are `h1rewdh`. The speaker knows this
quality before knowing the word: it is the color that means
either life pouring out or heat pouring out, and the two are
named together.
