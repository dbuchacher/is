# h2ews-b

**seed** | category: 1j

PIE: *h₂ews-
Gloss: to listen / hear
Descendants: audio, Latin *audire*, Greek *aiō*

## Definition

To take in through the open ear what [wekw](wekw.md) has put out
into the air, and carry it inward so that [men](men.md) holds
the sound after — the ear-reading that answers the
mouth-writing. Where [weid](weid.md) catches light from
[h1e](h1e.md), this one catches breath-shaped sound from the
speaker at [ke](ke.md) or [h1e](h1e.md) and closes the loop
between two mouths.
