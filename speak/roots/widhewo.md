# widhewo

category: 1c

PIE: *widʰéwo-
Gloss: widow
Descendants: widow, Latin *vidua*

## Definition

The [gwen](gwen.md) whose [h2wiH-ro](h2wiH-ro.md) has gone the
way of [mer](mer.md) — the woman left standing alone at the
hearth after the pairing was torn by death. Where [gwen](gwen.md)
and [h2wiH-ro](h2wiH-ro.md) stood as [sem](sem.md), now
[dwis](dwis.md) has cut the bond and only she holds the fire.
The root names the emptied side of the pair, the one whose mate
no longer [h1es](h1es.md)-es. The word carries the loss inside
it: widow is the being-without after the being-with.
