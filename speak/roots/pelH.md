# pelH

category: 1g

PIE: *pelH- / *pol(H)-
Gloss: flat open space
Descendants: field, floor

## Definition

The stretch of [dheghom](dheghom.md) where nothing stands — no
[deru](deru.md), no rock-pile, no camp. [h1eg](h1eg.md) looks
across a `pelH` and sees only ground, all the way out to where
the sky comes down. Neither [h2en](h2en.md) (the upward) nor
[upo](upo.md) (the downward) rises or falls on it: it holds as
[sem](sem.md), one continuous level, and the eye runs across it
without stopping. Where the forest breaks and the grass begins,
where the river's flood leaves behind a wide flat that herds
walk, where the cave-mouth opens onto ground a hunter can see a
running beast crossing from far off — that ground is `pelH`.
Named by what it is not: not up-tilted, not down-cut, not filled
with standing things. A pure openness reached by negation of the
vertical axis.
