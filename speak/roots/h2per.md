# h2per

category: 1n

PIE: *h₂per-
Gloss: before / through / beyond
Descendants: para-, per-, for-, fore-, pre-, Latin *prae*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing along the line of [per](per.md) and then past
its far end — the going-through held onward until the walker of
[h1ey](h1ey.md) clears the far side and keeps going. Where
[per](per.md) names the crossing itself, [h2per](h2per.md) names
the side that lies beyond, the fronting-out stretch that stands
past [h1en](h1en.md) and past [h1eghs](h1eghs.md) on the far
shore of the cross.
