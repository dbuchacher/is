# yu

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *yu- / *usmé-
Gloss: "you" (2pl)
Descendants: you, Latin *vos*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at other — the speaker sweeps outward across
multiple addressed listeners at once. No reduction; the outward
sweep across many listeners is the meaning.
