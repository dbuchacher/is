# h1ekwos

category: 1f

PIE: *h₁éḱwos
Gloss: horse
Descendants: equine, Latin *equus*, Greek *hippos*

## Definition

The [ghwer](ghwer.md) whose [h1ey](h1ey.md) is swiftest — the
long-legged grazer of the open grass that runs as wind
([h2weh1](h2weh1.md)) runs, faster than any [h1eg](h1eg.md) can
[sekw](sekw.md). Named from the swift going: the one whose four
[pods](pods.md) strike the ground so fast the strikes blur into
one sound. Lives in a herd that shifts as [sem](sem.md) across
open ground, turning together and fleeing together when a
big-toothed hunter appears. Before the later binding under
harness, `h1ekwos` named only this wild runner — the beast
glimpsed at the edge of the grass, already going.
