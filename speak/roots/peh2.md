# peh2

category: 1l

PIE: *peh₂-
Gloss: to protect / feed
Descendants: pastor, food, pan-, Latin *pascere*

## Definition

To stand over another and see that they eat and are not taken —
the parental-protective stroke that holds both feeding and
shielding as one act. `peh2` is what the [ph2ter](ph2ter.md) does
by name (the father IS the peh2-er), what the
[meh2ter](meh2ter.md) does at the breast, what the herder does
with the flock — bringing food, fending off the
[wlkwos](wlkwos.md), keeping the young in the bounded
[h1en](h1en.md) of the camp through the night. The root does not
split feed from guard because in PIE life they are one stance:
the one who feeds the child IS the one who keeps the
[ghwer](ghwer.md) off. Latin *pascere* keeps both —
the shepherd pastures and protects in one verb. `peh2` anchors
much of the kinship batch: to `peh2` is the work of being kin at
all.
