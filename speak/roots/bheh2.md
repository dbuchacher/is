# bheh2

category: 1k

PIE: *bʰeh₂-
Gloss: to speak / shine
Descendants: fable, fame, preface, Latin *fari*, Greek *phēmi*

## Definition

To speak IS to shine-forth-words in PIE: the mouth opens and the
breath-shaped sound goes out like [leuk](leuk.md) leaving
[peh2wr](peh2wr.md), carrying the shape of what [men](men.md)
holds into the air where other ears catch it. `bheh2` is the
shining-kind of speech — the telling that makes the tale stand
bright enough for the whole tribe to see it, the naming that
lifts a thing out of the dim. From this root comes "fate" — what
was spoken-out-loud-and-now-stands, distinct from
[dheh1](dheh1.md)'s "fiat" which is what-was-done-and-now-stands.
Latin *infans* names the child who has not yet `bheh2`-ed:
one still inside whom the shining-out has not broken.
