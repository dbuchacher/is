# welh1

category: 1l

PIE: *welh₁-
Gloss: to wish / will
Descendants: will, voluntary, Latin *velle*, Greek *elpo*

## Definition

To bend [men](men.md) toward one thing and not another — the
inward leaning that comes before the hand moves. `welh1` is the
wanting-and-choosing: the hunter who [derk](derk.md)-s two deer
and settles on one, the child who reaches for this berry past
that berry, the tribe that turns its face toward where they will
walk next. Where [gusH](gusH.md) chooses at the tongue and
[dheugh](dheugh.md) settles in the [men](men.md) on what is
good, `welh1` is the forward-leaning stance itself — the weight
of the whole self tilted toward one side before [dheh1](dheh1.md)
carries it out. Without `welh1` no act starts; with it, the body
is already half-gone toward the chosen.
