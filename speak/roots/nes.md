# nes

category: 1i

PIE: *nes-
Gloss: to return safely
Descendants: nostalgia, Greek *nostos*

## Definition

To come back to the camp alive — the hunter who walked out at
[h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md) reaches home again before [nokwts](nokwts.md),
and the walk-arc closes. Where [gwem](gwem.md) names bare
coming-toward, `nes` names the close-of-loop after an
[h1ey](h1ey.md) going-out: the round-trip completing with the
body still [gweyh3](gweyh3.md)-ing. This is the loop-closure of
the walk: [h1ey](h1ey.md) out, [gwem](gwem.md) back, and the
third stroke `nes` marks that the returned one is whole —
unwounded, unhungry, un-[mer](mer.md). Paired with
[kwel](kwel.md) (which also closes a loop, but by turning); the
descendants give Greek *nostos* (the homecoming) and nostalgia
(the ache for that homecoming when it is not possible).
