# kwrewh2

category: 1d

PIE: *kʷrewh₂-
Gloss: blood / raw flesh
Descendants: crude, Latin *cruor*, Greek *kreas*

## Definition

Blood seen the other way: not the hidden inner-flow that
[h1esh2r](h1esh2r.md) names, but the blood-and-flesh that
spills out and becomes meat — the wet red stuff of the kill.
Where [h1esh2r](h1esh2r.md) is blood inside the living body,
`kwrewh2` is blood outside the dead one: the hunter's hand
after the spear-work, the cut of raw meat before it meets
[peh2wr](peh2wr.md) or [h1ngwnis](h1ngwnis.md). The root fuses
blood and flesh because in the moment of killing and butchering
they come as one substance, and only later does cooked food
stop being `kwrewh2`. Same inside-body as
[h1esh2r](h1esh2r.md), named from the outside-view.
