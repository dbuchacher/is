# h2el

category: 1h

PIE: *h₂el-
Gloss: to grow / nourish
Descendants: **old**, **adult**, **alumnus**, Latin *alere*

## Definition

The feeding-that-makes-grow — what a mother does with milk to
the child, what a wet-nurse does to the fosterling, what the
clan does to the young hunter with shares of meat. Where the
other grow-roots name pushing-out from within and piling-on from
without, `h2el` names the feeding-across-time that carries a
body from young ([h2yuh1en](h2yuh1en.md)) through full-grown
(adult) into old ([senos](senos.md)) age. The root holds both
ends of the growth-arc: the nourishing and the old-one that
nourishing produced. A `h2el`-ed body is an alumnus — one fed by
the group — and eventually the elder. The feed-to-full-age shade
of the three-way growth polysemy.
