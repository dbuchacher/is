# bhruH

category: 1d

PIE: *bʰruH-
Gloss: brow
Descendants: brow, Greek *ophrus*

## Definition

The hair-ridge [h2en](h2en.md) the eye — the shelf of
[kap-ut](kap-ut.md) that sits over [h3okw](h3okw.md) and
guards the wet opening from sweat and glare. `bhruH` names
this small arched line that a speaker raises when surprised
and draws together when the mind works hard: the brow is
what moves to show what [men](men.md) holds, the earliest
face-signal before [wekw](wekw.md) puts it into sound. Points
at the place where the hard-ridge of [ker-a](ker-a.md) meets
the soft eye-opening — the border where skull turns to face.
