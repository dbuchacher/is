# deh3

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *deh₃-
Gloss: to give
Descendants: data, donate, dose, Latin *dare*, Greek *didōmi*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at a thing in one [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) passing into
another [mr-gh](mr-gh.md): [h1eg](h1eg.md) holds the meat, [tu](tu.md)
opens a palm, the meat crosses. Where [dheh1](dheh1.md) puts a thing
onto a spot, `deh3` puts a thing onto a receiving hand — the target
is a person, not a resting base. The thing leaves the giver's hold
and [h1es](h1es.md) in the taker's hold; after the passing, the
giver no longer has it. The root marks the simplest social move: one
body releases, another body receives, and the item survives the
crossing. Paired with [bher](bher.md) (the carrying that brings the
thing close enough to give).
