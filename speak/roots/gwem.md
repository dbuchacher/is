# gwem

**seed** | category: 1i

PIE: *gʷem-
Gloss: to come / step
Descendants: come, become, Latin *venire*, Greek *bainō*

## Definition

To step inward, closing the gap between some far spot and where
the speaker stands — each pace brings the walking body from
[h1e](h1e.md) toward [ke](ke.md), from [h2epo](h2epo.md) back
into [h1en](h1en.md). The foot-after-foot of [h1ey](h1ey.md)
reversed: the arrow points at self.
