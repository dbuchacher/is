# nu

category: 3f

PIE: *nu-
Gloss: now
Descendants: now, Latin *nunc*, Greek *nun*

## Definition

The time-side pointer at what [ke](ke.md) is on the space-side —
this-moment-here, the still-middle of time as [h1es](h1es.md) is
the still-middle of being. When [h1eg](h1eg.md) [wekw](wekw.md)-s
"the fire [h1es]-es," the "now" of the saying is `nu`: the moment
the word is spoken, the moment pointed at. Where [ke](ke.md) points
at the spot the speaker stands, `nu` points at the time the speaker
stands in. The root is small because its work is small — one
stroke at one time, at-this-moment. Gives English *now*, Latin
*nunc*, Greek *nun*, Sanskrit *nu* — all keeping the same pointing
with almost no shift. A deictic of time, kin to the deictic seeds
[ke](ke.md) and [h1e](h1e.md) but thrown along the time-axis
instead of the space-axis — the hub-point on the time-line, with
"before" falling one way and "after" the other.
