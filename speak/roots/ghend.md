# ghend

category: 1h

PIE: *gʰend-
Gloss: to grab / take
Descendants: get, forget, pregnant, prison, Latin *prehendere*

## Definition

The sharp snap of [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) onto a thing — the grab that
happens fast, the take where [h1eg](h1eg.md) reaches and the
thing is caught before it moves. Where [ghabh](ghabh.md) names
the keeping-after the grasp, `ghend` names the catching-moment:
the snatch that closes on prey, on a child about to fall, on
the root pulled out of [dheghom](dheghom.md). The womb `ghend`-s
a seed and holds it (pregnant); the walls `ghend` a body and
will not let go (prison); the mind `ghend`-s a thought or lets
it slip (forget). Snatch-shade sibling to the keep-through-time
head of [ghabh](ghabh.md).
