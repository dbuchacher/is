# gusH

category: 1j

PIE: *ǵ(e)usH-
Gloss: to taste / choose
Descendants: choose, gusto, Latin *gustare*, Greek *geuō*

## Definition

To take food onto the [dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md) and read what it is
before [h1ed](h1ed.md) sends it down — and in the same stroke to
pick this morsel over that one. Tasting IS choosing in PIE: the
mouth decides whether to swallow or spit, and the choosing hand
that later picks among many things grows out of the choosing
tongue that first picked among foods. What [h3ed](h3ed.md)
caught at the nose, `gusH` now holds in the mouth; the root
folds the perceiving and the picking into one act, because for
the body there is no gap between knowing-the-taste and
taking-it-in-or-refusing.
