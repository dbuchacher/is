# h2en

**seed** | category: 1n

PIE: *h₂en-
Gloss: up / on
Descendants: on, an-, Greek *ana-*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the upper side of any here — the speaker
tilts the pointing from [ke](ke.md) toward the sky-side, away from
the ground, the side that is not [upo](upo.md).

## Groups

- [up-down-pair](up-down-pair.md) — the upper leg, paired against [upo](upo.md)
- [vertical-axis](vertical-axis.md) — the sky-ward pole of the vertical split
