# spek

category: 1j

PIE: *spek̂-
Gloss: to observe
Descendants: spy, spectacle, Latin *specere*, Greek *skopos* (by metathesis: scope, skeptic)

## Definition

To sweep the [h3okw](h3okw.md) across a field and scan for what
stands out — the watching stance, the eye held open long across a
stretch of [dheghom](dheghom.md) or sky. Where [weid](weid.md)
catches the light from one [h1e](h1e.md) and [derk](derk.md) cuts
that light into hard shape, `spek` moves the gaze looking for
what might be there: the scout on the ridge, the hunter reading
tracks, the one who [seh2g](seh2g.md)-s with the eye before
setting foot. What the scanning picks up, [men](men.md) then
holds and weighs.
