# h1lewdh

category: 1l

PIE: *h₁lewdʰ-
Gloss: to grow / free
Descendants: liberty, Latin *liber*, Greek *eleutheros*

## Definition

To grow up into one's own standing — the young one coming into
full height, no longer held in hand, walking where the self
leans. `h1lewdh` binds growing and being-free because in PIE
life they are one arc: the child who [h2el](h2el.md)-s into
adulthood `h1lewdh`-s out of the parent's grip. The grown one is
the free one; the free one is the one who has grown. Greek
*eleutheros* names the full-grown tribesman — free because
grown, grown because free. Kin to [preyH](preyH.md)'s free-
beloved strand but taken from the other side: there the
freedom is love-given, here the freedom is grown-into.
