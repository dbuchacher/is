# leubh

category: 1l

PIE: *leubʰ-
Gloss: to love / care
Descendants: love, believe, leave (permit), Latin *lubet*

## Definition

To hold another in [kred](kred.md) with the leaning of
[welh1](welh1.md) — the caring-love that wants the other to
fare well and bends the self toward making it so. `leubh` is the
love that wills: the [meh2ter](meh2ter.md) who will go hungry so
the child eats, the one who trusts enough to let the other walk
free. "Believe" springs from here because to believe is to love
what one trusts — to lean one's [men](men.md) onto the word of
another as firmly as onto one's own. "Leave" (= permit) too: to
let be, to give over the choice, is a shape of this same caring.
`leubh` is the holding-dear pole of love — the love that wills
the other's good — set against the pleasing-love that joins two
as friends at the other pole.
