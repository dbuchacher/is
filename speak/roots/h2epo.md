# h2epo

**seed** | category: 1n

PIE: *h₂epó- / *apó
Gloss: away from / off
Descendants: off, of, apo-, ab-, Latin *ab*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the outward leaving of one spot — starts at
a here ([ke](ke.md)) and tracks the path that peels away from it
toward a there ([h1e](h1e.md)). The departing leg of the here-there
axis.

## Groups

- [inside-outside-pair](inside-outside-pair.md) — the outward leg, paired against [h1en](h1en.md)
- [here-there-pair](here-there-pair.md) — the peeling-away motion between the two poles
