# tu

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *tu-
Gloss: "you" (2sg)
Descendants: thou, Latin *tu*, German *du*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at other — the speaker points outward at a
single addressed listener. No reduction; the outward gesture toward
one listener is the meaning.
