# suHnus

category: 1c

PIE: *suHnús
Gloss: son
Descendants: son, Sanskrit *sūnu*

## Definition

The [h2wiH-ro](h2wiH-ro.md)-to-be that [ph2ter](ph2ter.md) and
[meh2ter](meh2ter.md) brought forth through [genh1](genh1.md) —
the young male of the down-generation, the one the parents
[peh2](peh2.md) and [bher](bher.md) until he can stand on his
own. The root holds the sense of what is begotten and still
growing: not yet the full [h2wiH-ro](h2wiH-ro.md) but on the
line to becoming one. Stands as the son-pole of the two kinds of
young kin the parent-pair bring forth.
