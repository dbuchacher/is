# solh2

category: 1m

PIE: *solh₂-
Gloss: whole
Descendants: solid, safe, holistic, Latin *sollus*, Greek *holos*

## Definition

Un-split — what stands before the eye with none of it missing,
none of it broken off, none of it [dwis](dwis.md)-ed. `solh2`
names the [h2ost](h2ost.md) that has not cracked, the pot that
has not fallen, the [krep](krep.md) of the child returned from
the woods with no wound. The `solh2` thing is one through and
through: no crack running its length, no piece gone from its
edge, the whole of it still [h1es](h1es.md)-ing together. It is
the opposite-pole of [dwis](dwis.md) (cut in two): where
[dwis](dwis.md) names the stroke that splits, `solh2` names the
rest that has not been struck. A beast brought back whole is
`solh2`; a family that still [steh2](steh2.md)-s together after
the hard [ghyem](ghyem.md) is `solh2`. From here the later
senses of safe and sound and sacred-whole.
