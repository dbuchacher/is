# steigh

category: 1i

PIE: *steigʰ-
Gloss: to walk / climb
Descendants: stile, stair, sty, Greek *steichō*

## Definition

To walk in a way that rises — the body of [h1eg](h1eg.md) going
uphill, step after step lifting against [upo](upo.md) toward
[h2en](h2en.md), each [pods](pods.md) higher than the last. Where
[h1ey](h1ey.md) is flat-ground walking, `steigh` is the climbing-
gait: the knees ([genu](genu.md)) bend deeper, the lungs work
harder, the path gains height. The climb-stroke becomes the
notched log, the stepped ledge, the stair — any made thing that
lets feet rise in regular paces. Descendants stile and stair
both keep the upward-stepping shade.
