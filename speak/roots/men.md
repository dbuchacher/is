# men

**seed** | category: 1j

PIE: *men-
Gloss: to think
Descendants: mind, mental, mention, Latin *mens*, Greek *menos*, Sanskrit *manas*

## Definition

To hold inside what [weid](weid.md) has seen or what
[h2ews-b](h2ews-b.md) has heard, turning it over at [ke](ke.md)
without needing the outside spot to still be there — the inward
[kwel](kwel.md) of what was taken in, kept alive past the
catching. When [gneh3](gneh3.md) closes its loop, it is
[men](men.md) doing the turning at the middle of the stroke.
