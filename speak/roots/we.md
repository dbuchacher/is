# we

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *we- / *n̥s-
Gloss: "we / us"
Descendants: we, us, Latin *nos*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at self plus others — the speaker points inward
at self and sweeps outward to gather further speakers or listeners
into one group. No reduction; the sweep is the meaning.
