# h3rneh

category: 1f

PIE: *h₃r̥neh- / *h₁ór-dh-
Gloss: eagle / bird of prey
Descendants: eagle, erne

## Definition

The winged [ghwer](ghwer.md) that hunts other beasts — the great
bird with hooked tooth ([h1dont](h1dont.md)-like beak) and
killing claws, circling high over the open ground until it sees
a small body stir, then [gwem](gwem.md)-ing straight down faster
than a thrown stone. `h3rneh` names the wing-killer that
[h1eg](h1eg.md) watches from below with the same eye
([h3okw](h3okw.md)) it turns on the pack-runner: another hunter,
working the same prey from above. Its feather is a token; its
cry from height marks that a beast has been sighted. Kin by
killing to the big-toothed hunters on the ground, kin by wing to
the seed-eating fliers.
