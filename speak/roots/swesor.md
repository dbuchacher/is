# swesor

category: 1c

PIE: *swésor
Gloss: sister
Descendants: sister, Latin *soror*, Russian *sestra*

## Definition

The [gwen](gwen.md) of one's own [genh1](genh1.md) line, same
generation — the [se](se.md)-side woman, the woman-of-our-own
(the root carries *swe-* self-ness inside it). Where
[bhreh2ter](bhreh2ter.md) names the man of one's shared
begetting, `swesor` names the woman of it: she stood through the
same childhood, shares the same [meh2ter](meh2ter.md) and
[ph2ter](ph2ter.md), points at the same hearth when naming kin.
The word marks her as self-side rather than outside-the-band.
