# steh2

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *steh₂-
Gloss: to stand
Descendants: stand, state, stay, static, Latin *stare*, Greek *histēmi*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at a body held upright on [pods](pods.md), long-axis
aligned with [h2en](h2en.md) (up) against [upo](upo.md) (down). The
root names the posture where weight passes straight down through the
legs into [dheghom](dheghom.md), so the body stops going and
[wes](wes.md)-es in one spot. A tree `steh2`-s; [h1eg](h1eg.md)
standing at the fire `steh2`-s; a post driven into the ground
`steh2`-s. Sister posture to [sed](sed.md) (folded onto haunches) and
[legh](legh.md) (long-axis laid along the ground); distinguished from
them by the upright stacking of the body along the up-down axis.
