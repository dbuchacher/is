# telh2

category: 1h

PIE: *telh₂-
Gloss: to lift / bear
Descendants: toll, Latin *tollere*

## Definition

To raise a thing off [dheghom](dheghom.md) against [upo](upo.md)
up into the hold of [mr-gh](mr-gh.md) — the upward stroke that
must happen before [bher](bher.md) can carry it across, and
before [ghabh](ghabh.md) can keep it. Where [bher](bher.md) names
the carrying-through-space and [ghabh](ghabh.md) names the
keeping, `telh2` names the bare lifting: the pull against weight
that gets the thing up into the holding-zone. A hunter `telh2`-s
a stone; a gatherer `telh2`-s a basket; a father `telh2`-s a
child. The raising-edge of the grasp-cluster headed by
[ghabh](ghabh.md).
