# h1eg

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *h₁eǵ- / *h₁eǵoh₂
Gloss: "I" (1sg)
Descendants: I, Latin *ego*, Greek *egō*, Sanskrit *aham*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at self — the one who is now speaking points
inward at the speaker-pole of the utterance. No reduction through
other roots; the gesture is the meaning.
