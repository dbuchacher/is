# bhegw

category: 1i

PIE: *bʰegʷ-
Gloss: to flee / run
Descendants: fugitive, Latin *fugere*, Greek *pheugō*

## Definition

To run fast with fear driving — the urgent flee-stroke where
[pods](pods.md) hit [dheghom](dheghom.md) as hard and quick as
the body can manage, the breath of [h2enh1](h2enh1.md) sharp,
escape the only aim. Where [bhugh](bhugh.md) holds the bend-and-
flee together (the body curved by fear), `bhegw` holds the
speed-of-the-flee: the sprint itself, unbent but racing. The two
flee-roots share the fear-driven-motion shade and split on which
half of the body's behavior gets named — the curve or the pace.
Sibling-polysemy with [bhugh](bhugh.md); descendants fugitive and
*pheugō* both keep the sprint-shade.
