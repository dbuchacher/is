# seh2g

category: 1i

PIE: *seh₂g-
Gloss: to seek / track
Descendants: seek, sage, Latin *sagire*

## Definition

To walk looking for — [h3okw](h3okw.md) reads the ground for
broken twig and hoof-print while [pods](pods.md) follow what the
eyes show, and the body goes where the trail leads. Where
[sekw](sekw.md) names the walking-behind an already-seen body,
`seh2g` names the walking-while-searching: the track may be
hours old, the prey out of sight, but the trail carries the
hunter onward. The tracker becomes the sage because following-
a-trail sharpens reading; descendants give both "seek" (the act)
and "sage" (the one who reads well enough to find). Sibling
with [sekw](sekw.md) in the follow-verbs group; uses the
read-force of [weid](weid.md) through the feet.
