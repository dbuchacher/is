# pro

category: 3f

PIE: *pro-
Gloss: before / forward
Descendants: pro-, for-, Latin *pro*, Greek *pro*

## Definition

The time-side "before" and the space-side "forward" held in one
small stroke — what lies ahead of where the body stands, what
lies behind of where time stands. Kin to [per](per.md) (the
through-forward seed) but shaded differently: where [per](per.md)
is the push-all-the-way-through, `pro` is the bare pointing-
forward-of — in front of this spot, earlier than this moment. On
the space-axis `pro` names what is ahead in [h1ey](h1ey.md)'s
drawn line; on the time-axis it names what has already been
[wes](wes.md)-ed through, what stands earlier than [nu](nu.md) in
the order of things done. Latin *pro* keeps both ("in front of,"
"on behalf of," "before"); Greek *pro* the same. Gives English
prefixes *pro-* and *for-* ("before, in advance, on behalf of").
The "earlier" pole of the time-line with [nu](nu.md) at its hub.
