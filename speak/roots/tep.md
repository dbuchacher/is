# tep

category: 1e

PIE: *tep-
Gloss: warm (mildly)
Descendants: tepid, Latin *tepere*

## Definition

Mildly warm — the lukewarm, the just-warm, the barely-above-cold.
Where [gwher](gwher.md) is the welcome-warm the body moves toward
and [kerh2](kerh2.md) is the burning-warm that forces change,
`tep` is the in-between: warm as [h2ekweh2](h2ekweh2.md) that has
sat by the fire for a while, warm as the body of a beast an hour
dead, warm as a stone the [soh2wl](soh2wl.md) has touched through
a weak [megh](megh.md). The body reads it as neither-cold-nor-
hot — the touch does not pull the hand away, does not make the
skin seek more. It is the idle middle of the temperature-axis,
where neither [gel](gel.md) nor [gwher](gwher.md) demands
anything. The speaker names it because not every warm is the
welcoming kind; some warm is just the absence of cold.
