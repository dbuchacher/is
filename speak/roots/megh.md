# megh

category: 1e

PIE: *meǵʰ- / *meǵh₂-
Gloss: cloud / mist

## Definition

The gray gathering in [dyews](dyews.md) that cuts off the
[leuk](leuk.md) of [soh2wl](soh2wl.md) — cloud overhead and mist
close to the ground, one root for both because they are the same
wet dimming met high or low. `megh` is the thickening of air
itself: what [h2weh1](h2weh1.md) pushes across the sky, what
hangs in the valley at [h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md) so the eye cannot
see the next [deru](deru.md) over, what piles up dark before rain
or [snoygwh](snoygwh.md) falls. [wodr](wodr.md) carried aloft,
turned pale and heavy, waiting to drop. When it sits on
[dheghom](dheghom.md) the world goes small — [weid](weid.md)
fails past a few paces, the hunter loses the trail, the track of
the beast dissolves. When it gathers above, the day is muted, the
shadows flatten, and soon water comes down.
