# h1es

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *h₁es-
Gloss: to be
Descendants: **is**, **am**, **are**, **Latin *est/sum***, Greek *esti*, Sanskrit *asti*, German *ist*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at a thing-here-now and saying: this-holds. The root
names the bare holding of being while [h1eg](h1eg.md) looks. It does
not name coming-into and it does not name staying-through — it names
the flat present that [ke](ke.md) marks as here. When a speaker points
at the fire and says "fire [h1es]," the root carries the still middle:
the fire holds, [h1eg](h1eg.md) sees it hold, nothing more. It sits
between [bhuH](bhuH.md) (the upward coming-into-being) and
[wes](wes.md) (the downward staying-put).
