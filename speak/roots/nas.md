# nas

category: 1d

PIE: *nas-
Gloss: nose
Descendants: nose, Latin *nasus*

## Definition

The breath-hole jutting from the middle of [kap-ut](kap-ut.md)
— the part of [krep](krep.md) through which air enters and
leaves while the mouth stays shut, and through which the
speaker takes in smells the hunter learns to read. `nas`
names the two-hole knob between [h3okw](h3okw.md) and
[dnghuh2](dnghuh2.md): above it the eyes look, below it the
tongue speaks, and itself it does the quiet inward-outward
work that [wes](wes.md) as long as [krep](krep.md) lives.
Points at where a mother presses her own against a
child's.
