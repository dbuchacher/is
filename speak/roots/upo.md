# upo

**seed** | category: 1n

PIE: *upo-
Gloss: under / below
Descendants: up, hypo-, sub-, Latin *sub*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the lower side of any here — the speaker
tilts the pointing from [ke](ke.md) toward the ground-side, away
from the sky, the side that is not [h2en](h2en.md).

## Groups

- [up-down-pair](up-down-pair.md) — the lower leg, paired against [h2en](h2en.md)
- [vertical-axis](vertical-axis.md) — the ground-ward pole of the vertical split
