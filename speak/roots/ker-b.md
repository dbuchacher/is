# ker-b

category: 1h

PIE: *ker-
Gloss: to grow
Descendants: create, Ceres, Latin *crescere*

## Definition

The pushing-out by which a young thing becomes a bigger thing —
what a sprout does rising through [dheghom](dheghom.md), what a
child does year by year, what a [deru](deru.md)-trunk does ring
by ring. Where [bhuH](bhuH.md) names the bare coming-into-being
at a start, `ker-b` names the carrying-on after: the steady
pushing-up that makes the new thing larger than it was. The same
stroke the farming-tongues later fastened onto grain (Ceres,
*crescere*) was already in the Paleolithic root for any
pushing-out of new substance.
