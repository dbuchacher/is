# bhuH

**seed** | category: 1h

PIE: *bʰuH-
Gloss: to become / be
Descendants: be, built, future, Latin *fui*, Greek *phuō*, Sanskrit *bhavati*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at a thing that was-not and is-now: a sprout pushing
up from [dheghom](dheghom.md), a fire kindling, a child coming out of
the womb. The root carries the upward crossing from not-yet into
[h1es](h1es.md): what [h1eg](h1eg.md) could not point at a moment
before is now pointable. Where [h1es](h1es.md) is the flat holding of
the now, `bhuH` is the edge where the holding begins — the upward trit
of being, paired against the downward staying of [wes](wes.md). The
sprout is `bhuH`-ing until it stops growing; after that it simply
[h1es](h1es.md).
