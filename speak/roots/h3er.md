# h3er

category: 1i

PIE: *h₃er-
Gloss: to move / rise
Descendants: orient, origin, Latin *oriri*, Greek *ornumi*

## Definition

To start moving upward from rest — the sun [soh2wl](soh2wl.md)
lifting out of the horizon at [h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md), the bird
[h2ewis](h2ewis.md) pushing off [dheghom](dheghom.md), the body
rising from [sed](sed.md) or [legh](legh.md) onto [pods](pods.md).
Where [gwem](gwem.md) names coming-toward and [h1ey](h1ey.md)
names going-away on the flat, `h3er` names the rising that starts
a motion: the upward push that sets a thing going. From this
start-stroke comes origin (the first rising, the place where
something first [bhuH](bhuH.md)-s up) and orient (the sun-rising
side, where [soh2wl](soh2wl.md) shows at [h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md),
keyed to reading the day).
