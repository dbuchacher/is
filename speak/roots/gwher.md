# gwher

category: 1e

PIE: *gʷʰer-
Gloss: warm / hot
Descendants: warm, Latin *formus*, Greek *thermos*

## Definition

Warm as the body welcomes it — the heat the speaker moves toward
on a cold day. `gwher` is the [peh2wr](peh2wr.md)'s gift to the
skin, the [soh2wl](soh2wl.md)'s gift to the back when it climbs
high, the warmth that stays in a cave with close bodies, the
warm of a fresh-killed beast when the hand reaches in for the
liver. The speaker feels it with skin before any thought — the
body reads warm-versus-cold at the surface, and `gwher` names the
welcome side of that reading. It is stronger than
[tep](tep.md) (which is mild-warm) and weaker than
[kerh2](kerh2.md) (which burns). Paired against [gel](gel.md) as
the pole the body approaches against the pole the body shrinks
from. In [ghyem](ghyem.md) `gwher` is scarce and precious; in
summer it is everywhere and the body turns instead toward
[h2ekweh2](h2ekweh2.md).
