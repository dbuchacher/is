# h2wiH-ro

category: 1c

PIE: *h₂wíH-ro-
Gloss: man / husband
Descendants: virile, Latin *vir*, Sanskrit *vīra*, werewolf (*wer-* = man)

## Definition

The adult male of the band — the one who stands full-grown, able
to [peh2](peh2.md) his own hearth and [bher](bher.md) his own
kill. The root carries the sense of life-force, the running body
that does the hunt and the fight, standing as the male-pole of
the adult pair. `h2wiH-ro` is the man plain — the vir of any
adult count, the male standing on his own feet ([pods](pods.md))
in full [bhuH](bhuH.md)-ed state, past the child-years and
before the way of [mer](mer.md).
