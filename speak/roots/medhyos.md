# medhyos

category: 1n

PIE: *médʰyos
Gloss: middle
Descendants: middle, mid, medium, Latin *medius*, Greek *messos*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the one [ke](ke.md) that sits the same
stride from each side — where [enteros](enteros.md) names the
whole caught stretch, [medhyos](medhyos.md) names the single spot
at its heart, the midpoint where [dwis](dwis.md) would split the
span in two equal halves. The speaker points not at a zone but at
a dead-center here: the spot picked out by [h2mbhi](h2mbhi.md)'s
surround shrunk to a single point.

## Groups

- [middle-zone](middle-zone.md) — the point, narrower than the [enteros](enteros.md) stretch
- [mid-point](mid-point.md) — the exact-center spot itself
