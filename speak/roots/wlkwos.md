# wlkwos

category: 1f

PIE: *wĺ̥kʷos
Gloss: wolf
Descendants: wolf, Latin *lupus*, Greek *lukos*

## Definition

The [ghwer](ghwer.md) that hunts in a pack and takes the big prey
— the one with long legs and long tooth ([h1dont](h1dont.md))
that [sekw](sekw.md)-s a herd through the cold, running with its
kin as if [sem](sem.md) bound them into one body. `wlkwos` names
the beast whose howl carries from [h2epo](h2epo.md) across the
night ([nokwts](nokwts.md)) and whose track the hunter learns to
read. Paired against [h1eg](h1eg.md) as rival hunter — the other
kind that eats what [h1eg](h1eg.md) would eat, sometimes taking a
child left unwatched. A killer that runs with its own `we`.
