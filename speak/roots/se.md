# se

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *sé- / *swe-
Gloss: reflexive "self"
Descendants: self, Latin *se*, German *sich*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at self — the speaker points back at whichever
pole is currently acting, bending the outward pointing back onto
its own source. No reduction; the bending-back is the meaning.

- [self-referential-pointers](self-referential-pointers.md) — the bend-back gesture itself
