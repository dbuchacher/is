# dheugh

category: 1j

PIE: *dheugh-
Gloss: to think / be useful
Descendants: think, thank, Latin *tongere*

## Definition

To turn something over in [men](men.md) and find it good — the
weighing that ends in yes. `dheugh` joins thinking and being-useful
because the thought that matters is the one that pays off: the
plan that brings the hunt home, the [med](med.md)-ing that lands
the flint-blow where it ought to land. Thanking springs from the
same stem: to thank another is to have turned over what they did
in [men](men.md) and found it good for you. Where [men](men.md)
is the bare holding-inside and [med](med.md) is the weighing
against a measure, `dheugh` is the settling — the thought that
closes with it-works.
