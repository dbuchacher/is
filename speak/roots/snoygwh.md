# snoygwh

category: 1e

PIE: *snóygʷʰ-
Gloss: snow
Descendants: snow, Latin *nix*, Greek *nipha*

## Definition

The cold white stuff that falls out of [megh](megh.md) in the
cold months — [wodr](wodr.md) turned hard and light, settling on
[dheghom](dheghom.md) in a soft layer that muffles every sound
the ear waits for. `snoygwh` is what comes down when
[ghyem](ghyem.md) has deepened: flakes drifting slow enough that
the eye can follow one, piling up on branches, filling every
hollow so the ground goes featureless. It holds the track of the
beast that crossed an hour ago; it takes the print of the foot
([pods](pods.md)) clean. It makes the camp cold — the
[gel](gel.md) that was in the air is now lying on the roof — and
forces the body to keep close to the [peh2wr](peh2wr.md).
[h2weh1](h2weh1.md) drives it sideways, piles it in drifts
against whatever [steh2](steh2.md)-s. Melts to [wodr](wodr.md)
again when [gwher](gwher.md) returns.
