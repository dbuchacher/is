# h2rtkos

category: 1f

PIE: *h₂ŕ̥tḱos
Gloss: bear
Descendants: arctic, Latin *ursus*, Greek *arktos*

## Definition

The great [ghwer](ghwer.md) that [legh](legh.md)-s through the
cold months — the beast that eats deep in the warm season and
then goes into its den and [legh](legh.md)-s like one dead until
warm air ([h2weh1](h2weh1.md)) returns. `h2rtkos` stands upright
when roused, the only beast that [steh2](steh2.md)-s on two
[pods](pods.md) like [h1eg](h1eg.md), tall enough to meet a
hunter's eye. Its claws open the earth ([dheghom](dheghom.md));
its weight breaks a man. Named for its size and for the long
sleep: the sleeper-in-winter, the one whose track crosses the
snow and then stops at a mouth in the hill.
