# h2yuh1en

category: 1m

PIE: *h₂yuh₁en-
Gloss: young
Descendants: young, Latin *iuvenis* (juvenile)

## Definition

Still-rising — what has passed the just-entered edge and
is now [bhuH](bhuH.md)-ing upward toward full size and strength
but has not yet held at that height. `h2yuh1en` names the
half-grown [h1ekwos](h1ekwos.md) running easy but not yet
carrying weight, the girl ([dhugh2ter](dhugh2ter.md)) whose
body is stretching faster than the eye can follow, the shoot on
the [deru](deru.md) pushing up past the old branches. The
`h2yuh1en` one has left the helplessness of the newborn and
gathers the thickness it will hold in middle-life, but does
not yet show the weathering of long-having-been. It is the
growing-middle of the age-line, where becoming outruns
settling — where [h1es](h1es.md) still leans toward
[bhuH](bhuH.md).
