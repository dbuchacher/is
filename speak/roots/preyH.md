# preyH

category: 1l

PIE: *preyH-
Gloss: to love / please
Descendants: friend, free, Latin *liber*

## Definition

To take pleasure in another and be taken pleasure in — the
mutual-gladness that binds two who are not blood-kin. `preyH` is
the friend-making love: where [leubh](leubh.md) is the caring
that wills the other's good, `preyH` is the joining that makes
two glad-together at being-together. "Friend" is one-who-is-
`preyH`-ed; "free" too, because among the tribe the free one is
the one who belongs by love and not by holding: the loved-one
and the free-one share a stem. Latin *liber* keeps this, marking
both the free man and the beloved child. The root sits at the
heart of the tribe as the glue that is not blood —
[ph2ter](ph2ter.md) and [meh2ter](meh2ter.md) give kin by birth;
`preyH` gives kin by gladness.
