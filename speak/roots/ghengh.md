# ghengh

category: 1i

PIE: *gʰengʰ-
Gloss: to go / walk
Descendants: Greek *bainō*? (overlap)

## Definition

The steady walking-gait of [pods](pods.md) on flat [dheghom](dheghom.md)
— foot after foot in even rhythm, neither the [h1ey](h1ey.md)
going-away nor the [gwem](gwem.md) coming-toward emphasized, just
the bare pacing itself. Where [h1ey](h1ey.md) points at the
away-motion and [gwem](gwem.md) at the toward-motion, `ghengh`
names the walking-as-walking: the body's default way of crossing
[dheghom](dheghom.md), what [h1eg](h1eg.md) does all day between
spots. Sibling with [steigh](steigh.md) in the walk-verbs cluster
— steady-flat versus rising-climb.
