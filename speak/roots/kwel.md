# kwel

**seed** | category: 1i

PIE: *kʷel-
Gloss: to turn / move around (wheel root)
Descendants: wheel, cycle, colony, Latin *colere*, Greek *pelō*

## Definition

To step around a held spot so that each pace returns near where
the last one started — the walking of [h1ey](h1ey.md) bent back
on itself until going-away becomes coming-back, [gwem](gwem.md)
and [h1ey](h1ey.md) fused into one closed path. The body loops;
the held center stays; [sem](sem.md) of start and end is the
turn completing.
