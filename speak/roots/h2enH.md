# h2enH

category: 1c

PIE: *h₂enH-
Gloss: grandmother / ancestress
Descendants: Latin *anus*, Hittite *ḫanna*

## Definition

The old [gwen](gwen.md) two [genh1](genh1.md) steps up the line
— the [meh2ter](meh2ter.md) of one's [meh2ter](meh2ter.md), or
any elder woman of the band whose own begetting goes back before
memory. She has [wes](wes.md)-ed long and carries what the band
holds of what came before. The root binds age and the female
up-line together; later tongues keep it for any old woman
because the grandmother is the old-woman the speaker's own line
knows by name.
