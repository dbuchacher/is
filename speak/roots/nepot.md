# nepot

category: 1c

PIE: *népōt-
Gloss: nephew / grandson
Descendants: nephew, Latin *nepos*

## Definition

The young male two [genh1](genh1.md) steps away from self along
the line: either the [suHnus](suHnus.md) of one's own
[suHnus](suHnus.md) (grandson) or the [suHnus](suHnus.md) of
one's [bhreh2ter](bhreh2ter.md) or [swesor](swesor.md) (nephew)
— not one's own begetting but kin through another's. Where
[suHnus](suHnus.md) sits one step down on the line, `nepot` sits
one step further, the child-of-the-child or the child-of-the-
sibling, still under the band's [peh2](peh2.md) but not at the
speaker's own hearth. The root holds both senses because both
describe the same kin-place: down-line-twice.
