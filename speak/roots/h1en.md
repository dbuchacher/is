# h1en

**seed** | category: 1n

PIE: *h₁én-
Gloss: in
Descendants: in, en-, em-, Latin *in*

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the inner side of any bounded region — the
speaker points past the boundary, at the held-within side, the
side that is not [h2epo](h2epo.md).

## Groups

- [inside-outside-pair](inside-outside-pair.md) — the inward leg, paired against [h2epo](h2epo.md)
- [held-within](held-within.md) — the inside pole of any boundary
