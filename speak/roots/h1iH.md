# h1iH

category: 1d

PIE: *h₁iH- / *h₁nter-
Gloss: entrails / inside
Descendants: enter, intestine, Latin *intus*

## Definition

All that is [h1en](h1en.md) the body — the coiled wet rope of
guts, the whole tangle of inside-things that spill out when
[krep](krep.md) is opened. `h1iH` does not single out
[kred](kred.md) or [yekwr](yekwr.md); it names the whole
inside-fill, everything the skin holds in. Points at the
belly-hollow, at what the hunter scoops out of a gutted
beast, at the place where food goes after [h1ed](h1ed.md) and
before the body takes what it needs. The same root carries
the "inside" pointing that later spreads to names of going-in
and being-among — because to be inside something is to be
among its `h1iH`. Kin to the spatial [h1en](h1en.md) seed:
where [h1en](h1en.md) names the in-ness of any bounded thing,
`h1iH` names the in-ness of the body in particular.
