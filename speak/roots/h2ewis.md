# h2ewis

category: 1f

PIE: *h₂éwis
Gloss: bird
Descendants: Latin *avis* (aviation)

## Definition

The [ghwer](ghwer.md) that goes by [h2en](h2en.md) rather than by
[pods](pods.md) — the beast with feather and wing that
[h1ey](h1ey.md)-s through air, rising from the branch into the
bright sky ([dyews](dyews.md)) and coming down again where it
chooses. `h2ewis` is the general pointing: anything winged,
anything that leaves [dheghom](dheghom.md) under its own going.
Its voice ([wekw](wekw.md)) carries from the branch at dawn
([h2ewsos](h2ewsos.md)); its eggs ([h1es](h1es.md)-ing in nests
above the hand's reach) feed the hunter who climbs. One of the
small-flesh beasts reached by the hunter's `per` — each by a
different medium, through air, through water, through grass.
