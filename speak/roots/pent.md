# pent

category: 1j

PIE: *pent-
Gloss: to try / find path
Descendants: find, path, Greek *pontos*

## Definition

To walk forward through land you do not yet know and come out on
the far side — the finding that happens by going. `pent` is how
the first track gets laid: [h1eg](h1eg.md) steps where no foot
has stepped, [spek](spek.md)-s ahead, turns when the ground will
not hold, and the walking itself cuts the path. It rides on
[per](per.md) — [per](per.md) is the straight-through drive,
`pent` is the trying-and-finding when the way is not yet clear.
"Find" springs from the same stem because in PIE finding IS the
walking-until-you-come-upon. Greek *pontos* names the open sea —
the hardest `pent` of all, water with no track yet cut.
