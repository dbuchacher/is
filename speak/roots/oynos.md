# oynos

category: 1b

PIE: *óynos / *sem-
Gloss: one
Descendants: one, Latin *unus*, Greek *heis*, sem- (same, simple)

## Definition

The single here picked out by pointing: [h1eg](h1eg.md) lifts the
hand, [ke](ke.md) marks the spot, and nothing stands beside it —
the count that stops at the first pace. Where [sem](sem.md) pulls
many onto the same spot, `oynos` names what is left after the
pulling: just the one that holds. It is the first step of the
count-line, the pace before [dwis](dwis.md) tears the holding into
two. The sibling of [sem](sem.md) on the number-side: same root
stem, one branch names the gathering, the other names the gathered.
