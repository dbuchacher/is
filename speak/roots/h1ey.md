# h1ey

**seed** | category: 1i

PIE: *h₁ey-
Gloss: to go
Descendants: go, exit, transit, Latin *ire*, Greek *ienai*

## Definition

To step out from where the speaker stands and keep stepping further
away — foot after foot carrying self from [ke](ke.md) toward
[h1e](h1e.md), from inside [h1en](h1en.md) toward
[h2epo](h2epo.md). The walking body becomes its own arrow: each
pace puts more ground between speaker-pole and the spot left behind.
