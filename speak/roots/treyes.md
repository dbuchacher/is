# treyes

category: 1b

PIE: *tréyes
Gloss: three
Descendants: three, Latin *tres*, Greek *treis*

## Definition

The pace past [dwoh1](dwoh1.md): [h1eg](h1eg.md) points at
[ke](ke.md), again, again — three spots held in the eye at once,
the smallest count that is neither [oynos](oynos.md) alone nor a
[dwoh1](dwoh1.md) pair but a many-that-still-fits-in-one-look.
Beyond three the speaker must walk the count with [h1ey](h1ey.md)
step by step; three is the last number the eye takes in one
[weid](weid.md). It names the first overflow of the pair, where
counting leaves behind the body's two hands and starts to lean on
[men](men.md).
