# mus

category: 1f

PIE: *mus-
Gloss: mouse
Descendants: mouse, Latin *mus*, Greek *mus*

## Definition

The small [ghwer](ghwer.md) that [legh](legh.md)-s in the ground
and slips [h1en](h1en.md) the grain and the stores — a warm body
no bigger than a thumb, all eye ([h3okw](h3okw.md)) and whisker,
scratching in the straw at night ([nokwts](nokwts.md)). `mus`
names the beast hunted from above by winged killers and from the
burrow by legless ones: small enough that it lives by hiding,
fast enough that it [h1ey](h1ey.md)-s under a stone before the
hand ([mr-gh](mr-gh.md)) closes. The smallest beast given its
own name — smaller than this, the world becomes insect and is
not named here.
