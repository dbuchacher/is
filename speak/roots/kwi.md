# kwi

**seed** | **deictic** | category: 1a

PIE: *kʷi- / *kʷo-
Gloss: interrogative / relative
Descendants: who, what, where, when, why, which, how; Latin *qui*; Greek *tis/ti*

## Definition

Grounds in pointing at question — the speaker points at an empty
slot in shared attention and hands the pointing back to the
listener, asking them to fill it. No reduction; the unfilled
pointing is the meaning.

- [deixis-at-hub](deixis-at-hub.md) — anchors the empty-slot pole
- [question-pointer](question-pointer.md) — the handoff gesture that turns pointing into a query
