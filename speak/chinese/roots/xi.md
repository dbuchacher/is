# xi

category: 1n

Chinese: **西** (xī, oracle-bone graph of a nest / bird-roost)
Gloss: west / where the sun sets
Source: oracle-bone corpus; paired with dong east

## Definition

The side where [ri](ri.md) sets at dusk — the last-bright side
of [di](di.md). The oracle-bone graph shows a nest: when birds
return to nest, the sun is on that side, so nest-side IS the
setting-side. The setting-pole of the east-west axis,
