# di

**seed** | category: 1e

Chinese: **土** / **地** (oracle-bone: 土 a mound-of-soil graph)
Gloss: earth / ground / soil / land
Source: the below-pole of oracle-bone cosmology

## Definition

Grounds by pointing down — at what the speaker-body stands on,
what the foot meets, what grows grain for
[nian](nian.md). Paired against [tian](tian.md): `tian` above,
`di` below — the two poles of oracle-bone cosmology. The soil-
ground AND the broader earth-expanse one word. Ancestors are
buried in `di`, harvests come up from `di` — it is the source-
and-destination-below.
