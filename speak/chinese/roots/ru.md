# ru

**seed** | category: 1a

Chinese: **汝** (oracle-bone attested; water-radical graph repurposed for 2sg)
Gloss: you (2sg pronoun)
Source: 2sg of oracle-bone and classical Chinese

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the addressee. The word the speaker uses
to name the body being-spoken-to. Paired with [wo](wo.md) (the
speaker) and [qi](qi.md) (the third-body). `ru` is the 2sg pole
of the speaking-triangle — the face the speaker turns toward when
producing [yan](yan.md).
