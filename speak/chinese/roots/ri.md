# ri

**seed** | category: 1e

Chinese: **日** (oracle-bone: a circle with a central dot — the sun-graph)
Gloss: sun / day / daytime
Source: foundational; the graph IS the sun

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the bright disc crossing [tian](tian.md)
from dawn to dusk — AND at the span-of-bright itself, the span
English calls "day." One word for the celestial body AND its
cycle-unit. Same unity Egyptian holds with `ra`: sun AND day one
morpheme. In oracle-bone divinations the king asks about "this
ri" meaning "today" — the body-of-the-sun and the span-of-light
it governs are named with a single word. Paired with
[yue](yue.md) as the day-body / night-body pair.
