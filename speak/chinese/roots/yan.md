# yan

**seed** | category: 1k

Chinese: **言** (oracle-bone: a tongue or a mouth with a marker above)
Gloss: speak / words / speech / saying
Source: oracle-bone speech-primitive

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what comes out of [kou](kou.md) — the
breath shaped into words, and the words themselves. One morpheme
for the verb of speaking AND the noun of the speech-produced.
In oracle-bone usage: the king's "yan" is his speaking-act AND
the edicts-spoken. Speech-event and speech-content named
together. Paired with [wen](wen.md) (hearing): `yan` is the
speaker-side, `wen` the hearer-side of the loop.
