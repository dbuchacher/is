# san

**seed** | category: 1n

Chinese: **三** (sān, oracle-bone graph of three horizontal strokes)
Gloss: three / triad / several
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

Grounds by three marks — the graph IS the count-of-three made
visible. `san` names three-things, and extends to "several/many"
in some oracle-bone contexts (three-as-generic-plurality). The
triad-count, completing the base trio after [yi](yi.md) and
[er](er.md). Chinese uses `san` in many triad compounds:
三才 "three-powers" (heaven/earth/human), 三皇 "three-sovereigns."
