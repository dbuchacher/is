# shen

category: 1e

Chinese: **神** (shén, oracle-bone graph of a lightning-altar)
Gloss: spirit / god / divine / mysterious
Source: oracle-bone corpus; later central to religious vocabulary

## Definition

A divine body — the kind of being [tian](tian.md) is, the kind
that receives offerings and sends [ming](ming.md). Where
[tian](tian.md) is the high-god-as-sky-body, `shen` is any
divine body: clan-ghosts, nature-spirits, deified-heroes.
The generic divine word. Paired with [gui](gui.md) (ghost /
restless-dead) in the two-way spirit classification: `shen`
are the settled-revered spirits, `gui` are the unsettled ones.
