# xiao

**seed** | category: 1m

Chinese: **小** (xiǎo, oracle-bone graph of three small dots)
Gloss: small / little / minor / humble
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

Grounds by showing the size — three small dots, the pointing-at-
small made visible. `xiao` names the smaller-than-baseline size,
and extends to humble, junior, minor. 小人 "small-person" =
commoner or petty-one, the inverse of 大人 "big-person." The
small-pole of the size axis, paired with [da](da.md).
