# lai

**seed** | category: 1i

Chinese: **來** (lái, oracle-bone graph of wheat, loaned for the coming-verb)
Gloss: come / arrive / toward-speaker
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a body approaching the speaker — the
opposite pole of [xing](xing.md) (going-away / acting-forward).
When a body moves toward [wo](wo.md), the verb is `lai`. The
graph was originally wheat (the ripened-grain) but was loaned
for the toward-speaker motion verb very early. The coming-
primitive.
