# bei

category: 1n

Chinese: **北** (běi, oracle-bone graph of two people back-to-back)
Gloss: north / back-side / behind
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

The cold side of [di](di.md) in the Shang homeland — the side
a body faces when turning its back to [ri](ri.md) at midday.
The oracle-bone graph shows two bodies back-to-back: the `bei`
side is the back-side, the one you turn AWAY from. Same morpheme
later named the body-part (back) and still works as compass-
pole. The north-pole.
