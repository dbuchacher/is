# wo

**seed** | category: 1a

Chinese: **我** (oracle-bone: a graph of a bladed weapon, repurposed for 1sg)
Gloss: I / me / my (1sg pronoun)
Source: universal 1sg across oracle-bone and later Chinese

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the speaker. The word the speaker uses to
name the body-doing-the-speaking. In oracle-bone divinations the
king writes `wo` to mean "I, the speaking-king" — the speech-
origin body. Paired with [ru](ru.md) (you, the addressed body)
and [qi](qi.md) (that, the third body named-but-not-present).
The 1sg pole of the speaking-triangle.
