# zhong

category: 1n

Chinese: **中** (zhōng, oracle-bone graph of a banner at the center)
Gloss: center / middle / the-middle-kingdom / hub
Source: oracle-bone corpus; highly productive in compounds

## Definition

The middle of a spread — what sits between [shang](shang.md) and
[xia](xia.md), between [dong](dong.md) and [xi](xi.md), between
[nan](nan.md) and [bei](bei.md). The oracle-bone graph shows a
banner planted at the central axis of a camp: the hub the four
sides face. `zhong` is the hub of the 4-cardinal system, the
fifth point completing shang-xia and the four compass-sides into
a full spatial frame. China named itself 中國 "middle-kingdom"
— the hub at the civilizational scale. One word for the
structural-middle of any frame.
