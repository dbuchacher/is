# er

**seed** | category: 1n

Chinese: **二** (èr, oracle-bone graph of two horizontal strokes)
Gloss: two / pair / second
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

Grounds by two marks — the graph IS the count-of-two made
visible. `er` names two-things, a pair, the second-in-order.
The paired-count, following [yi](yi.md) and preceding
[san](san.md).
