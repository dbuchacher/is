# shang

**seed** | category: 1e

Chinese: **上** (oracle-bone: a short stroke above a horizontal baseline)
Gloss: up / above / upon / high / upper
Source: the graph IS the up-pointer — a mark above a baseline

## Definition

Grounds by the graph itself: a small stroke placed above a long
horizontal line. `shang` IS that above-relation. One word for
"upward" (the way-of-going), "above" (relative position), "upon"
(on top of), and "upper" (the higher of two). The bare up-pointer
— more general than [tian](tian.md) (which is specifically the
cosmic sky-above). Paired against [xia](xia.md). In oracle-bone
court-phrases, the Shang king is "shang" in the rank-sense: the
one on top.
