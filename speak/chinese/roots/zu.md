# zu

**seed** | category: 1d

Chinese: **足** (zú, oracle-bone graph of a foot)
Gloss: foot / leg / enough / sufficient / basis
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the walking-part below the leg — the
organ that carries the body across [di](di.md). But `zu` also
names enough-ness, basis, sufficiency: 足夠 "foot-enough" means
"sufficient." The foot IS what is enough to stand on; the base
of support. Body-foot AND sufficiency fused into one morpheme.
