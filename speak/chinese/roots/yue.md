# yue

**seed** | category: 1e

Chinese: **月** (oracle-bone: a crescent — the moon-graph)
Gloss: moon / month / lunar-cycle
Source: foundational; the graph IS the moon

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the night-light crossing [tian](tian.md)
in its phases — AND at the span between one full-moon and the
next, the span English calls "month." One word for the celestial
body AND its cycle-unit. Parallel to [ri](ri.md) (sun=day):
[yue](yue.md) is moon=month. In oracle-bone divinations the
lunation counts the calendar — so the celestial body and the
calendar-unit naturally share a name. The night-pole paired with
[ri](ri.md)'s daytime-pole.
