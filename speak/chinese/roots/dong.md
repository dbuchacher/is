# dong

category: 1n

Chinese: **東** (dōng, oracle-bone graph of sun-through-tree)
Gloss: east / where the sun rises
Source: oracle-bone corpus; paired with xi west

## Definition

The side where [ri](ri.md) rises at dawn — the first-bright side
of [di](di.md). The oracle-bone graph shows [ri](ri.md) rising
through trees: the visible event of dawn made into the morpheme
for the compass-side where it happens. Paired against
[xi](xi.md) (where the sun sets). The rising-side pole of the
east-west axis.
