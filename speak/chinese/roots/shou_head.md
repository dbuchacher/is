# shou_head

**seed** | category: 1d

Chinese: **首** (oracle-bone: a head with hair-marks on top)
Gloss: head / top / chief / first / leader
Source: oracle-bone pictograph of a head in profile

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at the top of the body — where [kou](kou.md)
and eyes and the hair sit. AND at "top of anything" — top of a
list, top of a rank. AND at "chief" (the head of the court, the
head of the army). AND at "first" (shou-xian = first in a
sequence). One word for body-head AND top-position AND chief-
rank AND first-in-order. Same polysemic cluster Egyptian holds
with `tp` and Sumerian with `saŋ`: four-way unity, three
independent substrates converge.
