# da

**seed** | category: 1m

Chinese: **大** (dà, oracle-bone graph of a person with arms outstretched)
Gloss: big / great / large / vast / important
Source: oracle-bone corpus; appears in countless compounds

## Definition

Grounds by showing the size — the graph IS a body with arms
spread wide, the pointing-at-big made visible. `da` names the
bigger-than-baseline size, and extends to greatness, importance,
senior-rank. 大王 "big-king" = great-ruler, 大人 "big-person" =
adult or important-one. The big-pole of the size axis, paired
with [xiao](xiao.md).
