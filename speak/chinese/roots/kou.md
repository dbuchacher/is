# kou

**seed** | category: 1d

Chinese: **口** (oracle-bone: a square opening — the mouth-graph)
Gloss: mouth / opening / entrance / aperture
Source: foundational; the graph IS the opening

## Definition

Grounds by the graph: a square-shaped hole. `kou` is the body-
mouth AND any opening — the door-kou, the well-kou, the
mountain-pass-kou, the cave-kou, the river-kou (river-mouth
where it meets the sea). One word for body-opening and
world-opening. The speaker's mouth where [yan](yan.md) comes
out; any hole-in-a-surface where in-goes-or-comes-out. Same
unity Egyptian holds with `rA` and Sumerian with `ka`. The
mouth-primitive.
