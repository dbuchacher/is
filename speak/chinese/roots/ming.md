# ming

category: 1k

Chinese: **命** (mìng, oracle-bone graph of a mouth over a kneeling person)
Gloss: command / order / name / fate / life-span
Source: oracle-bone corpus; central term in Shang divination

## Definition

The authoritative utterance from [tian](tian.md) or a ruler —
the spoken decree that makes the world conform. But `ming`
extends: what is commanded IS one's fate, one's allotted span,
one's name-and-identity. Sky-decree AND fate AND name AND
life-span share the morpheme. In Shang divination, `ming` is
what the high-god sends down; one's `ming` is what one is given
to enact. Frame 5 hub-event at theological scale: [tian](tian.md)
commands, the world acts, the body `ming`-s its role.
