# xia

**seed** | category: 1e

Chinese: **下** (oracle-bone: a short stroke below a horizontal baseline)
Gloss: down / below / under / low / lower
Source: mirror of [shang](shang.md) in the oracle-bone graph system

## Definition

Grounds by the graph itself: a small stroke placed below a long
horizontal line. `xia` IS that below-relation. One word for
"downward" (the way-of-going), "below" (position), "under"
(beneath), and "lower" (the lower of two). Paired against
[shang](shang.md). More general than [di](di.md) (which is
specifically the cosmic earth-below). The Shang-era king's
subjects are "xia" in the rank-sense: those below the king.
