# qi

**seed** | category: 1a

Chinese: **其** (oracle-bone: a winnowing-basket graph, loaned for 3sg/demonstrative)
Gloss: his / its / that / that-one
Source: ubiquitous in oracle-bone and classical texts

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at a previously-named body that is neither
[wo](wo.md) nor [ru](ru.md). The third-pole of the speaking-
triangle. In oracle-bone divinations `qi` often marks "that which
was named in the preceding question" — the anaphoric pointer.
Also serves as possessive for 3sg ("qi-X" = "his X"). The
distal-but-known position in the deictic field.
