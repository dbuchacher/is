# tian

**seed** | category: 1e

Chinese: **天** (oracle-bone: a person-graph with an enlarged top/head)
Gloss: sky / heaven / high-god / Tian
Source: foundational; the Zhou high-god AND the overhead-vault

## Definition

Grounds by pointing up — what is above the speaker-body, what
holds [ri](ri.md) by day and [yue](yue.md) by night. And at the
high-god who IS the sky. In oracle-bone and early Zhou texts,
`tian` names both the overhead-vault AND the high-god who issues
the Mandate (tian-ming). Sky and god one word — the same unity
Sumerian holds with `an` and Egyptian with `ra`/`nTr`-cluster. The
oracle-bone graph shows a body with its top enlarged: the
overhead-part OF a person stood for the overhead-part OF the
world AND for the divine. Paired against [di](di.md).
