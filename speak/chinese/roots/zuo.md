# zuo

category: 1h

Chinese: **作** (zuò, oracle-bone graph of a person making with hands)
Gloss: do / make / act / produce
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

The bare doing-verb — what [shou_hand](shou_hand.md) does when
it shapes a thing from no-thing. Where [xing](xing.md) is walking-
and-doing-as-conduct (long-scale action), `zuo` is the discrete
make-this-thing act. Paired with the command-verb in
the hub-event of creation: thought in [xin](xin.md) → [ming](ming.md)
in utterance → `zuo` in the world. The make-happen primitive.
