# shi

**seed** | category: 1a

Chinese: **是** (shì, oracle-bone graph of a sun over a foot-stopping-mark)
Gloss: this / this-here / is-so (later: copula)
Source: oracle-bone corpus; originally demonstrative, later became the copula

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what is near-at-hand. Where [qi](qi.md)
points at the distal-or-previously-named and [wo](wo.md) is
self-pointing, `shi` is the proximal pointer — the this-here.
In later Chinese the morpheme grammaticalized into the copula
("X is Y"), but at oracle-bone scale the pointing-at-this sense
is primary. The proximal demonstrative.
