# jian

**seed** | category: 1j

Chinese: **見** (jiàn, oracle-bone graph of an eye over a person)
Gloss: see / perceive / meet / appear
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

Grounds by pointing at what the eye does — the taking-in of
[ri](ri.md)-light from a body or thing. But `jian` extends: to
see someone IS to meet them; a thing that `jian`-s is a thing
that appears. One morpheme for the act-of-seeing AND the
event-of-meeting AND the being-visible. The seeing-primitive.
