# nan

category: 1n

Chinese: **南** (nán, oracle-bone graph of a bronze bell)
Gloss: south / the warm-side / toward-the-sun
Source: oracle-bone corpus

## Definition

The warmer side of [di](di.md) in the northern-hemisphere Shang
homeland — the side where [ri](ri.md) passes at midday. The
oracle-bone graph shows a southern-style bell (instrument of the
southern barbarians), loaned into the compass-sense. South-pole
of the north-south axis, paired against [bei](bei.md).
