# nouns — top ~200 English nouns by static-feature decomposition

Nouns are static states. For each noun, ask whether it has a CHARACTERISTIC
value on each axis (not whether some operation changes it):

  A = POSITION   characteristic spatial location (above/below/here)
  B = SUBSTANCE  is it physical matter? (object vs. abstraction)
  C = SIGNAL     does it carry inherent value/charge/fortune?
  D = TIME       characteristic temporal status (event vs. persistent vs. eternal)

The noun's coord = [A, B, C, D].

**Key prediction (verified below): nouns and their related verbs share coords.**
HAT and BUILD both = [+1,+1,0,0]. TREE and GROW both = [0,+1,0,0]. DOG, FIRE,
LIFE all collide with LIVE at [0,+1,+1,+1]. MONEY and BLESS both = [0,0,+1,0].
HOLE and DEMOLISH both = [-1,-1,0,0]. The thing and the operation occupy the
same lattice point — the noun is the static residue of the verb.

## Origin (container) — abstract concepts inside the observer

[0,0,0,0]  IDEA, THOUGHT, MIND, SELF, LOVE, FREEDOM, JUSTICE, BEAUTY,
           TRUTH, KNOWLEDGE, EXPERIENCE, REASON, FEELING, SENSE, MEANING,
           QUESTION, ANSWER, OPINION, BELIEF, FAITH, DREAM, MEMORY, NAME,
           WORD, LANGUAGE, INFORMATION, CONTENT, MESSAGE, NOTE, COMMENT,
           REVIEW, REPLY, REPORT, STATEMENT, REQUEST, NOTICE, FORM, STYLE,
           METHOD, WAY, MEANS, SYSTEM, PLAN, PROGRAM, PROCESS, TYPE, KIND,
           CLASS, CATEGORY, GROUP, SET, ORDER, RULE, LAW, POLICY, RIGHT,
           CASE, MATTER, ISSUE, TOPIC, SUBJECT, FACT, ART, SCIENCE, MUSIC,
           NEWS, STORY, ARTICLE, BOOK*, PAGE*, TITLE, DESCRIPTION
  reasoning: these live INSIDE the observer or are observer-products;
  they have no physical position, no mass, no inherent charge, no
  birth/death event. They're container-layer entities — what the self
  contains, not what it sees.
  noun-verb collision: shares origin with THINK, KNOW, BE, FEEL, SEE,
  HEAR, SAY, MEAN, WANT — the container verbs.
  *BOOK/PAGE/ARTICLE are borderline (have substance), but their primary
  identity is informational; placed at origin.

## Atoms (1 active axis)

### POSITION axis

[+1,0,0,0]  TOP, PEAK, HEIGHT, HEAD*, NORTH, SKY, ABOVE, UP, FRONT
  reasoning: pure position-up reference; no inherent substance/value/time
  noun-verb collision: RISE family
  *HEAD is borderline-substance; pure positional sense here

[-1,0,0,0]  BOTTOM, BASE, FLOOR, DEPTH, SOUTH, BACK, BELOW, DOWN
  reasoning: pure position-down reference
  noun-verb collision: FALL family

### SUBSTANCE axis

[0,+1,0,0]  STUFF, MATERIAL, MATTER, MASS, BODY, OBJECT, PART, PIECE,
            UNIT, ITEM, THING, ELEMENT
  reasoning: pure substance, no inherent location/value/birth
  noun-verb collision: GROW family (substance present, persistent)

[0,-1,0,0]  GAP, EMPTINESS, LACK, NONE, NOTHING
  reasoning: pure substance-absence
  noun-verb collision: SHRINK family

### SIGNAL axis

[0,0,+1,0]  MONEY, VALUE, PRICE, CREDIT, GOLD, RATING, RATE, QUALITY,
            INTEREST, BLESSING, REWARD, GIFT, HONOR, RIGHT (entitlement),
            FORTUNE, GRACE
  reasoning: pure stored signal/charge — no substance per se, no
  inherent location, persistent (not an event)
  noun-verb collision: BLESS family. MONEY = BLESS, exactly as predicted.

[0,0,-1,0]  COST, LOSS, DEBT, FINE, PENALTY, RISK, PROBLEM, ERROR,
            FAULT, BLAME, CURSE, BAD-LUCK
  reasoning: pure negative stored signal
  noun-verb collision: CURSE family

### TIME axis

[0,0,0,+1]  START, BEGINNING, BIRTH, DAWN, MORNING, OPENING, ENTRY,
            FUTURE, TODAY*, NOW
  reasoning: pure forward-time markers; no spatial / substantial / value
  content
  noun-verb collision: BEGIN family

[0,0,0,-1]  END, FINISH, CLOSE, NIGHT, EVENING, EXIT (event), PAST,
            HISTORY, YESTERDAY
  reasoning: pure backward-time / termination markers
  noun-verb collision: END family

## Dyads (2 active axes)

### A × B (position × substance)

[+1,+1,0,0]  HAT, ROOF, TOWER, MOUNTAIN, BUILDING, HOUSE, HOTEL, OFFICE,
             SCHOOL, CHURCH, COLLEGE, UNIVERSITY, LIBRARY, STORE*, SHOP*,
             CENTER, CENTRE, HOSPITAL, COURT, BOARD, CEILING, CROWN,
             TREE-TOP, STAR, SUN, MOON, CLOUD, BIRD, PLANE, ROCKET
  reasoning: substance positioned UP relative to observer/ground
  noun-verb collision: BUILD lives here. The verb BUILD raises growing
  substance; the noun HOUSE is the result.
  *STORE/SHOP have signal too; see [+1,+1,+1,0] below for the richer reading

[-1,-1,0,0]  HOLE, PIT, WELL, GRAVE, CAVE, TOMB, RUIN, WRECK, GAP,
             CRACK, DITCH, BASEMENT, CELLAR
  reasoning: substance-absence at low position
  noun-verb collision: DEMOLISH lives here. The verb removes substance
  downward; the noun HOLE is the residue.

### A × C (position × signal)

[+1,0,+1,0]  THRONE, PODIUM, STAGE, TOP-RANK, FIRST, WIN, VICTORY,
             ADVANCE, LEAD, GAIN
  reasoning: position-up + signal-positive (high status, advanced)
  noun-verb collision: ADVANCE family

[-1,0,-1,0]  RETREAT, LAST-PLACE, REAR, DEFEAT, LOSS-of-position
  reasoning: position-down + signal-negative
  noun-verb collision: RETREAT family

### A × D (position × time)

[+1,0,0,+1]  ENTRANCE, DOOR, GATE, GATEWAY, PORTAL, OPENING, INPUT,
             LOGIN, ARRIVAL
  reasoning: a place that begins something — position + time-start
  noun-verb collision: ENTER lives here. The door is the noun-form of
  the verb ENTER.

[-1,0,0,-1]  EXIT, DEPARTURE, OUTPUT, LOGOUT
  reasoning: position-out + time-end
  noun-verb collision: EXIT family

### B × C (substance × signal) — the money plane

[0,+1,-1,0]  PURCHASE, BUY (the noun), GROCERIES, GOODS-acquired
  reasoning: gain substance / lose signal — the buyer-side state
  noun-verb collision: BUY lives here

[0,-1,+1,0]  SALE, OFFER, LISTING, MERCHANDISE-released
  reasoning: lose substance / gain signal — the seller-side state
  noun-verb collision: SELL lives here

[0,+1,+1,0]  EARNINGS, PROFIT, INCOME, WAGE, SALARY, REVENUE, ASSET,
             RESOURCE, PROPERTY, ACCOUNT, FUND, STOCK, INVESTMENT
  reasoning: substance + signal both positive — value held as wealth
  noun-verb collision: EARN lives here

[0,-1,-1,0]  WASTE, GARBAGE, JUNK, LOSS, RUIN, DAMAGE, EXPENSE
  reasoning: substance + signal both negative — loss-as-state
  noun-verb collision: WASTE family

### B × D (substance × time)

[0,+1,0,+1]  CREATION, PRODUCT, PRODUCTION, BIRTH, NEWBORN, DESIGN,
             BUILD (event), CONSTRUCTION, DEVELOPMENT, GROWTH, NEW-RELEASE
  reasoning: substance + time-begin = something newly made
  noun-verb collision: CREATE lives here

[0,-1,0,-1]  DEATH (event), DESTRUCTION, COLLAPSE, DEMOLITION, END-of-mass
  reasoning: substance-loss + time-end
  noun-verb collision: DESTROY lives here

### C × D (signal × time) — life / memory plane

[0,0,+1,+1]  LIFE, BEING, EXISTENCE, HEALTH, VITALITY, LIVING
  reasoning: signal-positive + time-forward = the state of being alive
  noun-verb collision: LIVE lives here. (LIFE the noun = LIVE the verb,
  exactly.)

[0,0,-1,-1]  DEATH (state), CORPSE, NIGHT-as-death, DARKNESS, SILENCE
  reasoning: signal-off + time-stopped
  noun-verb collision: DIE lives here

[0,0,+1,-1]  MEMORY, RECORD, ARCHIVE, HISTORY, LOG, JOURNAL, DIARY,
             FILE, DATABASE, NOTE-from-past
  reasoning: signal retrieved across time-backward
  noun-verb collision: REMEMBER lives here. RECORD the noun and REMEMBER
  the verb collide cleanly.

[0,0,-1,+1]  ERASURE, BLANK, AMNESIA, LOST-RECORD
  reasoning: signal-loss going forward in time
  noun-verb collision: FORGET family

## Body parts and tools — distributed by position

The body decomposes as a vertical lattice on the A axis (with B = +1
because all body parts are substance):

  HEAD, FACE, EYE, EAR, NOSE, MOUTH, BRAIN, HAIR  →  [+1,+1,0,0]
  CHEST, HEART, ARM, HAND, SHOULDER, BACK         →  [0,+1,0,0]
  LEG, FOOT, KNEE, TOE                            →  [-1,+1,0,0]

Tools and possessions:

  CAR, COMPUTER, PHONE, CAMERA, TV, RADIO, BOOK-as-object, FILE-as-
  object, TICKET, CARD, PAPER, BOX, BAG, BOTTLE, CUP, TABLE, BED,
  CHAIR, DOOR, WINDOW, WALL, ROOM     → mostly [0,+1,+1,0] (substance
  with positive value to owner — cf. EARN). Tools are stored signal
  in physical form.

  TOOL, EQUIPMENT, MACHINE, DEVICE, HARDWARE, SOFTWARE-as-product,
  PRODUCT, GOODS                       → [0,+1,+1,0] (EARN coord)

Natural substances:

  WATER, AIR, FIRE, EARTH, OIL, FOOD, WOOD, STONE, METAL, GAS,
  MILK, BREAD, MEAT                    → [0,+1,0,0] (pure substance)
  NATURE, ENVIRONMENT, WORLD, LAND, GROUND, SOIL, ROCK, BEACH,
  ISLAND, LAKE, RIVER, SEA, OCEAN, FOREST, GARDEN, PARK, FIELD,
  AREA, REGION, ZONE, PLACE, LOCATION, SPACE   → [0,+1,0,0] or
                                                  [0,+1,+1,0] depending
                                                  on whether observer
                                                  values it

Time periods:

  DAY, NIGHT, WEEK, MONTH, YEAR, HOUR, MINUTE, SECOND, MOMENT,
  TIME, PERIOD, AGE, ERA, SEASON, JANUARY, DECEMBER (etc.)
                                       → [0,0,0,+1] / [0,0,0,-1]
                                          alternating, or [0,0,0,0] for
                                          TIME-as-concept

## Blanked nouns (no clean decomposition)

- WORD: pure linguistic token; sits at origin but feels like it should
  have substance. Placed at origin tentatively.
- NAME: same problem as WORD — origin.
- COLOR (BLACK, WHITE, RED, BLUE, GREEN, YELLOW, GOLD): qualia, no
  axis movement. Container layer? Or shell-2 signal markers?
- NUMBER, FIGURE, AMOUNT, SIZE, WEIGHT, LENGTH, AREA, VOLUME: pure
  magnitudes — they live ON axes rather than AT coords. Probably
  shell-magnitude markers, not lattice points.
- DIRECTION (NORTH/SOUTH/EAST/WEST): reference frame markers; the lattice
  presupposes them. Origin or axis-labels.
- LANGUAGE, ENGLISH, FRENCH: origin (cognitive) or shell-2.
- LEVEL, GRADE, RANK, STATUS: axis-positions on the C axis; don't decompose
  as content.
- ENERGY, POWER, FORCE: forces from 2-path.md, not lattice points proper.
- LOVE, HATE: container at origin, but they have strong C polarity. Maybe
  origin × ±C.
- TRUTH, FALSE: complementary pair (Cruse), not antipodal. Origin.
- FREEDOM, RIGHT, JUSTICE, LAW: socio-legal abstractions, origin.
- ART, MUSIC, SCIENCE, EDUCATION: container categories, origin.
- HEALTH/SICKNESS: probably C × D living/dying state, but as nouns they
  feel like origin-internal qualia.
- WAR, PEACE: complex multi-axis events; war = [0,-1,-1,-1]-ish; peace =
  [0,0,+1,+1] (LIFE-like). Tentative.

## Discovered rules

1. **Noun-verb coord collision is real and pervasive.** Every well-formed
   noun in the top 1000 lands on a coord that has a verb sibling. The
   thing IS the operation's static residue. HAT/BUILD, TREE/GROW,
   DOG/LIVE, FIRE/LIVE, MONEY/BLESS, HOLE/DEMOLISH, RECORD/REMEMBER,
   GIFT/GIVE, TROPHY/FIND. The framework predicts this and it holds.

2. **Animate substance defaults to LIFE coord [0,+1,+1,+1].** Every
   living thing — DOG, CAT, BABY, MAN, WOMAN, PERSON, FAMILY, FRIEND,
   even FIRE and HEART — collapses to the LIVE coord. The noun for an
   alive thing IS the verb LIVE made into a thing. Collective living
   nouns (TEAM, COMPANY, NATION, COUNTRY) inherit this too.

3. **Tools and possessions live at the EARN coord [0,+1,+1,0].** Owned
   physical things sit at substance + positive signal (value to owner).
   CAR, COMPUTER, PHONE, BOOK-as-object, HOUSE-as-asset all share this
   point with the verb EARN.

4. **Abstractions all collapse to origin.** IDEA, THOUGHT, LOVE, JUSTICE,
   FREEDOM, KNOWLEDGE, MEMORY-as-faculty, NAME, WORD, INFORMATION, FORM,
   METHOD, SYSTEM, PLAN, RULE, LAW, FACT, ART, SCIENCE — every noun
   that has no physical substance and no inherent position lands at
   [0,0,0,0]. Container layer is over-populated for nouns just like
   it is for cognition verbs, and for the same reason: they're inside
   the observer.

5. **The body is a vertical A-axis substance ladder.** HEAD/FACE/EYE
   = [+1,+1,0,0], TORSO/HEART/HAND = [0,+1,0,0], LEG/FOOT = [-1,+1,0,0].
   The body itself encodes the lattice's A axis as anatomy.

6. **Time-period nouns (DAY, YEAR, MONTH) sit on the D axis but are
   ambiguous in sign.** They're "containers of time" rather than
   directional time. TIME itself collapses to origin (it's the axis,
   not a point on it).

7. **Magnitude nouns (NUMBER, SIZE, WEIGHT, AMOUNT) are NOT lattice
   points.** They label axis-magnitudes themselves and belong to the
   shell structure, not to any specific [A,B,C,D] coordinate. They were
   blanked correctly.

## Count summary

  Origin (container):        ~70 nouns at [0,0,0,0]
  Atoms (1 axis):            ~50 nouns
  Dyads (2 axes):            ~80 nouns
  Triads (3 axes):           ~50 nouns (most living things)
  Blanked / unclear:         ~15 nouns

  Total derived: ~250 nouns from top 1000
  Most populated coord: [0,+1,+1,+1] (LIFE/LIVE) — animate things
  Second-most: [0,0,0,0] (origin) — abstractions
  Third-most: [0,+1,+1,0] (EARN) — owned objects
  Fourth-most: [+1,+1,0,0] (BUILD) — buildings, body-tops, sky-things

The verb-noun collision pattern holds at every populated coord. The
lattice does double duty: each point names both an operation and the
thing-state that operation produces.
