Wrong. It's **3 triality-peer H's + 1 Z2-mirror H**. The 4th is
qualitatively different, not another generation. Drawing 4 symmetric

framework gives 3+3 via triality + SO(10) 16-spinor).

Tensor product (QC Hilbert space) ≠ direct-sum-with-involution
(CD doubling). Different functors. Clean identity only at
single-qubit = SU(2) = unit H level. Quantum/classical distinction
lives at VERB tier (frame 6), not at algebra-layer tier. Quantum

### Don't use htmlpreview.github.io for the viz

Breaks on ES modules (importmap + `<script type="module">`). Use
**raw.githack.com** instead, or enable GitHub Pages on the repo
for native serving.

non-F4 wheel positions couple only via hub (gravity), not via F4
forces. Look for discrete ultralight DM peaks (0.05, 0.1 μeV in
ADMX-style haloscopes) and gravitational lensing microstructure
instead.
