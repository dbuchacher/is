tian
chinese morpheme — atomic file
Chinese **天** (oracle-bone: a person-graph with an enlarged top/head) "sky / heaven / high-god / Tian"
Definition
Grounds by pointing up — what is above the speaker-body, what holds ri by day and yue by night. And at the high-god who IS the sky. In oracle-bone and early Zhou texts, `tian` names both the overhead-vault AND the high-god who issues the Mandate (tian-ming). Sky and god one word — the same unity Sumerian holds with `an` and Egyptian with `ra`/`nTr`-cluster. The oracle-bone graph shows a body with its top enlarged: the overhead-part OF a person stood for the overhead-part OF the world AND for the divine. Paired against di.
