Chinese **月** (oracle-bone: a crescent — the moon-graph) "moon / month / lunar-cycle"
Definition
Grounds by pointing at the night-light crossing tian in its phases — AND at the span between one full-moon and the next, the span English calls "month." One word for the celestial body AND its cycle-unit. Parallel to ri (sun=day): yue is moon=month. In oracle-bone divinations the lunation counts the calendar — so the celestial body and the calendar-unit naturally share a name. The night-pole paired with ri's daytime-pole.

  • unity-preservation — moon AND month as one word
  • celestial-bodies — the nighttime celestial
  • time-cycles — the moon-cycle unit, paired with [ri](ri.md) and [nian](nian.md)
