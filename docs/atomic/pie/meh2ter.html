meh2ter
pie morpheme — atomic file
PIE *méh₂tēr "mother"
Descendants: mother, Latin *mater*, Greek *mētēr*
Definition
The gwen who bher-s the child in the womb, then feeds it from her own body — the first ke the infant points at, the first mouth-sound *ma* the infant makes while feeding. `meh2ter` carries both the nursing motion and the standing-over-the-young that binds it to peh2: she feeds and she shields. The root is the child's-eye-view of the nearest adult, the one whose mr-gh and whose warmth hold the whole early world. Paired with ph2ter as the two poles the young stand between.
