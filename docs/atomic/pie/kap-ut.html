kap-ut
pie morpheme — atomic file
PIE *kap-ut- "head"
Descendants: head, captain, chapter, Latin *caput*
Definition
The top-knob of krep where the mouth sits and the eyes look out — the part h1eg points at when saying "here I am," the part that rises highest when the body steh2-s. `kap-ut` names the head as the leading end of the walking body: what goes first through the door, what h1ey ahead, what turns to face what wekw-s. The one who walks at the front of the band is the head of the band because the head of the body is what leads; the later split into "captain" and "chapter" holds this same pointing — the first-part, the going-first-part. Sits on the neck h2en the shoulders, carries h3okw, h2ews-a, nas, dnghuh2, and h1dont as its crew.
