Sumerian **an** (written 𒀭) "sky / heaven / god / divine / up"
Definition
Grounds by pointing up — what is above the speaker's head, what the body cannot touch, what the igi looks toward when lifted. `an` is the sky, and `an` is also god — one sign, two "concepts" modern languages treat as separate. In Sumerian writing every god's name is prefixed with the sign `an` because a god IS a sky-being; sky and divine are one substrate fact. Paired against ki (down, earth, place): `an-ki` names the whole-cosmos as the up-down pair. The up-pole of the vertical axis.
