Sumerian **ka** "mouth / gate / opening / pass-through point"
Definition
Grounds by pointing at the opening of the body through which dug4 comes out and food comes in — the hole between lips, the threshold of ge26 and zae in communication. But `ka` is also the city-gate: the opening in the wall of a settlement, the pass-through between inside and outside at the scale of a whole city. Body-mouth and city-gate share one word because they share one operation: bounded threshold through which things pass. The opening-primitive — at the body scale the opening is a mouth, at the city scale it is a gate, both are `ka`.
Groups this morpheme belongs to:
  • body-parts — the opening of the body
  • unity-preservation — mouth AND gate as one word (same stroke at two scales)
  • threshold-primitive — bounded opening that things pass through
