Egyptian **rnpt** (written with a notched palm-rib 𓆳) "year / annual-cycle"
Definition
The great-cycle that holds three tr seasons — the span in which ra completes a full return through all points of the sky-arch, and the Nile completes one mw flood- cycle. One `rnpt` is ~365 hrw days. Counted from the heliacal rising of Sirius (wpt-rnpt, "opening of the year"). Year-regnal-count is how kings dated their reigns: "year-5 of nb-X." The outer-scale of the time-cycle, holding the three seasons inside it.
Groups this morpheme belongs to:
  • time-cycle — the outer-scale year-count
  • time-span-scales — the year-scale above season-scale
