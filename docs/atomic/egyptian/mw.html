Egyptian **mw** (written with water-ripple sign 𓈗) "water"
Definition
Grounds by pointing at the liquid — the Nile flowing past tA, rain falling from pt, the ra-god's path through the waters of nwn (primeval flood) each night. The water-primitive. Central to Egyptian civilization because the Nile's annual flood IS the life-bringing `mw`. Also used for semen, saliva, and any liquid bodily substance — the liquid category is one word.
