Egyptian **tp** "head / top / chief / first / upon"
Definition
Grounds by pointing at the top of the body — the round part above the shoulders where Hr (face), rA (mouth), and the eyes all sit. But `tp` is also "top of anything" — the top of a mountain, the top of a list, the first-of-a-series. And it is "chief" (the tp-of-the-staff, the leading one). And it is the preposition "upon" (on top of). One word for head-of-body, top-of-thing, first-in-order, chief-of-group, on-top-of. Same five-way unity Sumerian showed with `saŋ`.
