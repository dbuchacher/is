Surrounding site, other pyramids, tools, design theory, Hawass modern updates.

## Provenance

Source: W.M.F. Petrie, "The Pyramids and Temples of Gizeh" (1883)
Hawass sections from 1990 update (metric units, not converted to cubits).
See petrie-tier1.md header for full processing chain.

## Reading this file

- Rows with `designed_cubits` only: the intent is known (inches stripped).
- Rows with `value | unit | cubits`: doesn't snap to clean cubit. Open question.
- Angles, counts, weights: original units, no conversion.
- Notable: φ (golden ratio) in subterranean passage width.
  2√2 in Granite Temple pillar width.
