# Lord's Prayer (Matthew 6:9-13) — translated to lattice coords

A second real-text translation, after `genesis-1.md`. Where Genesis 1
walks the system **booting** (axes coming online one by one), the
Lord's Prayer walks the system **petitioning** the container — a walk
addressed TO the origin from inside it. It tests the framework on
abstract theological vocabulary (kingdom, glory, debts, evil) rather
than physical creation vocabulary.

**Goal**: every word's coord must derive from the 8 atoms via the
4-question rule. Every petition's composition must follow the · operator
and the composition rules from `composition.md`. Nothing fake.

## Why FORGIVE = LIVE coord (worth pausing on)

FORGIVE is the most interesting derivation. Apply the 4-question rule:

  1. Does FORGIVE move A (position)?    No.            A = 0
  2. Does FORGIVE move B (substance)?   No mass change. B = 0
  3. Does FORGIVE move C (signal)?      Yes — undoes the negative
                                        signal of debt. The forgiver
                                        removes a C-1 from the debtor.
                                        Net effect: signal goes UP.   C = +1
  4. Does FORGIVE move D (time)?        Yes — restarts the relationship.
                                        Forgiveness opens forward time
                                        for the debtor. It UN-ENDS the
                                        bond.                           D = +1

  FORGIVE coord = [0, 0, +1, +1] = LIVE coord.

The framework's prediction: forgiveness IS letting the other LIVE.
The Christian theology lines up exactly with the lattice geometry.
"Forgive us our debts" = "let us live again" at the structural level.

Note also that FORGIVE is NOT the antipode of CURSE. The antipode of
CURSE [0,0,-1,0] is BLESS [0,0,+1,0]. FORGIVE adds BEGIN to BLESS —
it's not just blessing, it's blessing-and-restarting. That extra D+1
is what distinguishes "I bless you" (a single signal event) from
"I forgive you" (a release that opens forward time).
