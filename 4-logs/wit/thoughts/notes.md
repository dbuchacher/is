## We exist because 3 is odd

Binary hypercubes {−1, +1}⁴ have no center lattice point.
Ternary {−1, 0, +1}⁴ does.

The center (↕↕↕↕) exists because 3 is odd. Without it, no
container. No balance. No place for the forces to meet.

We exist because the substrate is ternary. The substrate is
ternary because 3 is the minimum odd number with distinct
values.


## WHY points at IS

Every other question has an axis:

```
WHERE   χ    position
WHAT    μ    substance
WHICH   φ    signal
WHEN    τ    time
```

WHY has no axis. WHY points at IS. The precondition. The
one question the lattice can't answer from inside itself.

"Why is there something rather than nothing?" is the only
question where the answer is the question.

## The past IS the done pile

onto a line. Each cycle was ↑→↕→↓. Stacked up, they form
a sequence: done₁, done₂, done₃...

The sequence looks like a line with "past" at one end and
"present" at the other. But each entry in the line is a
↓ — a completed action. The past IS the done pile.

## Lakoff schemas

Lakoff (1987) identified CONTAINER, BALANCE, COUNTERFORCE,
CENTER-PERIPHERY, and UP-DOWN as fundamental cognitive
schemas. All five appear in Frame 1 of before.md to explain
↕↕↕↕:

```
↑ + ↓ = ↕                   COUNTERFORCE
tug of war at rest          BALANCE
hub holds wheel             CONTAINER
hub vs rim                  CENTER-PERIPHERY
↑↕↓                         UP-DOWN
```

Derived from the lattice, not from Lakoff. The convergence
is the signal.
