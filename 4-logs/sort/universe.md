# universe

From Latin *universus* ("all together, whole, entire") ← *unus*
("one") + *vertere* ("to turn"), from PIE *wer-/wert-*. Literally
**"turned into one."**

**The totality of existing things** — all matter, energy, space,
and time regarded as a single whole.

Cognates of *unus*: unit, unity, union, onion, only, alone.
Cognates of *vertere*: verse, vertex, vortex, version, reverse,
convert, invert, divert, adversity.
