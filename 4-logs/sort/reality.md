# reality

From Latin *realitas* ← *res* ("thing, matter, affair"), from PIE
*reh₁-* ("thing, property").

**The state of things as they actually are**, as opposed to how
they appear, are described, or are imagined. The bare fact of
existence — that there is something rather than nothing.

Cognates: real, realize, republic (*res publica* = "the public
thing"), rebus, Sanskrit *rāḥ* ("wealth, property").
